FRENCH LEGAL AND ECONOMIC DATA BASES AND DATA BANKS.
     
A PRACTICAL GUIDE TO THE ON-LINE SERVICES AVAILABLE TO FRENCH USERS, FROM THE
VIEWPOINT OF INFORMATION PROVISION AS AN INDUSTRY WITH SPECIALISED PRODUCTION
AND DISTRIBUTION AGENCIES. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF SUBJECT COVERAGE AND SERVICES
FOR DATA BANKS AND BASES IN THE ECONOMIC AND LEGAL FIELDS DESIGNED FOR THE
FRENCH MARKET, AND THE INTERNATIONAL MARKET. OTHER RELEVANT BUT LESS
SPECIALISED INFORMATION SERVICES ARE SURVEYED, AND DEVELOPMENTS IN SERVICE
PROVISION DISCUSSED IN RELATION TO CURRENT STANDARDS, WITH PARTICULAR
REFERENCE TO THE PROBLEMS OF DOCUMENT DELIVERY.
