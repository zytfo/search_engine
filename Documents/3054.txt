INFORMATION IS POWER: THE LIBRARY AS A RESOURCE.
     
TRANSLATED BY TAMAS VOLGYES. IN A RAPIDLY DEVELOPING AND STEADILY CHANGING
WORLD, EXAMINES THE ROLE OF INFORMATION AND OF ACCUMULATED HUMAN KNOWLEDGE.
OUTLINES THE LACK OF INFORMATION IN SOME AREAS AND THE LIBRARIAN'S ROLE IN
ELIMINATING, OR AT LEAST ALLEVIATING THIS PROBLEM, WITH UP TO DATE SERVICES.
LIBRARY FUNCTIONS MUST BE EXTENDED AND TRAINING DEVELOPED.
