DOCUMENTATION SERVICES IN HEAD OFFICES OF MONTREAL BUSINESS COMPANIES.
     
REPORT OF A POSTGRADUATE STUDY UNDERTAKEN AT THE UNIVERSITY OF MONTREAL. FULL
DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF SAMPLE SELECTION TECHNIQUE AND METHODOLOGY' 92 FIRMS WERE
CONTACTED BY TELEPHONE IN AUTUMN 80, AND ASKED 4 QUESTIONS ABOUT THEIR
INFORMATION SERVICES. RESULTS ARE TABULATED, GIVING DETAILS OF THE NUMBER OF
FIRMS WITH DOCUMENTATION SERVICES, THE LEVEL OF PERSONNEL TRAINING,
INTEGRATION INTO COMPANY ACTIVITIES, AND SERVICES PROVIDED. THE CONCLUSION IS
DRAWN THAT DEMAND FOR INFORMATION SPECIALISTS IN BUSINESS ACTIVITIES IS
INCREASING.
