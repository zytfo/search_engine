A REVIEW OF PUBLIC LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT IN ASIA.
     
WITH REFERENCE TO THE AUTHOR'S VISITS TO ASIAN COUNTRIES, DISCUSSES PUBLIC
LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT IN ASIA. EMPHASISES THE NEED FOR LIBRARY LEGISLATION WHICH
HAS BEEN INTRODUCED BY ONLY 6 OF THE 41 COUNTRIES IN THE REGION. THE COUNTRIES
LACK LIBRARY STANDARDS (EXCEPT FOR A FEW), ADEQUATE FINANCES, PHYSICAL
FACILITIES, AND LIBRARY RESOURCES. SUGGESTS ACTION BY NATIONAL AND
INTERNATIONAL LIBRARY ASSOCIATIONS AND NATIONAL GOVERNMENTS.
