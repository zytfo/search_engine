DIRECTIONS FOR THE DECADE' LIBRARY INSTRUCTION IN THE 1980S.
     
PAPERS PRESENTED AT THE 10TH ANNUAL CONFERENCE ON LIBRARY ORIENTATION FOR
ACADEMIC LIBRARIES, 8-9 MAY 80. TOPICS COVERED INCLUDED COMPUTER-ASSISTED
INSTRUCTION; LIBRARY SIGN SYSTEMS; BIBLIOGRAPHIC INSTRUCTION FOR THE ADVANCED
RESEARCHER AND LIBRARY USER EDUCATION EVALUATION.
