WHAT TIME IS THE NEXT BUS TO GLASGOW? THE ROLE OF COMMUNITY INFORMATION IN
PUBLIC LIBRARIES.
     
OUTLINES THE HISTORY OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN SCOTLAND AND THE PHILOSOPHY BEHIND
THEM. DESCRIBES THE ROLE AND VALUE OF THE PUBLIC LIBRARY IN AREAS OF LOW
LITERACY AND HIGH UNEMPLOYMENT. THE COMMUNITY INFORMATION AND REFERENCE
LIBRARY CENTRE IN LINWOOD IS DISCUSSED. ITS ROLE IS NOT TO GIVE INFORMATION OR
ADVICE BUT TO ACT AS A CATALYST AND TO ALLOW THE MATERIAL AND INFORMATION TO
BE USED BY OTHERS, BOTH GROUPS AND INDIVIDUALS.
