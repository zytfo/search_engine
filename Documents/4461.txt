INTERVIEW WITH PROFESSOR GASTON LITTON.
     
A PROMINENT US LIBRARIAN AND AUTHOR OF SOME 20 BOOKS ON LIBRARY SCIENCE AND
ARCHIVES, WHO HAS COLLABORATED ON SEVERAL LIBRARY TRAINING PROGRAMMES IN
BRAZIL, ANSWERS QUESTIONS ON THE STATE OF THE PROFESSION. TOPICS COVERED
INCLUDE THE PROSPECTS LIBRARY SCIENCE OFFERS AS A CAREER, PROBABLE
DEVELOPMENTS OVER THE NEXT 25 YEARS, NEGLECTED AND OVERDEVELOPED FIELDS, THE
POSSIBILITIES FOR LIBRARIANS IN NON-LIBRARY SITUATIONS, AND CURRENT TRENDS IN
RESEARCH AND TRAINING.
