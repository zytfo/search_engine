BUILDING THE FEDERATION.
     
THE FEDERATION OF RADICAL BOOKSELLERS (FORMERLY THE FEDERATION OF ALTERNATIVE
BOOKSELLERS) IS AN ORGANISATION FORMED BY ABOUT 40 UK RADICAL AND COMMUNITY
BOOKSHOPS. DESCRIBES THE ORGANISATION, AIMS AND MEMBERSHIPS OF THE FEDERATION
WHICH, IN 1981 IS WIDENING ITS SCOPE TO INCLUDE SHOPS AFFILIATED TO POLITICAL
PARTIES OR OTHER GROUPS. A LIST OF THE FEDERATION'S BOOKSHOP MEMBERS AND THEIR
ADDRESSES IS APPENDED.
