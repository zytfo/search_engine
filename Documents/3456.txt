DIMENSIONS, AS THE POWER, ECONOMY AND USER-EMPATHY OF A NEW GENERATION OF
MICROPROCESSOR-BASED EQUIPMENT OPEN UP NEW AVENUES OF SYSTEMS DEVELOPMENT AND

COMPUTERS, USER-EMPATHY, INTEGRATED INFORMATION HANDLING AND COMMUNICATION.

SCIENTIST-CUM-DIPLOMAT NOT YET SEEN ON ANY EXTANT CORPORATE SCENARIO.
