THE PATENT SYSTEM AND THE TRANSFER OF TECHNOLOGY TO DEVELOPING NATIONS.
     
EXAMINES THE PATENT SYSTEM AS A SOURCE OF TECHNOLOGICAL INFORMATION AND ITS
ROLE IN THE TRANSFER OF TECHNOLOGY TO DEVELOPING NATIONS. INCLUDED ARE THE
ACTIVITIES OF THE UNITED NATIONS AND ITS SPECIALISED AGENCIES IN THE TRANSFER
OF TECHNOLOGY FROM DEVELOPED NATIONS TO DEVELOPING NATIONS. IT ALSO EXAMINES
THE SUCCESSES AND OBSTACLES IN THE TRANSFER OF TECHNOLOGY.
