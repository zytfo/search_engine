POLITICAL SETTING OF LIBRARIES IN POST-SECONDARY INSTITUTIONS.
     
DISCUSSES THE RELEVANCE OF POLITICAL POWER TO THE ADMINISTRATION OF A
POST-SECONDARY INSTITUTION LIBRARY, IN RELATION TO THE REPORTING STRUCTURE,
LIBRARY/SENATE RELATIONS, STATUS OF THE LIBRARY COMMITTEE, THE ROLE OF THE
CHIEF LIBRARIAN AND OTHER LIBRARIANS ON UNIVERSITY COMMITTEES, INDEPENDENCE OF
LIBRARY MANAGEMENT, PROTECTION OF THE LIBRARY, NETWORKS, AND RELATIONS WITH
THE LOCAL COMMUNITY.
