FACSIMILE COMMUNICATION. JUNE 1970-1981 (CITATIONS FROM THE NTIS DATA BASE).

THE BIBLIOGRAPHY CITES REPORTS THAT DISCUSS DESIGN AND APPLICATIONS OF
FACSIMILE COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS. DISCUSSIONS OF SATELLITE AND TELEPHONE
NETWORKS ARE INCLUDED. THE USE OF FACSIMILE COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS BY BUSINESS,
THE MILITARY, AND GOVERNMENT IS COVERED.
