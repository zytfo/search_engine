SOURCES OF FUNDING FOR VIRGINIA LIBRARIES.
     
THE REPORT IS DESIGNED TO (1) EDUCATE NON-LIBRARIANS AS TO HOW VIRGINIA
LIBRARIES ARE FUNDED, (2) INFORM LIBRARIANS OF FUNDING OUTSIDE THEIR
PARTICULAR SPECIALTY, (3) SUGGEST THE ADEQUACY AND INADEQUACY OF SUCH FUNDING
AND ITS DEPENDABILITY, AND (4) OUTLINE THE FORMULAS BY WHICH FUNDS ARE
DISTRIBUTED. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
