EFFECT OF ONLINE SERVICES ON PURCHASE OF A PRINTED INDEX.
     
REVIEWS THE TREND OF PURCHASING OF A PRINTED INDEX (2INDEX MEDICUS1) WITHIN
THE SOUTH WEST THAMES REGIONAL HEALTH AUTHORITY (UK). SHOWS THAT AS THE
MACHINE READABLE COUNTERPART (MEDLINE) HAS BECOME MORE EASILY ACCESSIBLE THE
PURCHASE AND USE OF 2INDEX MEDICUS1 HAS INCREASED RATHER THAN DIMINISHED.
