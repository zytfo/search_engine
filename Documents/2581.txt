FROM THE WORK OF THE HENTEL AIMAK LIBRARY.
     
THE STOCK OF THE HENTEL AIMAK LIBRARY IN MONGOLIA HAS GROWN FROM 200 VOLUMES
IN 1944 TO 50,000 VOLUMES TODAY. ACQUISITIONS ARE BASED ON AN ANNUAL
THEMATICAL PLAN, THE HIGHEST % BEING SOCIO-POLITICAL LITERATURE (26,3%)
FOLLOWED BY CHILDREN'S BOOKS (25,7%) AND BOOKS ON ARTS (24,7%). CIRCULATION
AND BOOK PROCESSING METHODS ARE ALSO DISCUSSED. 1 OF THE MAIN FUNCTIONS OF THE
LIBRARY IS THE PROVISION OF METHODOLOGICAL GUIDANCE TO SMALLER LIBRARIES IN
ITS AREA INCLUDING TOWN, VILLAGE AND SCHOOL LIBRARIES. THIS IS DONE THROUGH
LIBRARY SEMINARS AND THE PUBLICATION OF METHODOLOGICAL MATERIALS. PRACTICAL
HELP IS PROVIDED BY LIBRARIANS-METHODOLOGISTS WHO TRAVEL TO THE SMALLER
LIBRARIES ON REGULAR VISITS.
