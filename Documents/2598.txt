RECONSTRUCTION PLAN OF THE MEDICAL LIBRARY AT GERLE.
     
DEVELOPMENT OF THE MEDICAL LIBRARY AT THE NYIREGYHAZA COUNTY HOSPITAL HAS BEEN
PLANNED TO BECOME AN INFORMATION SUPPLY BASIS AND SCIENTIFIC WORKSHOP OF THE
PARENT ESTABLISHMENT. THE PRESENT POOR CONDITIONS ARE CHARACTERISED BY A
40-SQ.M READING ROOM, THE FREE-SHELF STORAGE ROOM IS AT THE SAME TIME A STUDY
ROOM. THE NUMBER OF POTENTIAL READERS IS 250 PHYSICIANS, 1,200 NURSES AND
OTHER PROFESSIONALS. DEVELOPMENT PLANS RELATE TO A BETTER COVERAGE OF
HOLDINGS; SUBJECT CATALOGUE; INTRODUCTION OF SDI; AND BROADER INTERLIBRARY
LOANS.
