STANDARDS FOR LIBRARY BUILDING.
     
THE IMPORTANCE OF STANDARDS IN THE PLANNING AND RUNNING OF ALL TYPES OF
LIBRARY ARE STRESSED. THE EFFECT OF TECHNOLOGICAL DEVELOPMENTS, SUCH AS
COMPUTERISED CATALOGUING, CIRCULATION CONTROL AND COOPERATIVE ACQUISITION
COUPLED WITH TELECOMMUNICATIONS ON LIBRARY STANDARDS ARE CONSIDERED ALONG WITH
THE NEED TO DESIGN FOR HANDICAPPED LIBRARY USERS.
