ESTABLISHING AN ENVIRONMENTAL INFORMATION CENTER' A GUIDE TO ORGANIZATION AND
OPERATION.

THE CENTER FOR ENVIRONMENTAL INFORMATION (CEI) WAS ESTABLISHED TO SERVE THE
ROCHESTER, NY METROPOLITAN REGION AS A CLEARING-HOUSE FOR ENVIRONMENTAL
CONCERNS. SINCE ITS INCEPTION, CEI HAS BEEN DEVELOPING INTO A PUBLIC SERVICE
SCIENCE CENTRE. PRESENTS DETAILED INFORMATION OF CEI'S (1) EARLY HISTORY; (2)
ADMINISTRATION AND MANAGEMENT; (3) PROGRAMMES AND SERVICES; (4) MEMBERSHIP AND
CLIENTELE; AND (5) FINANCES, BUDGETS, AND FUND RAISING. THE POSSIBILITY OF
USING COMPUTERISED INFORMATION SUPPORT FOR A COMMUNITY-BASED CENTRE SUCH AS
CEI IS EXPLORED.
