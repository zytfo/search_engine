THE INFORMATION SYSTEM OF THE CZECHOSLOVAK FEDERAL ASSEMBLY.
     
THE INFORMATION SYSTEM OF THE CZECHOSLOVAK FEDERAL ASSEMBLY CONSISTS OF A
LIBRARY, AN ARCHIVE AND A PRESS OFFICE. ITS MAIN TASK IS TO PROVIDE
INFORMATION TO THE MPS THROUGH ITS BOOK AND PERIODICAL COLLECTIONS AND THROUGH
ARCHIVAL MATERIAL CONTAINING DOCUMENTS CONCERNING THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE
CZECHOSLOVAK REPUBLIC. BASIC INFORMATION ON THE ACTIVITIES OF THE ORGANS OF
THE PARLIAMENT IS PROVIDED BY A BULLETIN. THE HISTORY AND A DESCRIPTION OF THE
LIBRARY, FOUNDED IN 1918, ARE GIVEN IN GREATER DETAIL.
