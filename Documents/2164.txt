LIBRARY COOPERATION' SITUATION IN JORDAN (IN ARABIC).
     
DISCUSSES THE DIFFERENT ASPECTS OF LIBRARY COOPERATION INCLUDING; PURCHASE,
EXCHANGE, CATALOGUING, INTERLIBRARY LOAN, REFERENCE AND BIBLIOGRAPHIC SERVICES
AND STORAGE. THE BENEFITS OF ALL THESE ASPECTS ARE DISCUSSED IN RELATION TO
THE JORDANIAN MINISTRY OF EDUCATION, SCHOOL AND COMMUNITY COLLEGE LIBRARIES,
MUNICIPALITY PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND THE UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES.
