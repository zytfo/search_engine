BILD-INTEGRATED ONLINE SYSTEM FOR ECONOMIC AND BUSINESS LIBRARIES.
     
BILD, BIBLIOGRAPHIC INDEX OF LIBRARY DOCUMENTS IS THE NEW INTEGRATED ON-LINE
SYSTEM DEVELOPED FOR THE HELSINKI SCHOOL OF ECONOMICS LIBRARY. IT CONSISTS OF
THE ACQUISITION, CATALOGUING AND CLASSIFICATION FUNCTIONS OF THE LIBRARY.
THERE IS ALSO A SEARCH PROGRAM FOR INFORMATION RETRIEVAL AVAILABLE. THE SYSTEM
WAS TESTED DURING THE WINTER 1980-81.
