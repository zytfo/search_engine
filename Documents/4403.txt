MICROFICHE' AN ANSWER TO A MEDICAL RECORDS PAPER PROBLEM.
     
MCDONALD HEALTH CENTER, BRIGHAM YOUNG UNIVERSITY BEGAN INVESTIGATING THE
POSSIBILITY OF USING MICROFICHE TO SOLVE THE PROBLEMS OF PAPER COPY MEDICAL
RECORDS IN 1977. THE 3 BASIC STEPS IN THE PROCESS; CREATION OF THE MICROFICHE
RECORD, RECORD RETRIEVAL PROCESS AND ARCHIVING OF RECORDS ARE DESCRIBED
TOGETHER WITH A FULL COST COMPARISON BETWEEN PAPER HARD COPY AND MICROFICHE
BASED ON THE 1977 STUDY.
