THE PROPOSED CANADIAN LAW ON FREEDOM OF INFORMATION.
     
EXAMINES THE LEGISLATION ON FREEDOM OF INFORMATION PROPOSED BY THE CANADIAN
LIBERAL ADMINISTRATION, PUBLISHED IN JULY 80. SUMMARISES THE POINTS COVERED BY
THE LAW AND EXPLAINS HOW IT WOULD WORK IN PRACTICE; DISCUSSES THE ROLE OF
GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS; AND FINALLY CONSIDERS IN DETAIL WHAT THE EFFECT ON THE
PUBLIC ARCHIVES WOULD BE.
