INVESTIGATION OF REQUIREMENTS AND IMPLEMENTATION OF FACILITIES FOR CONNECTING
UK HOSTS TO EURONET.

DESCRIBES WORK DONE ON X25 SOFTWARE AND NETWORK MEASUREMENT WITH A NUMBER OF
X25 NETWORKS. INVESTIGATES THE IMPLEMENTATION OF SEVERAL HIGH LEVEL PROTOCOLS
INCLUDING TERMINAL, DATA ENTRY, FILE TRANSFER AND REMOTE PRINTING PROTOCOLS. A
NUMBER OF ACTIVITIES PURSUED IN DIRECT SUPPORT OF BLAISE CONNECTION TO EURONET
ARE ALSO DESCRIBED.
