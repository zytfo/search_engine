THE FUTURE OF VIDEOTEX SYSTEMS' A PERSONAL VIEW.

REVIEWS THE TECHNOLOGY OF VIEWDATA, IN EUROPE AND THE UK, ITS BRIEF HISTORY,
PRESENT PROBLEMS AND POSSIBLE FUTURE. REFERENCE IS MADE TO PANDA (PRESTEL
ADVANCED NETWORK DESIGN ARCHITECTURE), PRESTEL'S GATEWAY SYSTEM AND PICTURE
PRESTEL. CONCLUDES THAT VIDEOTEX ON AN INTERNATIONAL SCALE COULD DEVELOP IN 2
DIRECTIONS' LOCAL INFORMATION PROVISION BY DISTRIBUTED DATA BASE NETWORKS IN
INDIVIDUAL COUNTRIES, AND AS A 1ST ENTRY PORT TO INTERNATIONAL DATA BASES AND
DATA BANKS.
