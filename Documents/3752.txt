THE PROGRAMMER/ANALYST.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS OF SELECTED
PROFESSIONALS. JIM MCGILL IS A PROGRAMMER ANALYST FOR A FIRM WHICH DESIGNS AND
SELLS COMPUTING SYSTEMS. HE WAS QUESTIONED ABOUT HIS INFORMATION NEEDS AND
INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS IN THE FOLLOWING AREAS' INFORMATION ON A SUBJECT
OUTSIDE THE COMPUTER SCIENCE FIELD; PAST DEVELOPMENTS IN COMPUTER SCIENCE;
CURRENT AWARENESS; SPECIFIC FACTS OR DATA; THEORIES; AND PROCEDURES AND
TECHNIQUES. FOR EACH INSTANCE, HE WAS ASKED ABOUT THE MEDIUM BY WHICH THE
INFORMATION CAME TO HIM, AND ABOUT ANY PROBLEMS HE EXPERIENCED IN ACQUIRING
IT.
