THE NEW MEDIA AND THE PRESS' EXPERIENCES AND EXPECTATIONS IN THE USA (IN
GERMAN).

FELT THAT THESE NEW MEDIA REPRESENT MORE OF A THREAT TO TV AND RADIO SERVICES
THAN TO NEWSPAPERS, ALTHOUGH THE POSITION OF MAGAZINES AND JOURNALS IN A
PERIOD OF ECONOMIC STAGNATION IS NOT FELT TO BE SO CERTAIN.
