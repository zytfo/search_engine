A SEARCH FOR JEWISH CONTENT IN AMERICAN CHILDREN'S FICTION.
     
(PHD THESIS-NEW YORK UNIVERSITY.) STUDY OF THE AMOUNT OF JEWISH CONTENT IN
CONTEMPORARY JUVENILE REALISTIC FICTION WITH JEWISH CHARACTERS, FOCUSING ON
RELIGIOUS AND SECULAR ACTIVITIES IN THE DAILY LIVES OF THE FICTIONAL
CHARACTERS; THEIR PARTICIPATION IN FORMAL AND INFORMAL ASSOCIATIONS AND
INSTITUTIONS; THE EFFECT BEING JEWISH HAD ON THEM AND THEIR RESPONSES TO WORLD
AND NATIONAL EVENTS OF RELEVANCE TO JEWS; AND THE ATTITUDES, VALUES, CONCERNS,
AND GOALS, ROOTED IN JEWISH TRADITION, ATTRIBUTED TO THEM BY THE AUTHORS.
