A SELECT ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF SOCIAL SCIENCE MATERIALS FOR UGANDA,
FOLLOWED BY AN EXPANDED BIBLIOGRAPHY.
     
THIS BIBLIOGRAPHY IDENTIFIES AVAILABLE, CLEARLY WRITTEN, NON-TECHNICAL
PUBLICATIONS IN THE SOCIAL SCIENCES THAT WILL HELP USAID PERSONNEL AND
CONTRACTORS IN THE UGANDA PROGRAMME TO PLAN AND IMPLEMENT DEVELOPMENT
ASSISTANCE PROGRAMMES AND PROJECTS ADAPTED TO THE DISTINCTIVE SOCIO-ECONOMIC
INSTITUTIONS, THE CULTURE, AND THE HISTORIC SETTING OF THAT COUNTRY.
