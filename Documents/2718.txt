RATIOS OF STAFF TO USERS. IMPLICATIONS FOR LIBRARY-INFORMATION WORK AND THE
POTENTIAL FOR AUTOMATION.
     
A STUDY OF THE UK SPECIAL LIBRARY/INFORMATION FIELD. COVERAGE INCLUDES'
STAFF-USER RATIOS; STAFF AND USER POPULATION STRUCTURES; QUANTITATIVE AND
QUALITATIVE ADEQUACY OF CURRENT STAFFING; TRAFFIC ANALYSIS; AND AUTOMATION
POTENTIAL. DATA WAS COLLECTED BY QUESTIONNAIRE IN LATE OCT 79.
