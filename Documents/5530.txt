THE SWEDISH SCHOOL OF LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SCIENCE AND THE EDUCATION OF
LIBRARIANS FOR SPECIAL LIBRARIES.

CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO PUBLIC LIBRARY SERVICES IN SWEDEN.
(ABBREVIATED VERSION OF AN ARTICLE PUBLISHED IN 2IATUL PROCEEDINGS1, 1981).
GIVES AN ACCOUNT OF EDUCATION FOR LIBRARIANS AT THE SWEDISH SCHOOL OF LIBRARY
AND INFORMATION SCIENCE (SSLIS) AND PRESENTS BOTH THE CURRENT AND THE REVISED
CURRICULA OF THE SCHOOL. IN THE CONTEXT OF THE REVISED CURRICULUM, DISCUSSES
GENERAL VERSUS SPECIALISED TRAINING, PROGRAMME FLEXIBILITY AND THE MARGINS
AVAILABLE FOR THE EDUCATION OF LIBRARIANS FOR SPECIAL LIBRARIES. 2SEE ALSO1
82/5537.
