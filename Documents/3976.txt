COMPUTER NETWORKS. AUGUST, 1979-NOVEMBER, 1981 (CITATIONS FROM THE NTIS DATA
BASE).
     
CITES RESEARCH REPORTS ON ASPECTS OF COMPUTER NETWORKS, INCLUDING HARDWARE,
SOFTWARE, DATA TRANSMISSION, TIME SHARING, AND APPLICABLE THEORY TO NETWORK
DESIGN. SPECIFIC STUDIES ON THE ARPA NETWORK, THE OCTOPUS NETWORK, THE TENEX
SYSTEM, DISTRIBUTED NETWORKS, AND OTHER SUCH SYSTEMS ARE CITED.
