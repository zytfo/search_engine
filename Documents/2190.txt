ARL ANNUAL SALARY SURVEY, 1978-1979.
     
THIS REPORT INCLUDES TABULATIONS OF MEDIAN AND BEGINNING PROFESSIONAL SALARIES
FOR BUDGETED POSITIONS IN ALL ASSOCIATION OF RESEARCH LIBRARIES (ARL) MEMBER
LIBRARIES, AND TABLES LISTING AVERAGE SALARIES FOR FILLED POSITIONS IN ARL'S
UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES. THE TABLES DISPLAY INFORMATION ON AVERAGE SALARIES BY
POSITION, SEX, MINORITY GROUP MEMBERSHIP, AND GEOGRAPHICAL LOCATION, SIZE AND
TYPE OF INSTITUTION. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
