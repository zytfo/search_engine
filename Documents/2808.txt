USER EDUCATION' ITS APPLICATIONS IN UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES IN MALAYSIA.
     
(MLS THESIS.) A STUDY OF THE FEASIBILITY OF LIBRARY USER EDUCATION PROGRAMMES
IN MALAYSIAN UNIVERSITIES ADAPTED FROM UK UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES. OUTLINES THE 5
MALAYSIAN UNIVERSITY CURRICULUM AND TEACHING STYLES. LIBRARY USER EDUCATION
MUST BE RELEVANT AND BLEND WITH THE CURRICULUM PATTERNS TO MEET STUDENTS'
NEEDS FOR INFORMATION.
