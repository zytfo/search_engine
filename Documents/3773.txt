THE AGRICULTURAL INFORMATION REVIEW COMMITTEE REPORT AND AFTERWARDS' A SUMMARY.
     
THE WORK OF THE AGRICULTURAL INFORMATION REVIEW COMMITTEE, SUPPORTED BY THE
BRITISH LIBRARY RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT, IS DESCRIBED. THE
FINDINGS OF THE COMMITTEE'S REPORT (BLRD REPORT 5469) IS OUTLINED. A SERIES OF
ONE-DAY SEMINARS HELD TO MAKE THE CONTENTS OF THE REPORT BETTER KNOWN TO
INFLUENTIAL INDIVIDUALS WITHIN THE AGRICULTURAL INFORMATION SYSTEM, AND TO
OBTAIN THEIR VIEWS ON THE FINDINGS OF THE REPORT ITSELF IS DESCRIBED. A FINAL
MEETING WAS HELD TO DISCUSS SOME OF THE PRIORITY ISSUES IDENTIFIED. THESE
WERE: THE 'GREY' (NON-CONVENTIONAL) LITERATURE; REVIEW LITERATURE; DIRECTORY
OF INFORMATION SOURCES; TRAINING AND EDUCATION IN INFORMATION HANDLING; USER
NEEDS; CO-ORDINATION OF INFORMATION ACTIVITIES.
