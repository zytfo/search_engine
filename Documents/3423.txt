VISUAL DESIGN OF INFORMATION SYSTEMS.
     
THE MOSAIK PROGRAM WHICH IS USED FOR THE GERMAN BILDSCHIRMTEXT VIEWDATA SYSTEM
IS DESCRIBED. A FIELD TEST IS PLANNED TO START IN JUNE 80 AND CONTINUE FOR ONE
YEAR. THE HISTORICAL DEVELOPMENT OF THE PROGRAM AND REASONS FOR ITS PARTICULAR
CHARACTERISTICS ARE DISCUSSED. EXAMPLES OF SCREEN DISPLAYS ARE GIVEN.
