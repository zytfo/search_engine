AUTOMATING THE VERTICAL FILE INDEX.
     
THE BOYS TOWN CENTER'S LIBRARY SERVICES DIVISION MAINTAINS A SUBSTANTIAL
COLLECTION OF EPHEMERAL RESEARCH MATERIALS. EXTENSIVE SUBJECT INDEXING WAS
REQUIRED FOR OPTIMUM USE OF TIMELY DATA IN THE FILE. THROUGH COOPERATIVE
EFFORTS WITH A COMPUTER PROGRAMMER, AN ON-LINE INDEXING SYSTEM WAS IMPLEMENTED
ON A LOCAL MINICOMPUTER. THE USER-ORIENTED SYSTEM HAS IMPROVED REFERENCE
SERVICES AND SERVES AS A PROTOTYPE FOR AN ON-LINE CATALOGUE.
