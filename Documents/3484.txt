FONOFAX SF' AN AUTOMATIC DOCUMENT TRANSMISSION SYSTEM.
     
THE BRITISH POST OFFICE HAS RECENTLY COMPLETED DEVELOPMENT OF AN ADVANCED
STORE AND FORWARD FACSIMILE TERMINAL. IT PROVIDES FOR FAST DOCUMENT LOADING
AND STORAGE, HAS MANY SOPHISTICATED FACILITIES AND OFFERS A SERVICE WITH HIGH
INTEGRITY. TRANSMISSION IS EFFECTED USING STANDARD FACSIMILE METHODS TO ENSURE
MAXIMUM COMPATIBILITY WITH EXISTING EQUIPMENT.
