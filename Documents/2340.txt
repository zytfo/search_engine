FRENCH INTERLIBRARY LOAN STATISTICS, 1978-79.
     
THE OFFICIAL STATISTICS FOR FRENCH ACADEMIC LIBRARIES SHOW A QUANTITATIVE
INCREASE SINCE 1975. RESULTS ARE TABULATED, TO SHOW' TOTAL INCREASE IN LOANS;
EXCHANGES BETWEEN UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES AND OTHER ESTABLISHMENTS; LOANS BY
DISCIPLINE; LOANS BETWEEN PARIS AND THE PROVINCES; FOREIGN LOANS; CHIEF
PARTICIPANTS IN THE SERVICE; AND KINDS OF DOCUMENTS CIRCULATED.
