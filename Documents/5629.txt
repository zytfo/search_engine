LIBRARY SERVICE TO IMMIGRANT CHILDREN IN DENMARK.

SINCE THE MIDDLE OF THE 1970S THE NUMBER OF CHILDREN OF FOREIGN CITIZENS HAS
INCREASED HEAVILY AS THE RELATIVES OF FOREIGN WORKERS WERE GIVEN ENTRY
PERMITS. DISCUSSES EDUCATION, HEALTH CARE, PUBLIC LIBRARY ORGANISATIONS,
ORGANISATION OF LIBRARY MATERIAL, STOCKTAKING AND OTHER ASPECTS OF PROVISION
FOR IMMIGRANT CHILDREN.
