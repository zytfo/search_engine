THE IMPORTANCE OF TEACHING USE OF THE LIBRARY.

INTRODUCTION TO AN OCCASIONAL SERIES ON USE INSTRUCTIONS IN ACADEMIC
LIBRARIES. BIBLIOGRAPHIC INSTRUCTION IS TEACHING STUDENTS HOW TO FIND
INFORMATION EFFICIENTLY AND EFFECTIVELY IN THE MODERN LIBRARY. INSTITUTING
BIBLIOGRAPHIC INSTRUCTION PROGRAMMES PERMITS STUDENTS TO DO BETTER WORK, TO BE
MORE INDEPENDENT IN THEIR WORK AND TO HAVE MORE MOTIVATION. THIS ALLOWS
ASSIGNMENTS TO BE MORE VARIED AND EXPECTATIONS CAN BE HIGHER. BIBLIOGRAPHIC
INSTRUCTION ALSO HELPS TO INSURE THAT THE COLLECTION IS WELL USED-AN IMPORTANT
ARGUMENT WHEN COST EFFECTIVENESS IS A PRIME CONSIDERATION IN ALL
ADMINISTRATIVE DECISIONS.
