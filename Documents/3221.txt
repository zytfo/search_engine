INFORMATION ACTIVITIES IN THE MAIN TECHNICAL UNIVERSITIES' LIBRARIES.
     
ANALYSES THE ACTIVITIES OF INFORMATION DEPARTMENTS IN THE MAIN TECHNICAL
UNIVERSITIES' LIBRARIES WITH REGARD TO TECHNICAL EQUIPMENT, LOCAL CONDITIONS,
AUTOMATION OF THE INFORMATION PROCESS, THE STAFF AND PROFESSIONAL EDUCATION,
REFERENCE COLLECTIONS, REFERENCE PUBLICATIONS AND OTHER TOOLS. THE RESPONSE TO
A SURVEY CARRIED OUT OF THE MAIN TECHNICAL UNIVERSITIES' LIBRARIES WAS USED AS
A BASIS FOR THE EVALUATION.
