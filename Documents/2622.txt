A SURVEY OF NON-RESIDENT LENDING AND BORROWING ACTIVITY IN MASSACHUSETTS.
     
THIS SURVEY PRESENTING RAW DATA FOR THE PLANNING OF RESOURCE SHARING AND OTHER
COOPERATIVE LIBRARY ACTIVITIES IN MASSACHUSETTS FOCUSES ON THE BORROWING AND
LENDING CHARACTERISTICS OF LIBRARIES WITH REGARD TO NONRESIDENT BORROWING
ACTIVITY. IT IS INTENDED TO PROVIDE UP-TO-DATE ESTIMATES OF SUCH ACTIVITY,
FORMULATE LONG TERM SOLUTIONS TO FISCAL PROBLEMS, AND EXPAND ACCESS POTENTIAL
FOR LIBRARY USERS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
