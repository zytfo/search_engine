PROCEEDINGS OF A SEMINAR SPONSORED BY THE UNIVERSITY AND COLLEGE LIBRARIES
SECTION, LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA, AT THE UNIVERSITY OF NEWCASTLE,
18-20 AUG 78. THE AIM WAS TO DISCUSS TECHNICAL AND FURTHER EDUCATION (TAFE)
LIBRARY PROBLEMS, AND TO SUGGEST SOLUTIONS. PAPERS ARE IN 5 SECTIONS'
INTRODUCTION AND OVERVIEW; MANAGEMENT; EDUCATIONAL ASPECTS; STANDARDS FOR TAFE
LIBRARIES; AND CONCLUSIONS AND FUTURE RECOMMENDATIONS.
