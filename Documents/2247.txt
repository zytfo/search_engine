INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN MEDICINE.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 CONFERENE OF THE SOUTH AFRICAN INSTITUTE FOR
LIBRARIANSHIP AND INFORMATION SCIENCE, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. BRIEFLY
DESCRIBES THE AGENCIES PRODUCING INFORMATION RELEVANT TO SOUTH AFRICAN GENERAL
PRACTITIONERS AND APPLIES THE PRINCIPLES OF ADULT EDUCATION TO INFORMATION
TRANSFER IN THE GENERAL PRACTICE CONTEXT.
