THE BOOK HOUSE APPEAL: IMPORTANT IN THESE TIMES.
     
THE NATIONAL BOOK LEAGUE (UK) RECENTLY MOVED TO NEW PREMISES AT 'BOOK HOUSE',
WANDSWORTH, LONDON. GENERAL REFURBISHMENT AND REDECORATION IS NEEDED AND A
BOOK HOUSE APPEAL IS BEING MOUNTED BY THE PUBLISHERS AND BOOKSELLERS
ASSOCIATIONS. DESCRIBES, BRIEFLY, THE WORK OF THE NBL, AND THE IMPORTANCE OF
THE NEW BUILDING IN FURTHERING THE AIMS AND OBJECTIVES OF THE NBL.
