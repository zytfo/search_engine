RIGOROUS, SYSTEMATIC APPROACH TO AUTOMATIC DATA EDITING AND ITS STATISTICAL
BASIS.
     
AUTOMATIC DATA EDITING IS THE COMPUTERISED IDENTIFICATION AND CORRECTION OF
DATA ERRORS. THESE TECHNIQUES CAN PROVIDE ERROR STATISTICS THAT INDICATE THE
FREQUENCY OF VARIOUS TYPES OF DATA ERRORS, DIAGNOSTIC INFORMATION THAT AIDS IN
IDENTIFYING INADEQUACIES IN THE DATA COLLECTION SYSTEM, AND A CLEAN DATA BASE
APPROPRIATE FOR USE IN FURTHER DECISION MAKING, IN MODELLING, AND FOR
INFERENTIAL PURPOSES.
