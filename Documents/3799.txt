A COMPARATIVE STUDY OF DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS OF NURSING INTEREST.
     
(EDD THESIS-THE AMERICAN UNIVERSITY, WASHINGTON DC). A COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS OF
4 MAJOR COMPUTERISED DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS AND A MANUAL SYSTEM CONTAINING
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CITATIONS TO THE NURSING AND NURSING-RELATED LITERATURE-ERIC,
INTERNATIONAL NURSING INDEX, MEDLINE, NATIONAL HEALTH PLANNING INFORMATION
CENTER, AND SOCIAL SCIENCE CITATION INDEX. MEDLINE WAS FOUND TO BE THE MOST
EFFECTIVE.
