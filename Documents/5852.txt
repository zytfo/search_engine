A SURVEY OF ARCHITECTURE MAGAZINES.

REVIEWS, BY ACTIVE MEMBERS OF THE ASSOCIATION OF ARCHITECTURE SCHOOL
LIBRARIANS AND/OR THE ART LIBRARIES SOCIETY OF NORTH AMERICA, OF 54
ARCHITECTURE JOURNALS. JOURNALS FALL INTO SEVERAL BASIC SUBJECT CATEGORIES,
INCLUDING A PRIMARY GROUP OF STANDARD PROFESSIONAL JOURNALS, AMERICAN AND
NON-AMERICAN; US STATE AND REGIONAL ARCHITECTURE PERIODICALS; AND INTERIOR
DECORATION AND DESIGN MAGAZINES.
