ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING AND ITS IMPLICATIONS FOR LIBRARIES.

DISCUSSES THE FUTURE EFFECTS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ON LIBRARIES AND
LIBRARY SERVICES, EMPHASISING THE ROLES OF ON-LINE RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS AND
ELECTRONIC PROCESSES INVOLVING PUBLISHED LITERATURE. THE FOLLOWING ASPECTS ARE
EXAMINED' LINE CHARGES AND TERMINAL COSTS, A COMPREHENSIVE ELECTRONIC JOURNAL
SYSTEM, INTERLIBRARY LOANS, ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING VIA TEXT EDITING SYSTEMS,
ECONOMIC CONSTRAINTS, AND TECHNOLOGICAL CONSTRAINTS. THE FOCUS THROUGHOUT IS
ON SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
