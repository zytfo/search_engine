GUIDE TO REFERENCE SOURCES DEALING WITH NORTH AMERICAN INDIANS.
     
THIS IS AN ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF REFERENCE SOURCES DEALING WITH NORTH
AMERICAN INDIANS AND INCLUDES SELECTED UNPUBLISHED RESOURCES IN THE CHESTER
FRITZ LIBRARY, UNIVERSITY OF NORTH DAKOTA. GENERAL LIBRARY CATALOGUE
DESCRIPTIONS ARE INCLUDED FOR THE CHESTER FRITZ LIBRARY, THE PEABODY MUSEUM OF
ARCHAEOLOGY AND ETHNOLOGY, THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS, AND THE NEWBERRY LIBRARY.
3 SPECIAL SECTIONS ARE DEVOTED TO THE PLAINS INDIANS, FOLKLORE, AND LANGUAGE
AND LITERATURE. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
