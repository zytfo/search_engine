REFERENCE AID' DATA ON SERIAL PUBLICATIONS OF VIETNAM.
     
THE REPORT IS AN ALPHABETICAL LISTING OF PERIODICALS PUBLISHED IN THE
SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM ALONG WITH AVAILABLE DATA ON ALTERNATE TITLES,
SPONSORSHIP, OFFICERS AND CONTENTS FOR EACH PUBLICATION. APPROXIMATELY 311
NEWSPAPERS, MAGAZINES, JOURNALS, BULLETINS AND NEWSLETTERS ARE PUBLISHED
INCLUDING 225 NATIONAL LEVEL PUBLICATIONS AND 86 LOCAL LEVEL PUBLICATIONS.
