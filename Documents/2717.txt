TIME MANAGEMENT FOR LIBRARY STAFF.
     
WITH PRESSURES AND WORK LOADS INCREASING FOR LIBRARY STAFF AT ALL RANKS, WAYS
MUST BE FOUND TO MAXIMISE USE OF TIME. SUGGESTIONS ARE GIVEN FOR BOTH
SUPERVISORS AND OTHER LIBRARY STAFF FOR SETTING PRIORITIES, COPING WITH
STRESS, AND ANALYSING TIME-RELATED ISSUES. A NUMBER OF SPECIFIC TECHNIQUES FOR
OPTIMISING WORK TIME ARE LISTED.
