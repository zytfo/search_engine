CENTRAL SERVICE OF AUDIOVISUAL MEDIA IN SWEDEN.

BIBLIOTEKSTJANST (BTJ) IS THE SWEDISH NATIONAL CENTRE FOR LIBRARY SERVICES AND
IS OWNED BY THE SWEDISH LIBRARY ASSOCIATION (SAB) AND THE SWEDISH ASSOCIATION
OF LOCAL AUTHORITIES . BTJ WORKS IN THE BIBLIOGRAPHICAL AS WELL AS IN THE
TECHNICAL FIELD. IN THE BIBLIOGRAPHICAL FIELD THE CATALOGUING AND THE
CENTRALISED BINDING SERVICES ARE THE MOST IMPORTANT OF BTJ'S ACTIVITIES. IN
THE SECTION FOR CENTRALISED BINDING THE DEPARTMENT FOR AUDIOVISUAL MEDIA
PROVIDES 3 TYPES OF SERVICES' TALKING BOOKS, MUSIC AND FILMSTRIPS, SLIDE
SERIES, LANGUAGE COURSES ETC. IN EACH OF THESE 3 FIELDS THE EDITORIAL STAFF OF
BTJ IS SUPPORTED BY A COMMITTEE OF SPECIALISTS AND EXPERIENCED LIBRARIANS
CHOSEN BY THE SWEDISH LIBRARY ASSOCIATION AND THE SWEDISH ASSOCIATION OF LOCAL
AUTHORITIES.
