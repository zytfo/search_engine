WHICH PRIVATE VIEWDATA SYSTEM?.
     
REPORT OF A 1-DAY SEMINAR, LONDON 2 OCT 81. PRIVATE VIEWDATA SYSTEMS ARE
COMPARED WITH PRESTEL GIVING THE RELATIVE ADVANTAGES/DISADVANTAGES OF BOTH
SYSTEMS. FACTORS ENCOURAGING PRIVATE VIEWDATA DEVELOPMENT AND TRENDS IN
PRIVATE VIEWDATA ARE GIVEN ALONG WITH SYSTEM COSTS. CONCLUDES THAT THE 2
SYSTEMS ARE COMPLEMENTARY, PARTICULARLY IN VIEW OF THE DEVELOPMENT OF PRESTEL
GATEWAY.
