FOCII OF HUMAN ACTIVITY, ESSEX COUNTY, ONTARIO, 1825-52' ARCHIVAL SOURCES AND
RESEARCH STRATEGIES.
     
EXPLOITS A VARIETY OF TEXTUAL AND CARTOGRAPHIC SOURCES IN ORDER TO RECONSTRUCT
A USEFUL BASE FOR AN INVESTIGATION INTO THE HISTORICAL GEOGRAPHY OF ESSEX
COUNTY, ONTARIO, 1825-52, WHICH CONTAINS THE EARLIEST WHITE SETTLEMENT IN WHAT
IS NOW ONTARIO AND WAS THE MOST WESTERLY OF THE 4 EARLY CORE AREAS IN THE
PROVINCES.
