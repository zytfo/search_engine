A DOCUMENTATION FRANCAISE SERVICE' THE POLITICAL AND CURRENT AFFAIRS
INFORMATION BANK (B.I.P.A.).

SUMMARY OF A PRESENTATION DELIVERED AT THE FRENCH LIBRARIANS ASSOCIATION
PUBLIC LIBRARIES GROUP STUDY SESSION IN JAN 81. B.I.P.A. IS A PUBLIC ARCHIVE
FOR POLITICAL, ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL INFORMATION. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF
INFORMATION COLLECTION AND PROCESSING ACTIVITIES, IN RELATION TO
ADMINISTRATIVE, POLITICAL, PRESS, AND PHOTOGRAPHIC INFORMATION; AND DIFFUSION
ACTIVITIES, WITH PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO ON-LINE ACCESS TO DATA, MICROFICHE
MATERIALS, AND PUBLICATIONS, INCLUDING CATALOGUES AND INDEXES.
