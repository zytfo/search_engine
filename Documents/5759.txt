PLANNING AND IMPLEMENTING SOCIAL SERVICE INFORMATION SYSTEMS' A GUIDE FOR
MANAGEMENT AND USERS.

DEALS WITH THE DESIGN OF A SOCIAL SERVICE INFORMATION SYSTEM. IDENTIFIES
CRITICAL ELEMENTS, EFFECTIVE STRATEGIES, AND POTENTIAL PITFALLS IN THE
PLANNING, IMPLEMENTATION, AND UTILISATION OF SUCH SYSTEMS. THE INFORMATION IS
INTENDED TO HELP ADMINISTRATORS AND USERS ESTABLISH SUCCESSFUL SOCIAL SERVICE
INFORMATION SYSTEMS, ANTICIPATE AND AVOID PROBLEMS ALREADY FACED BY SIMILAR
AGENCIES, AND MAXIMISE THE USE OF EXISTING AND FUTURE SYSTEMS.
