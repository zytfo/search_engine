CAFS 800' SOME PRINCIPLES AND PRACTICES.
     
DESCRIBES THE FUNDAMENTALS OF THE CONTENTS ADDRESSABLE FILE STORE SYSTEM AND
THE ICL CAFS 800 SYSTEM, AND ITS APPLICABILITY TO TEXT RETRIEVAL AND DATA
PROCESSING. IT GIVES EXAMPLES OF ITS USE IN A BUREAU ENVIRONMENT, AND
SUMMARISES THE EXPERIENCE OF OPERATING SUCH A DEVICE FOR A YEAR.
