BRAZILIAN OFFICIAL SERIAL PUBLICATIONS' AN ACQUISITION STRATEGY.
     
REVIEWS PROBLEMS IN THE ACQUISITION OF OFFICIAL BRAZILIAN PUBLICATIONS AND
SUMMARISES VARIOUS BIBLIOGRAPHIC TOOLS AVAILABLE AS RESOURCES AND RECENT
ADVANCES IN INTERNAL BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL. A COLLECTION DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
UNDERTAKEN BY THE UNIVERSITY OF ILLINOIS LIBRARY IS DESCRIBED, INCLUDING
METHODS OF SELECTING TARGET AGENCIES AND THE RESPONSE RATE, AND ACQUISITIONS
RESULTING FROM REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION ABOUT OFFICIAL PUBLICATIONS'
AVAILABILITY. RESULTS INDICATE A PREPONDERANCE OF SERIALS AMONG BRAZILIAN
GOVERNMENT PUBLICATIONS. THE IMPLICATIONS OF SUCH A PROJECT FOR OTHER
LIBRARIES, AND PARTICULARLY FOR SERIALS ACQUISITION AND COLLECTION
DEVELOPMENT, ARE NOTED.
