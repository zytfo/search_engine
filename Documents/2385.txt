FLC/FEDLINK AACR2 CATALOGING MANUAL FOR FEDERAL LIBRARIES.
     
THE MANUAL IS INTENDED TO BE USED IN CONJUNCTION WITH AACR2. THE EMPHASIS IS
ON MATERIAL AND PROBLEMS LIKELY TO BE ENCOUNTERED BY CATALOGUERS IN FEDERAL
AGENCY LIBRARIES. GOVERNMENT DOCUMENTS, TECHNICAL REPORTS, SERIALS,
MICROFORMS, AND MAPS ARE ADDRESSED. THE FOCUS IS ON DESCRIPTIVE CATALOGUING.
