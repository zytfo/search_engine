ANALYSIS OF DOCUMENT AVAILABILITY AT THE PROF. LUIS DE BESSA CENTRE FOR
CONTINUING EDUCATION.

SUMMARY OF A DISSERTATION PREPARED FOR UFMG LIBRARY SCHOOL, BRAZIL. THE STUDY
USED KANTOR'S MATHEMATICAL AVAILABILITY ANALYSIS MODEL, WHICH IS PRESENTED IN
DETAIL. AN ACCOUNT IS GIVEN OF THE ORGANISATION OF THE PROF. LUIS DE BESSA
CENTRE FOR CONTINUING EDUCATION, LIBRARY HOLDINGS, AND USER HABITS. RESULTS
ARE REPORTED IN FULL, AND ANALYED TO SHOW CAUSES OF FAILURE AT THE SHELF. THE
PROBABLE RATE OF USER SATISFACTION CALCULATED WAS 70%.
