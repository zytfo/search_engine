THESAURI IN INFORMATICS.
     
THE THESAURUS IS CONSIDERED AS A DEFINITE STRUCTURED SYSTEM OF SEMANTIC UNITS
WITH A DEVELOPED SET OF RELATIONS. QUESTIONS CONCERNING CORRELATIONS BETWEEN
THE THESAURUS AND DIFFERENT CLASSIFICATIONS AND BETWEEN BRANCH THESAURI ARE
CLARIFIED. THE CONNECTION BETWEEN TEXTS AND THESAURUS CONSTRUCTION PROBLEMS
ARE INVESTIGATED.
