CATALOGUING IN PUBLICATION' THE ROLE OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY AND THE NIGERIAN
PUBLISHERS.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT A SEMINAR ON CATALOGUING-IN-PUBLICATION, HELD DURING THE
4TH IFE BOOK FAIR, 5 MAR 79. OUTLINES THE MEANING OF A NATIONAL
CATALOGUING-IN-PUBLICATION PROGRAMME; ITS ADVANTAGES TO THE BOOK TRADE,
LIBRARIES AND INFORMATION CENTRES, AND THE NATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHIC AGENCY; AND
HOW SUCH A PROGRAMME COULD BE OPERATED IN NIGERIA.
