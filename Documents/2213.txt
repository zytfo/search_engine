WISCONSIN STATISTICS' A DIRECTORY OF SOURCES.
     
DIRECTORY OF WISCONSIN AND FEDERAL INFORMATION SOURCES MEANT TO PROVIDE THE
USER WITH INITIAL CONTACTS AND SOURCES OF A WIDE RANGE OF SOCIOECONOMIC DATA.
THE MATERIALS DESCRIBED ARE AVAILABLE THROUGH THE WISCONSIN LIBRARY NETWORK.
MAJOR RESOURCES FOR THE DOCUMENTS INCLUDE FEDERAL AND STATE DEPOSITORY
LIBRARIES, PUBLIC LIBRARIES, AND SYSTEM HEADQUARTERS LIBRARIES, AS WELL AS THE
LIBRARIES OF VARIOUS STATE AGENCIES; HOWEVER, ANY WISCONSIN LIBRARY CAN OBTAIN
MATERIALS FOR THE USER. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
