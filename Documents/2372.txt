HEMLOC-HEALTH AND MEDICAL LIBRARIES' ON-LINE CATALOGUE.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA CATALOGUERS'
SECTION NATIONAL CATALOGUING SEMINAR, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. IN OCT
79, THE AUSTRALIAN HEALTH DEPARTMENT LIBRARY CLOSED ITS CARD CATALOGUE AND
COMMENCED INPUT INTO HEMLOC-HEALTH AND MEDICAL LIBRARIES' ON-LINE CATALOGUE.
DISCUSSES INPUT, OUTPUT, AND BASIC CATALOGUING ASPECTS OF HEMLOC.
