TRANSINOVE' CONCEPTION, INFORMATION AND INNOVATION.
     
TRANSINOVE INTERNATIONAL WAS ESTABLISHED IN JAN 81 AROUND A SPECIALISED
TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER DATA BASE. COVERS THE HISTORY OF THE CREATION OF THE
COMPANY, FILE STRUCTURE, THE VARIOUS SOURCES OF INFORMATION, THE HOST AND ITS
SOFTWARE AND THE USERS, AND THE RELATED SERVICES WHICH ARE INTENDED TO FINANCE
OPERATION.
