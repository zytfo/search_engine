FILMS ON MENTAL RETARDATION.
     
PUBLIC LAW 94-142, THE US EDUCATION OF ALL HANDICAPPED CHILDREN ACT OF 1975,
HAS BROUGHT A NEW AWARENESS OF THE NEEDS AND PROBLEMS OF THE HANDICAPPED.
LIBRARIANS CAN HELP TO DEVELOP THIS AWARENESS, PARTICULARLY AMONGST CHILDREN,
BY PROVIDING FILMS. PRESENTS AN ANNOTATED LIST OF FILMS ABOUT THE MENTALLY
RETARDED AND THEIR ROLE IN SOCIETY.
