THE 1ST SOVIET-AMERICAN LIBRARY SEMINAR AND US LIBRARIES (PARTICIPANT'S NOTES).
     
REPORTS ON AND PROVIDES SUMMARIES OF CONTRIBUTIONS PRESENTED AT THE SEMINAR ON
'LIBRARY SERVICES IN INFORMATION SOCIETY' HELD IN WASHINGTON IN MAY 79. BOTH
US AND USSR PAPERS COVERED A WIDE VARIETY OF SUBJECTS INCLUDING LIBRARY
PLANNING, EDUCATION AND STATISTICS, INFORMATION NEEDS, USE OF INFORMATION IN
THE USSR, SERVICES TO CHILDREN, NATIONAL LIBRARIES AND NATIONAL LIBRARY
SYSTEMS. VISITS TO LIBRARIES AND INFORMATION CENTRES, ORGANISED FOR THE SOVIET
PARTICIPANTS, ENABLED THEM TO SEE, AT 1ST HAND, THE WORK OF US LIBRARIES IN
PRACTICE.
