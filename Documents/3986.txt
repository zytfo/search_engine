TELECOMMUNICATIONS' ECONOMIC STUDIES. 1979-AUGUST, 1981 (CITATIONS FROM THE
NTIS DATA BASE).
     
RESEARCH ON ECONOMIC EVALUATIONS OF THE TELECOMMUNICATIONS INDUSTRY IS CITED.
COMMUNICATION SATELLITES, COMMON CARRIERS, CABLE TELEVISION, TELEPHONE
SYSTEMS, AND TELEVISION SYSTEMS ARE ALL COVERED IN THE BIBLIOGRAPHY. ECONOMIC
STUDIES OF THE FOREIGN TELECOMMUNICATIONS INDUSTRY ARE INCLUDED.
