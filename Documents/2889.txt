DEWEY IN THE UK' A BRITISH VIEWPOINT.
     
BASED ON A PAPER GIVEN AT THE DEWEY DECIMAL CLASSIFICATION MEETING, 3 MAR 81,
UNIVERSITY COLLEGE LONDON (UK). DESCRIBES THE MAIN STRANDS OF DEWEY DECIMAL
CLASSIFICATION DEVELOPMENT TO DATE, THE RESPONSE OF LIBRARIES TO THE USE OF
MORE RECENT EDITIONS, FROM LARGELY THE PUBLIC LIBRARY POINT OF VIEW AND OFFERS
SOME TALKING POINTS CONCERNING POSSIBLE DDC DEVELOPMENT.
