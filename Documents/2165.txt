COOPERATIVE SYSTEMS AND PUBLIC LIBRARIES ENTERING THE 1980'S.
     
EXAMINATION OF THE PROBLEMS AND OPPORTUNITIES OF SYSTEM ORGANISATION FROM THE
VIEWPOINT OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES PROVIDES A PERSPECTIVE ON THE GROWTH OF LIBRARY
NETWORKS, THEIR ADMINISTRATION, AND THE PRESSURES THAT OPPOSED THEIR
DEVELOPMENT IN THE 70S. IDENTIFIES THE REQUIREMENTS FOR THEIR FURTHER
DEVELOPMENT, AND LOOKS AT THE NEED FOR NEW INFORMATION SYSTEMS AND MORE
EFFECTIVE MARKETING FOR THE 80S. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
