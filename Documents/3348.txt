THE CATALOGING AND CLASSIFICATION OF MACHINE-READABLE DATA FILES. PART I. A
CASE FOR INCORPORATING RECORDS OF MACHINE-READABLE DATA FILES INTO THE PUBLIC
CATALOG.
     
PART I OF A 3-PART ARTICLE. EXPLORES THE FEASIBILITY AND DESIRABILITY OF
INCORPORATING RECORDS OF MACHINE-READABLE DATA FILES (MRDF) IN US PUBLIC
LIBRARY CATALOGUES BY CONSIDERING THE EXISTING CONDITIONS WHICH MOTIVATE
PEOPLE TO RAISE THE ISSUE; THE VARIOUS EFFORTS AT SOLVING THE PROBLEMS OF
DOCUMENTATION AND BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL OF MRDF; THE CURRENT STATUS OF MRDF
CATALOGUING ACTIVITIES; AND THE AUTHOR'S 11 RECOMMENDATIONS REGARDING MRDF
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL.
