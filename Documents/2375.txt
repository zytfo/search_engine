A PROPOSAL FOR ACHIEVING UNIFORMITY IN THE CITATION OF INTERNATIONAL DOCUMENTS.
     
PROPOSAL FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND ADOPTION OF A STANDARD FOR THE BIBLIOGRAPHIC
CITATION OF INTERNATIONAL DOCUMENTS, WHICH WOULD FOSTER IMPROVED BIBLIOGRAPHIC
CONTROL BY ENSURING COMPLETENESS OF INFORMATION WITHIN THE CITATION. THE
INTENDED AUDIENCE FOR THE STANDARD WOULD BE AUTHORS, EDITORS, AND PUBLISHERS,
INCLUDING PRODUCERS OF INDEXES, ABSTRACTS AND SALES CATALOGUES.
