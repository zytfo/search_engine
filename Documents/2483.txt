TH-CENTURY TECHNOLOGY AND THE JEFFERSON PAPERS.AD
     
APTED FROM A PAPER PRESENTED AT THE MODERN TECHNOLOGY AND HISTORICAL EDITING'
NATIONAL HISTORICAL PUBLICATIONS AND RECORDS COMMISSION WORD PROCESSING
CONFERENCE, PHILADELPHIA, PENNSYLVANIA, 5 MAY 81. DESCRIBES THE PREPARATION ON
THE COMPUTER OF THE TEXT AND CUMULATIVE INDEX OF THE MULTI-VOLUME 2PAPERS OF
THOMAS JEFFERSON1 THROUGH THE COOPERATION OF THE PROJECT'S EDITORS AND
PUBLISHER WITH THE COMPUTER CENTRE AT PRINCETOWN UNIVERSITY.
