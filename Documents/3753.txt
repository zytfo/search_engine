PATENT INFORMATION ACTIVITIES OF INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY OFFICES.
     
THE 2ND OF A SERIES OF REPORTS (2SEE1 81/4292). A COMPILATION OF DATA ON THE
TECHNICAL ACTIVITIES OF THE ROYAL PATENT AND REGISTRATION OFFICE OF SWEDEN,
1974-80. THE DATA HAVE BEEN BROKEN DOWN INTO SEVERAL PARTS, EACH DEALING WITH
A MAJOR ASPECT OF PATENT INFORMATION WORK. THE USE OF ELECTRONIC DATA
PROCESSING EQUIPMENT IS NOT DESCRIBED AS A SEPARATE ITEM, BUT IS COVERED AS
APPROPRIATE WITHIN THE OTHER ITEMS.
