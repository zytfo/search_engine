DEVELOPMENT OF COMPREHENSIVE STATE DISSEMINATION PLANS' AN OVERVIEW OF PRESENT
STATUS.
     
THE STATE DISSEMINATION GRANTS PROGRAM PROVIDES RESOURCES TO STATE EDUCATION
AGENCIES TO DEVELOP COMPREHENSIVE AND GENERALISED DISSEMINATION CAPACITY, TO
PROVIDE INFORMATION AND TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE IN THE SOLUTION OF PROBLEMS
IDENTIFIED BY THE DISSEMINATION AGENCY OR ITS CLIENTELE. GENERALISED
DISSEMINATION MEANS PROVIDING ACCESS TO ALL INFORMATION RESOURCES FOR ALL
EDUCATORS REGARDLESS OF SUBJECT FIELD OR ROLE. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
