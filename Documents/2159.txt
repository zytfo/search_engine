THE OHIO LONG RANGE PROGRAM FOR IMPROVEMENT OF LIBRARY SERVICES.
     
PRESENTS THE LONG TERM PROGRAMME FOR IMPROVING LIBRARY SERVICES IN OHIO USING
LIBRARY SERVICES AND CONSTRUCTION ACT (LSCA) FUNDS, ADOPTED BY THE STATE
LIBRARY BOARD IN SEPT 80. INCLUDES SEPARATE SECTIONS ON THE STATE LIBRARY,
SERVICES TO THE DISABLED, PUBLIC, ACADEMIC, SCHOOL, SPECIAL, AND INSTITUTION
LIBRARIES, AND ADMINISTRATION AND CLASSES OF LSCA FUNDS.
