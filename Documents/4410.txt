PARKS CANADA MANUSCRIPT REPORT SERIES (WITH INDEX).
     
THE MANUSCRIPT REPORT SERIES IS A COLLECTION OF SOME 350 TITLES ON A VARIETY
OF CANADIAN HISTORICAL SUBJECTS, PREPARED BY HISTORIANS, ARCHAEOLOGISTS AND
CONTRACT RESEARCHERS WORKING FOR THE 5 REGIONAL OFFICES OF PARKS CANADA, AND
FOR THE RESEARCH DIVISION OF ITS OTTAWA-BASED NATIONAL HISTORIC PARKS AND
SITES BRANCH. MOST OF THE REPORTS ARE UNPUBLISHED. THE AUTHOR HAS PREPARED A
SUBJECT INDEX TO THE COLLECTION AND PRESENTS A BRIEF INTRODUCTION TO THEIR
ROLE AND HISTORICAL VALUE. THE INDEX IS REPRINTED HERE.
