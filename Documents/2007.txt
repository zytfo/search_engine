LIBRARY PROBLEMS IN THE HUMANITIES' CASE STUDIES IN REFERENCE SERVICES,
COLLECTION BUILDING, AND MANAGEMENT.
     
A SERIES OF 28 CASE STUDIES DESIGNED FOR LIBRARIANSHIP STUDENTS TO ASSIST THEM
IN LEARNING TO COPE WITH LIBRARY PROBLEMS IN ART, LITERATURE, MYTHOLOGY,
MUSIC, PHILOSOPHY AND RELIGION. ALSO TO BE USED FOR IN-SERVICE TRAINING IN
LIBRARIES AND BY STUDENTS OF HUMANITIES.
