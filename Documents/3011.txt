LIBRARY AND INFORMATION ACTIVITIES IN EUROPEAN COUNTRIES. (1) IMPRESSIONS OF
HUNGARY.
     
DISCUSSES A VISIT MADE BY THE AUTHOR TO HUNGARY IN ORDER TO ATTEND THE 2ND
INTERNATIONAL SEMINAR ON 'APPLICATIONS OF STANDARDS IN INFORMATION' AND TO
VISIT SEVERAL LIBRARIES AND ATTEND LECTURES AND MEETINGS CONCERNED WITH
HUNGARIAN LIBRARIES. DESCRIBES VISITS TO THE NATIONAL LIBRARY, THE PARLIAMENT
LIBRARY AND THE LIBRARY OF THE HUNGARIAN ACADEMY OF SCIENCES. GIVES
IMPRESSIONS OF HUNGARIAN LIFE IN GENERAL AND THE STATE OF HUNGARIAN
LIBRARIANSHIP IN PARTICULAR.
