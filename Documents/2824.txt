THE SERIALS DEPARTMENT'S RESPONSIBILITIES FOR REFERENCE.
     
THE COMPLEXITY OF REFERENCE IN THE SERIALS DEPARTMENT IS DISCUSSED WITH
EXAMPLES FROM A USER ORIENTATION. SOLUTIONS TO THE PROBLEM OF HOW TO PROVIDE
GOOD SERIALS SERVICE ARE GIVEN WITH RELATION TO STAFFING THE SERIALS REFERENCE
DESK AND TRAINING PERSONNEL. THE BENEFITS OF IMPROVING SERIALS REFERENCE ARE
DISCUSSED AS WELL AS THE BENEFITS DERIVING FROM COOPERATION WITH THE GENERAL
REFERENCE DEPARTMENT.
