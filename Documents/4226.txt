LIBRARY BUILDING IN AUSTRIA.
     
AFTER A HISTORICAL OUTLINE ON LIBRARIES IN AUSTRIA, STARTING WITH THE MEDIEVAL
LIBRARIES OF THE MONASTERIES, EMPHASIS IS LAID ON THE BUILDINGS AND PLANNING
SINCE WORLD WAR II. IT IS SHOWN THAT IN RECENT YEARS 15,000 SQ.M. WERE MADE
AVAILABLE WHILE MORE THAN 30,000 SQ.M. ARE UNDER CONSTRUCTION AND 45,000 SQ.M.
ARE IN THE PLANNING STAGE.
