THE PREPARATION OF A COMPUTER-GENERATED CONCORDANCE.
     
DESCRIBES THE PREPARATION OF 2A CONCORDANCE AND WORD-LISTS TO THE POEMS OF
DYLAN THOMAS 1USING THE COCOA COMPUTER BASED CONCORDANCING PROGRAM. DISCUSSES'
THE SELECTION OF THE BASE TEXT; THE PRODUCTION OF THE INITIAL WORKING
CONCORDANCE; THE COMPILATION, FROM HEAD WORDS IN THE CONCORDANCE, OF COMPLETE
WORD LISTS SORTED BOTH ALPHABETICALLY AND BY FREQUENCY OF OCCURRENCE; AND THE
COMPILATION, (MAINLY BY HAND), OF A NUMBER OF WORD LISTS OF SPECIFIC INTEREST.
