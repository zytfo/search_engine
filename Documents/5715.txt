THE NETWORKING OF WEST VIRGINIA' A WORKSHOP SUMMARY.

REPORT ON 2 WEST VIRGINIA NETWORKING WORKSHOPS HELD IN JULY AND AUG 80. THE
HIGHLIGHT OF BOTH MEETINGS WAS A PRESENTATION BY VINOD CHACHRA OF VIRGINIA
POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE AND STATE UNIVERSITY, WHO HAS BEEN INVESTIGATING THE
STATE OF THE ART OF LIBRARY TECHNOLOGY AND ITS IMPLICATIONS FOR THE
DEVELOPMENT OF EFFECTIVE, EFFICIENT LIBRARY SYSTEMS IN WEST VIRGINIA. THE
PRESENTATION IS SUMMARISED IN SOME DETAIL.
