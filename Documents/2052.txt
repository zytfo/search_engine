CALIFORNIA LIBRARY STATISTICS AND DIRECTORY, 1980. FISCAL YEAR 1978-79 DATA
FOR PUBLIC, ACADEMIC, SPECIAL STATE AGENCY AND COUNTY LAW LIBRARIES.
     
THIS ANNUAL DIRECTORY CHARACTERISES LIBRARIES IN CALIFORNIA THROUGH
STATISTICAL TABLES, A PROFILE OF EACH LIBRARY, DESCRIPTIONS OF STATE LIBRARY
ASSOCIATIONS, A PUBLIC LIBRARY DIRECTORY, AND SEVERAL INDEXES. REPORT NOT
AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
