INDIVIDUALIZED APPROACH TO LEARNING LIBRARY SKILLS.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO A THEMATIC ISSUE ON LIBRARY USE INSTRUCTION. DISCUSSES THE
VALUE OF THE INDIVIDUALISED APPROACH TO LEARNING LIBRARY SKILLS, AND DESCRIBES
A NUMBER OF ACTIVITIES CURRENTLY BEING USED IN LIBRARIES WHICH MIGHT BE
CLASSIFIED AS INDIVIDUALISED APPROACHES. THESE ARE' SIGNS; GUIDES AND
HANDBOOKS; PROGRAMMED INSTRUCTION; COMPUTER-ASSISTED INSTRUCTION; LEARNING
PACKAGES; AND PSI (PERSONALISED SYSTEM OF INSTRUCTION). IT IS ARGUED THAT
LIBRARIANS MAY MAKE MORE USE OF THE INDIVIDUALISED APPROACH TO LEARNING DURING
THE 1980S.
