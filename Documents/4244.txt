CURRENT AFFAIRS INFORMATION IN LOCAL GOVERNMENT.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE ASLIB CONFERENCE, CURRENT AFFAIRS' INFORMATION AND
RESOURCES, LONDON, 4 JUNE 81. DISCUSSES THE SCOPE OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT IN THE
UK, ITS RESPONSIBILITIES AND THE PRESSURES THAT IT CURRENTLY FACES. ALSO
CONSIDERS THE TYPES OF CURRENT AFFAIRS INFORMATION THAT LOCAL GOVERNMENT NEEDS
AND HOW THIS INFORMATION IS COORDINATED. DESCRIBES HOW DEVON AND CHESHIRE
COUNTY COUNCILS AND THE GREATER LONDON COUNCIL HAVE TACKLED THE PROBLEMS OF
COORDINATION.
