EVALUATION OF LIBRARY BINDINGS.

REVIEWS THE WORK OF THE TESTING LABORATORY AT THE LIBRARY BINDING INSTITUTE AT
ROCHESTER INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, WHICH IS THE STANDARD-PRODUCING BODY FOR
THE USA. DISCUSSES DECREASING BUDGETS AND THEIR EFFECTS ON LIBRARY BINDING
METHODS, ALTERNATIVE BINDINGS, COMPETITION AMONG LIBRARY BINDERS, BOOK
PERFORMANCE TESTING, TESTING EQUIPMENT, SCIENTIFIC EVALUATION OF METHODS,
SERVICES TO LIBRARIANS AND NEW SPECIFICATIONS FOR ALTERNATE BINDING METHODS.
