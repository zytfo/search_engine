REFORM AND RENEWAL IN HIGHER EDUCATION' IMPLICATIONS FOR LIBRARY INSTRUCTION.
     
PAPERS PRESENTED AT THE 9TH ANNUAL CONFERENCE ON LIBRARY ORIENTATION FOR
ACADEMIC LIBRARIES, EASTERN MICHIGAN UNIVERSITY, 3-4 MAY 79. PAPERS DEAL WITH
A WIDE RANGE OF TOPICS, INCLUDING CURRICULUM CHANGE IN THE COMMUNITY COLLEGE,
BUDGETS FOR LIBRARY INSTRUCTION, AND PRACTICAL SUGGESTIONS FOR THE
IMPLEMENTATION AND PROMOTION OF LIBRARY INSTRUCTION.
