AGRICOLA.
     
AGRICOLA (AGRICULTURAL ONLINE ACCESS) IS A FAMILY OF DATA BASES CONSISTING OF
INDEXES TO WORLDWIDE JOURNAL AND MONOGRAPHIC LITERATURE AND US GOVERNMENT
REPORTS ON GENERAL AGRICULTURE AND RELATED SUBJECT FIELDS. IT INCLUDES
CITATIONS TO MONOGRAPHS, SERIAL TITLES AND JOURNAL ARTICLES SELECTED FROM
6,000 SERIAL TITLES FROM 1970 TO THE PRESENT, AND A FILE SIZE OF OVER 1.4
MILLION RECORDS. IT IS PREPARED BY TECHNICAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS, SCIENCE AND
EDUCATION ADMINISTRATION, US DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE. DESCRIBES ITS SUBFILES
AND A NEW VIDEOTAPE PRODUCED TO OUTLINE ITS INFORMATION SYSTEMS AND SERVICES.
