BUCHANAN, LOCKE AND WITTGENSTEIN ON CLASSIFICATION.
     
BUCHANAN'S ACCOUNT OF CLASSIFICATION (2THEORY OF LIBRARY CLASSIFICATION,
1BINGLEY, 1969) CLAIMS THAT (1) CLASS MEMBERS NECESSARILY SHARE SOMETHING IN
COMMON AND (2) CLASSIFICATION IS A MENTAL ACTIVITY. TRACES BOTH CLAIMS TO
LOCKE AND MAINTAINS THAT THEY ARE CONTENTIOUS. DISCUSSES WITTGENSTEIN'S
ALTERNATIVE THEORY.
