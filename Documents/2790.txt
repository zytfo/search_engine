INTELLIGENT AUTOMATIC INFORMATION RETRIEVAL.
     
CONSIDERS THE PRESENT STATE AND POSSIBLE DEVELOPMENT OF DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL
SYSTEMS AS INTELLIGENT AUTOMATIC INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS. IT RELATES
DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL TO DATA RETRIEVAL ON THE ONE HAND AND FACT RETRIEVAL ON THE
OTHER, AND ARGUES THAT DOCUMENT RETRIEVAL SHOULD BE LESS NARROW IN ITS
PRECONCEPTIONS.
