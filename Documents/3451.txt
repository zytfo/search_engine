RETRIEVING TIME INFORMATION FROM NATURAL-LANGUAGE TEXTS.
     
PRESENTS A PROCEDURE (DEVELOPED DURING RESEARCH IN NATURAL-LANGUAGE PROCESSING
AT THE NEW YORK UNIVERSITY LINGUISTIC STRING PROJECT) TO AUTOMATICALLY
RETRIEVE FROM HOSPITAL DISCHARGE SUMMARIES INFORMATION ABOUT A PATIENT'S STATE
AT VARIOUS TIMES DURING A HOSPITAL STAY AND INFORMATION ABOUT PERFORMANCE OF
CERTAIN MEDICAL PROCEDURES AND THEIR RESULTS.
