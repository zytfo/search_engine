STATEMENT OF POLICY RELATING TO ARCHIVES.
     
A STATEMENT OF POLICY RELATING TO ARCHIVES IN THE UK DEVELOPED BY THE MUSEUMS
ASSOCIATION, SOCIETY OF ARCHIVISTS AND THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION, WITH AN
INTRODUCTION BY PATRICK J. BOYLAN. THE POLICY COVERS' THE LEGAL POSITION OF
ARCHIVE COLLECTIONS IN MUSEUMS AND LIBRARIES; COLLECTING
RESPONSIBILITIES-GENERAL COLLECTIONS; SPECIAL COLLECTIONS; OTHER COLLECTING
AGENCIES; PURCHASE GRANTS; DISPOSAL OF COLLECTIONS; RECORDS MANAGEMENT; AND
AREAS OF POSITIVE COLLABORATION BETWEEN THE 3 PROFESSIONS.
