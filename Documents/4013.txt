VIDEODISCS TECHNOLOGY' EQUIPMENT, SOFTWARE, AND EDUCATIONAL APPLICATIONS.
     
REVIEWS THE DEVELOPMENT OF VIDEODISCS IN GENERAL AND DESCRIBES THE LASER
SYSTEMS AND CAPACITANCE ELECTRONIC DISC SYSTEMS IN PARTICULAR. AVAILABILITY
AND DISTRIBUTION OF VIDEODISC SOFTWARE IS COVERED ALONG WITH THE DEVELOPMENT
AND TESTING OF SYSTEMS IN EDUCATION AND BUSINESS. THE ARTICLE IS FOLLOWED BY A
SURVEY REPORT (P 325-334) COVERING 4 VIDEODISC PLAYERS.
