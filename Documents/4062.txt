THREE PIOUS HOPES.
     
GREETING OF THE INCOMING PRESIDENT TO THE SOCIETY OF AMERICAN ARCHIVISTS AT
THEIR 44TH ANNUAL MEETING, CINCINNATI, OHIO, 3 OCT 80, EXPRESSING 3 'PIOUS
HOPES': (1) THAT MEMBERS OF THE SOCIETY WILL VOICE THEIR FEELINGS STRONGLY TO
COUNCIL AND OFFICERS; (2) THAT THE SOCIETY CAN THINK CONSTRUCTIVELY ABOUT THE
ROLES OF THE PROFESSIONAL STAFF AND ELECTED OFFICERS; AND (3) THAT ATTENDANCE
WILL BE GOOD AT THE SOCIETY'S NEXT ANNUAL MEETING IN BERKELEY.
