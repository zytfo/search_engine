PROLEGOMENA TO THE HISTORY OF INTERNATIONAL LIBRARIANSHIP.
     
IN THE CLOSING DECADES OF THE 20TH CENTURY, THE HISTORY OF INTERNATIONAL
LIBRARIANSHIP IS STILL LARGELY UNCHARTED TERRITORY. DISCUSSES EVIDENCE OF
INTEREST IN INTERNATIONAL LIBRARIANSHIP (WHICH IS DISTINGUISHED FROM
COMPARATIVE LIBRARIANSHIP), THE SUBJECT AND SCOPE OF INTERNATIONAL
LIBRARIANSHIP, HYPOTHESES UNDERLYING HISTORICAL INVESTIGATION, SOURCES OF THE
HISTORY OF THE FIELD, AN AGENDA FOR HISTORICAL SCHOLARSHIP, AND USES OF THE
HISTORY OF INTERNATIONAL LIBRARIANSHIP.
