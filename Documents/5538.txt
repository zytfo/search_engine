LIBRARY MANAGEMENT DEVELOPMENT.

ATTEMPTS A SYSTEMATIC EVALUATION OF THE PERENNIAL DEBATE ON THE RATIONALE AND
PRACTICE OF EDUCATION FOR LIBRARY MANAGEMENT BY REVIEWING CURRENT ATTITUDES,
PERCEIVED NEEDS AND THE EXTENT TO WHICH NEEDS ARE MET BY THE TEACHING
AVAILABLE. CONCLUDES THAT UNSYSTEMATIC APPROACHES TO LIBRARY MANAGEMENT
EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT MAY BE ALIENATING SOME POTENTIAL MANAGERS.
