BRAZILIAN LIBRARIES AS SEEN BY LIBRARIANS AND USERS.

ANSWERS OBTAINED TO A QUESTIONNAIRE CIRCULATED TO PROMINENT BRAZILIAN
INTELLECTUALS AND LIBRARIANS AS A PRELIMINARY TO A SURVEY OF ALL BRAZILIAN
LIBRARIANS AND USERS. 4 USERS, COMPRISING A PROFESSOR, 2 WRITERS, AND A
TEACHER, EACH DISCUSS THE SIGNIFICANCE OF LIBRARIES IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF
BRAZILIAN CULTURE AND THE TRAINING OF INTELLECTUALS; 11 LIBRARIANS FROM
UNIVERSITIES, PUBLIC LIBRARIES, RESEARCH INSTITUTES AND LIBRARY SCIENCE
SCHOOLS, DISCUSS THE FACTORS IMPEDING THE DEVELOPMENT OF LIBRARIES AND
PREVENTING PUBLIC ACCESS.
