THE VIEWTEL 202 EXPERIENCE IN PRESTEL.
     
THE EXPERIENCE GAINED BY THE BIRMINGHAM POST AND MAIL LTD IN SUPPLYING
INFORMATION FOR A VIDEOTEX SYSTEM IS REPORTED. VIEWTEL 202 WAS LAUNCHED IN OCT
78 AS AN ELECTRONIC NEWSPAPER CARRYING ONLY NEWS AND ADVERTISEMENTS ON 4,000
PAGES. 400,000 ACCESSES PER MONTH MAKES IT THE MOST SUCCESSFUL ORGANISATION
INVOLVED IN PRESTEL. THE INCORPORATION OF INFORMATION FROM NORWAY (BERGENS
TIDENDE) IS BRIEFLY MENTIONED. THE LESSONS WHICH HAVE BEEN LEARNT THROUGH
COMMERCIAL INVOLVEMENT WITH VIDEOTEX ARE SUMMARISED.
