UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF INFORMATION.
     
THE UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF PUBLICATIONS (UAP) AND THE UNIVERSAL
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL (UBC) PROGRAMMES ORGANISED BY IFLA SHOULD ENABLE
INFORMATION TO BECOME MORE WIDELY ACCESSIBLE. THEY WILL BE HELPED IN THE NEAR
FUTURE BY THE UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF INFORMATION (UAI) PROGRAMME, THE
OBJECTIVE OF WHICH IS THE REMOVAL OF ALL BARRIERS AND THE UNIFICATION OF
ACTIVITIES FOR THE AVAILABILITY OF INFORMATION IN COUNTRIES ALL OVER THE
WORLD.
