THE SPECIAL GROUPS AND THE ANNUAL MEETING 1981.
     
GIVES THE ANNUAL REPORTS FOR 1980-81 OF THE FOLLOWING SPECIAL GROUPS OF THE
LIBRARIANS' UNION' THE ART GROUP, THE GROUP FOR LIBRARY LEADERS, THE GROUP FOR
OUTREACH LIBRARY SERVICES, THE GROUP FOR BRANCH AND MOBILE LIBRARY WORK, AND
THE GROUP OF MUSIC LIBRARIANS.
