SURVEY' ATTITUDES OF MILITARY PHARMACISTS TOWARD DRUG INFORMATION CENTER
SUPPORT.
     
A STUDY WAS CONDUCTED USING A MAILED QUESTIONNAIRE TO DETERMINE THE ATTITUDES
OF MILITARY PHARMACISTS TOWARD DRUG INFORMATION CENTRE SUPPORT. THE RESEARCH
INVOLVED FOR STUDY AREAS INCLUDING DEMOGRAPHIC INFORMATION; HISTORICAL
EXPERIENCE CONCERNING USE, SATISFACTION, TYPE OF SERVICE DESIRED AND PERCEIVED
NEED FOR THIS SERVICE; SOURCES THAT WERE CURRENTLY UTILISED TO ANSWER DRUG
INFORMATION QUESTIONS; AND HYPOTHESIS TESTING PROCEDURES.
