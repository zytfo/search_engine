CLASSIFICATION AS A STRUCTURAL TYPE OF SUBLANGUAGES OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY.
     
DISCUSSES SOME ASPECTS OF THE STRUCTURE AND LEXICAL COMPOSITION ANALYSIS OF
THE SUBLANGUAGES OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY. DEMONSTRATES THE POSSIBILITIES OF
THE SIMULTANEOUS USE OF CLASSIFICATIONS (PARTICULARLY SUBJECT CLASSIFICATIONS)
AS AN INFORMATION RETRIEVAL LANGUAGE AND AS A SUBLANGUAGE OF SCIENCE AND
TECHNOLOGY.
