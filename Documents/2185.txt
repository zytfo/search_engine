A COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS OF THREE MAJOR MODELS MOST FREQUENTLY USED IN APPLYING
COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY IN ACADEMIC LIBRARIES IN THE SOUTHEAST.
     
(PHD THESIS-GEORGE PEABODY COLLEGE FOR TEACHERS). SURVEYS THE USE MADE OF THE
3 MAJOR MODELS MOST FREQUENTLY USED IN APPLYING COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY IN COLLEGE
AND UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES-NETWORKING, SELF-CONTAINED COMPUTER SYSTEMS, AND A
COMBINATION OF THE 2. IDENTIFIES COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES IN THE SOUTHEAST
USA AND PRESENTS A SERIES OF RECOGNISED OR ASSUMED SERVICE AND OPERATION
REQUIREMENTS, SURVEYS MAJOR TECHNOLGIES APPLICABLE TO THESE LIBRARIES AND
REVIEWS THE DECISION MAKING PROCESS INVOLVED IN SELECTING SUCH.
