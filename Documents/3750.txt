THE ELECTRICIAN.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS OF SELECTED
PROFESSIONALS. REPORT ON THE INFORMATION NEEDS AND USES MADE OF THAT
INFORMATION MADE BY AN ELECTRICIAN, JIM WILSON. 4 WAYS OF OBTAINING
INFORMATION WERE DISCUSSED' (1) INFORMAL INTERPERSONAL OR TALKING WITH OTHERS;
(2) FORMAL INTERPERSONAL, WHICH INCLUDES MEETINGS AND CONFERENCES; (3)
LITERATURE; AND (4) MASS MEDIA. ALSO DISCUSSED WERE BARRIERS AND LIMITATIONS
IN ACQUIRING INFORMATION.
