AN UPDATE OF A.H. HOLLOWAY'S 1970 WORK 'A STUDY OF COPYRIGHT' (ADVISORY REPORT
NO 23). IT DESCRIBES DEVELOPMENTS SINCE THAT DATE, CONCENTRATING ON THE
SITUATION IN THE USA AND UK BUT INCLUDING COMMENTS ON THE POSITION IN THE NATO
MEMBER NATIONS AND SEVERAL OTHER COUNTRIES. AS IN THE EARLIER PUBLICATION, THE
EMPHASIS IS ON PHOTOCOPYING IN LIBRARIES AND ON OTHER MATTERS WHICH DIRECTLY
AFFECT INFORMATION TRANSFER, SUCH AS SOFTWARE AND DATA BASE PROTECTION. SOME
OF THE CURRENT INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENTS ARE MENTIONED, INCLUDING THE EFFECTS
OF THE EEC AND THE ACCESSION OF THE USSR TO THE UNIVERSAL COPYRIGHT
CONVENTION.
