UNIVERSITY OF DAR ES SALAAM LIBRARY.
     
THE UNIVERSITY OF DAR-ES-SALAAM LIBRARY SERVES SEVERAL THOUSANDS OF READERS
REGULARLY. THE LIBRARY ACQUIRES ABOUT 10,000 DOCUMENTS PER YEAR. THE PROBLEMS
THE LIBRARY IS FACING ARE MANY AND VARIED. HIGHLIGHTS SOME OF THE PROBLEMS AND
DESCRIBES SOME MEASURES THAT HAVE BEEN TAKEN TO SOLVE THEM. THE SERVICES,
CATALOGUING AND ACQUISITION PROCEDURES OF THE LIBRARY ARE DESCRIBED.
