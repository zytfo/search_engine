THE CONGRESSIONAL RESEARCH SERVICE OF THE UNITED STATES CONGRESS.
     
THE CONGRESSIONAL RESEARCH SERVICE, FOUNDED IN 1914, IS STAFFED BY 542
RESEARCH SPECIALISTS WHO PROVIDE INFORMATION SERVICES SOLELY TO THE
LEGISLATIVE FUNCTION OF THE US GOVERNMENT. CRS SERVICES ARE AVAILABLE TO ANY
MEMBER OF CONGRESS OR STAFF ASSISTANT SEEKING INFORMATION AND CURRENTLY
SUPPORT 2,000 INQUIRIES PER DAY. THE REPORT DETAILS THE OPERATIONS OF THESE
SERVICES AND DISCUSSES SOME OF THE DIFFICULTIES AND LIMITATIONS FACED BY CRS.
REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
