THE FUTURE OF THE BOOK.

SUMMARY OF A PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 FESTIVAL OF BOOKS, SPONSORED BY THE
NATIONAL BOOK DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL OF SINGAPORE. PERSONAL VIEW OF THE FUNCTION
OF THE BOOK IN SOCIETY, SUGGESTING THAT NEW TECHNOLOGY WILL NOT SUPERCEDE THE
BOOK-WHAT IS NEEDED IS NOT EVEN BETTER BOOKS, BUT BETTER DISSEMINATION OF
BOOKS. ULTIMATELY, THE BOOK PUTS US IN TOUCH WITH OURSELVES.
