MULTUM IN PARVO': APPLICATIONS OF MICROFILM IN CATHEDRAL LIBRARIES.P
     
APER DELIVERED AT THE CATHEDRAL LIBRARIANS CONFERENCE, LONDON, 10 NOV 80.
DISCUSSES THE ADVANTAGES ADHERENT IN MICROFILMING ARCHIVAL COLLECTIONS. NOTES
ITS PARTICULAR APPLICATION TO PRESERVATION AND SECURITY NEEDS AND TO THE
DISSEMINATION TO SCHOLARS AND INSTITUTIONS WORLDWIDE OF COPIES OF RARE AND
DIFFICULT-TO-ACCESS MATERIALS FOR USE IN RESEARCH.
