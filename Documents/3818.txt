INFORMATION AND REFERRAL SERVICES: THE RESOURCE FILE.
     
AT THE PRESENT TIME THERE IS A SURGE OF INTEREST IN INFORMATION AND REFERRAL
(I&R) SERVICES. THIS MANUAL IS PART OF A SERIES OF PUBLICATIONS THAT IS
INTENDED TO RESPOND TO THAT INTEREST AND TO PROVIDE A SOLID BASE FOR A
COMPREHENSIVE APPROACH TO THE PROMISES AND THE PROBLEMS OF I&R SERVICES. IT
HAS BEEN PREPARED TO FILL A CRITICAL NEED' TO PROVIDE DETAILED PROCEDURES OF A
METHOD FOR GATHERING, STORING, AND RETRIEVING INFORMATION ABOUT HUMAN
SERVICES.
