DATABASE USER AIDS AND MATERIALS-A STUDY.
     
BASED ON INFORMATION SOLICITED FROM DATA BASE PRODUCERS WHICH HAD FILES
PUBLICLY AVAILABLE ON SDC AND/OR LOCKHEED, THIS STUDY IS INTENDED TO HELP
SEARCHERS ACCESS AVAILABLE DATA BASE SUPPORT MATERIALS WITH REGARD TO THEIR
COST, FORMAT, DEPTH, AND PURPOSE. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
