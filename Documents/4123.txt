UNIVERSITY LIBRARY REPORTS FOR 1980.
     
DISCUSSES THE PREDOMINANT THEMES WHICH EMERGE FROM A SURVEY OF AUSTRALIAN
UNIVERSITY LIBRARY REPORTS FOR 1980. THESE THEMES INCLUDE' REDUCTIONS IN
PURCHASING POWER; THE EFFECT OF THE INCREASING DEMAND FOR INTERLIBRARY LOANS;
INADEQUATE STAFFING AND THE CONSEQUENT DETERIORATION OF SERVICES; AND
REDUCTIONS IN BORROWING FIGURES.
