GUIDE TO APPLICATIONS' PUBLIC USE ENERGY STATISTICAL DATA BASE (PUESDB) OF THE
NATIONAL ENERGY INFORMATION SYSTEM (NEIS).
     
DESCRIBES THE PUBLIC USE ENERGY STATISTICAL DATA BASE OF THE NATIONAL ENERGY
INFORMATION SYSTEM (NEIS). THE PUESDB IS COMPOSED OF ENERGY QUANTITY AND PRICE
INFORMATION AT THE NATIONAL LEVEL RELATING TO PETROLEUM, NATURAL GAS, COAL,
AND ELECTRICITY, AS WELL AS ECONOMIC AND OTHER DATA RELEVANT TO ENERGY
ANALYSIS.
