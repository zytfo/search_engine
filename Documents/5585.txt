BOOKS BY BOAT TO THE ARCHIPELAGO POPULATION.

CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO PUBLIC LIBRARY SERVICES IN SWEDEN. OFF THE
COAST OF SWEDEN'S BOHUS COUNTY LIE OVER 3,000 ISLANDS, MANY INHIBITED BUT
LACKING PERMANENT COMMUNICATION WITH THE MAINLAND. EACH APRIL AND SEPTEMBER A
'BOOK BOAT' SETS OUT FROM THE GOTHENBURG COUNTY LIBRARY FOR A 2-WEEK TRIP
ALONG THE 120-MILE COASTLINE. IN 1981 IT LENT OVER 18,000 VOLUMES FROM 49
LENDING POINTS. DESCRIBES THE BOAT'S ORGANISATION, STAFFING, FUNCTIONS AND
OPERATIONS.
