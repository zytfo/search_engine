THE MONTREAL MECHANICS' INSTITUTE' 1828-1870.
     
A HISTORY OF THE MONTREAL MECHANICS' INSTITUTE, QUEBEC, THE 1ST INSTITUTION OF
ITS KIND IN BRITISH NORTH AMERICA. THE MONTREAL MECHANICS' INSTITUTION
OPERATED FROM 1828-35 WHEN IT LAPSED DUE TO SOCIAL AND POLITICAL PRESSURES.
REVIEWED IN 1840 AS THE MONTREAL MECHANICS' INSTITUTE, IT CONTINUES TO THIS
DAY AS THE ATWATER LIBRARY OF MONTREAL.
