THE STATUS OF MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS (MIS) IN STATE DEPARTMENTS OF
EDUCATION. CASE STUDIES.
     
COMPLEMENTING A 1973 SURVEY OF MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN STATE
DEPARTMENTS OF EDUCATION, THE CONCLUSIONS DEVELOPED IN THIS STUDY PRESENT AN
OVERVIEW OF MIS DEVELOPMENT AND IMPLEMENTATION IN 6 STATES' GEORGIA, IOWA,
MARYLAND, OREGON, TEXAS, AND UTAH. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
