WARGAMES' A MODEST DEFENSE OF SOME BLOODY DOINGS.
     
STRESSES THE GREAT EDUCATIONAL VALUE OF WARGAMES AND ADVOCATES THEIR PRESENCE
IN MEDIA CENTRES AND LIBRARIES. GIVE TITLES OF BASIC WARGAMES LITERATURE AND
MAGAZINES; DISCUSSES HOW TO START A COLLECTION; AND SUGGESTS ITS SCOPE. A
SUCCESSFUL COLLECTION WILL BECOME THE CENTRE OF A SUBCULTURE, WITH ITS OWN
ORGANISATION, CONVENTIONS, AND AMATEUR PUBLICATIONS. A WARGAMES PROGRAMME
COULD INCORPORATE RELATED MATERIAL SUCH AS HISTORIES, BIOGRAPHIES, AND
HISTORICAL NOVELS AND FILMS.
