MICROELECTRONICS' THE IMPLICATIONS FOR IRELAND.
     
A REPORT DEALING WITH THE IMPLICATIONS OF MICROELECTRONICS TECHNOLOGY FOR
EIRE'S ECONOMY OVER THE NEXT 10 YEARS. OUTLINES THE POTENTIAL IMPACT OF THE
TECHNOLOGY ON THE NATURE AND LEVELS OF EMPLOYMENT IN INDUSTRY AND ASSESSES THE
NEW INDUSTRIAL AND EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES ARISING FROM ITS USE.
