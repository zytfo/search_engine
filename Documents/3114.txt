THE BRILLOUIN INFORMATION MEASURE APPLIED TO MATERIALS SELECTION.
     
DESCRIBES THE USE OF THE BRILLOUIN FORMULA TO ASSESS THE MEASURE OF IMPACT OF
AN ADDED DOCUMENT ON THE DIVERSITY OR RELATEDNESS OF A SUBJECT COLLECTION. THE
BRILLOUIN FORMULA IS AN INFORMATION MEASURE WHICH HAS BEEN USED TO MEASURE THE
ORDER OR DISORDER OF A PARTICULAR POPULATION DISTRIBUTED OVER A NUMBER OF
UNIQUE CLASSES. CONCLUDES THAT THIS FORMULA CAN MEASURE THE IMPACT OF AN ADDED
DOCUMENT ON THE DIVERSITY OR RELATEDNESS OF A SUBJECT COLLECTION WHEN THE
COMPUTATION IS MADE USING ONE-WAY COMMUNICATION.
