RESEARCH SEMINAR ON TECHNOLOGICAL CHANGE.
     
REPORT BASED ON A RESEARCH SEMINAR AT THE UNIVERSITY OF SHEFFIELD,
POSTGRADUATE SCHOOL OF LIBRARIANSHIP AND INFORMATION SCIENCE (UK), 20 JUNE 80.
AIMS OF THE SEMINAR WERE' TO ATTEMPT TO ISOLATE PARTICULAR FORCES OF
TECHNOLOGICAL CHANGE OPERATING UPON LIBRARIES/INFORMATION AGENCIES AND TO
DETERMINE WHICH OF THESE DESERVED SYSTEMATIC STUDY; AND TO DISCUSS AND SUGGEST
RESEARCH TECHNIQUES AND APPROACHES APPROPRIATE TO SPECIFIC CHANGE ISSUES AND
PROBLEMS. SUMMARISES THE DISCUSSIONS AND A KEY ADDRESS BY PETER CLARK.
