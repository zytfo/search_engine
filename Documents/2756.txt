HANDICAPPED PERSONS' SERVICE DIRECTORIES, GUIDES, AND HANDBOOKS. JANUARY,
1970-SEPTEMBER, 1981 (CITATIONS FROM THE NTIS DATA BASE).
     
THE BIBLIOGRAPHY CITES VARIOUS DIRECTORIES, HANDBOOKS, CATALOGUES, GUIDES AND
MANUALS CONCERNING DISABLED PERSONS AND THOSE WHO PROVIDE A VARIETY OF
SERVIES, EQUIPMENT, AND PRODUCTS TO THEM. SOME OF THE PUBLICATIONS CITED
CONCERN RESULTS OF RESEARCH, DEVELOPMENT, OR DEMONSTRATION PROJECTS RELATED TO
DISABLED OR HANDICAPPED PERSONS.
