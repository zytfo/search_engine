THE ROLE OF THE MEDICAL LIBRARY ASSOCIATION IN EDUCATION, STANDARDS AND OTHER
SUPPORT SERVICES FOR MEMBERS.
     
EMPHASISES THE MAJOR ACTIVITIES OF THE MEDICAL LIBRARY ASSOCIATION IN THE
EXCHANGE OF MATERIALS AMONG MEDICAL LIBRARIES, THE EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION OF
IMPORTANCE TO THE EFFICIENT OPERATION OF MEDICAL LIBRARIES, THE EDUCATION OF
MEDICAL AND MEDICAL-RELATED LIBRARIANS AND DEVELOPMENT OF STANDARDS AND
GUIDELINES. POSES SOME OF THE PROBLEMS RELATED TO EACH OF THESE ACTIVITIES AND
ROLES.
