POLICY IMPLICATIONS OF DATA NETWORK DEVELOPMENTS IN THE OECD AREA.
     
PROCEEDINGS OF A SPECIAL SESSION OF THE ICCP WORKING PARTY, PARIS, 13-15 SEPT
78. THE OBJECTIVES OF THE MEETING WERE TO CONSTITUTE A FORUM FOR PRESENTATION
OF PLANS AND DEVELOPMENTS IN THE BUILD-UP OF DATA NETWORKS IN MEMBER
COUNTRIES; DISCUSSION OF OPERATIONAL ISSUES; CONSIDERATION OF THE NEED FOR
OECD-WIDE DATA NETWORK POLICIES; AND IDENTIFICATION OF SPECIFIC PROBLEMS FOR
THE SOLUTION OF WHICH THE OECD MAY BE THE PROPER SETTING.
