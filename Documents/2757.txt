SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION SERVICES FOR SOCIOECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT.
VOLUME 11. RESEARCH PAPER.
     
3 PAPERS WRITTEN IN PREPARATION FOR THE 1979 U.N. CONFERENCE ON SCIENCE AND
TECHNOLOGY FOR DEVELOPMENT. THE 1ST PAPER, BY TEFKO SARACEVIC, SURVEYS THE
VIEWS ON SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION EXPRESSED BY DEVELOPING
COUNTRIES AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATIONS. THE 2ND PAPER, BY DAVIS MCCARN,
SURVEYS THE INFORMATION RESOURCES OF THE U.S., PARTICULARLY FROM THE VIEW
POINT OF THEIR RELEVANCE AND AVAILABILITY TO LESS DEVELOPED COUNTRIES. THE
LAST PAPER, BY VLADIMIR SLAMECKA, SUMMARISES THE DIMENSIONS INVOLVED IN THE
USE OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION FOR ADVANCING SOCIOECONOMIC
DEVELOPMENT.
