LIBRARY CAREER, PROCESS OF CHANGES, DEVELOPMENT TENDENCIES.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO A THEMATIC ISSUE ON LIBRARY TRAINING. THE ACCELERATED
DEVELOPMENT OF CULTURE, SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY HAS RESULTED IN A NEW TYPE OF
LIBRARY USER. THE USER NOW REQUIRES NOT ONLY LIBRARY MATERIAL, BUT ALSO
ANALYSED, EVALUATED INFORMATION. THIS IN TURN HAS GIVEN RISE TO NEW
PROFESSIONAL BRANCHES OF LIBRARIANSHIP AND INFORMATION SCIENCE. SUMMARISES THE
ESSENTIAL ELEMENTS WHICH FORM AND SHAPE CAREERS IN THIS FIELD.
