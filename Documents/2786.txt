TELECONFERENCED GROUP DECISION MAKING' DESIGNING FOR IMPROVED PERFORMANCE. (2
PARTS).
     
THIS PROJECT IS CONCERNED WITH TRYING TO GET THE BEST OUT OF TELECONFERENCING.
IT FORMS PART OF A BROADER PROGRAMME OF RESEARCH BY DARPA TO BUILD A PERSONAL
COMMUNICATION SYSTEM TO LINK MEMBERS OF THE GOVERNMENT EXECUTIVE AND THE
MILITARY COMMAND AT TIMES OF NATIONAL EMERGENCY WHEN IT MAY BE INOPPORTUNE OR
INADVISABLE TO HAVE ALL THE CONTRIBUTORS TO DECISION MAKING IN 1 PLACE.
