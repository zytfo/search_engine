U.S. INFORMATION POLICY ISSUES.
     
THE ROLE OF THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON LIBRARIES AND INFORMATION SCIENCE IN
FORMULATING US INFORMATION POLICIES IS DESCRIBED. THE ISSUES DISCUSSED ARE
GROUPED INTO 4 CATEGORIES. PUBLIC VS. PRIVATE SECTOR QUESTIONS; ECONOMIC
ISSUES; EDUCATION AND TRAINING; INTERNATIONAL ISSUES. FINALLY, A PLEA IS MADE
FOR THE PRINCIPLE OF INTELLECTUAL FREEDOM.
