PROCEEDINGS OF THE SEMINAR ON THE DOD COMPUTER SECURITY INITIATIVE PROGRAM,
NATIONAL BUREAU OF STANDARDS.
     
A SERIES OF 3 SEMINARS TO ACQUAINT COMPUTER SYSTEM DEVELOPERS AND USERS WITH
THE STATUS OF 'TRUSTED' ADP SYSTEM DEVELOPMENTS WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT OF
DEFENSE AND CURRENT PLANNING FOR THE INTEGRITY EVALUATION OF COMMERCIAL
IMPLEMENTATIONS OF SIMILAR SYSTEMS.
