LIBRARIES AND ADULT EDUCATION' THE RUSSIAN EXPERIENCE.
     
DISCUSSES THE DEVELOPMENT OF ADULT EDUCATION IN RUSSIA, A PROCESS WHICH
OCCURRED IN THE 2ND HALF OF THE 19TH CENTURY, SPECIFICALLY FOCUSING ON HOW
PUBLIC LIBRARIES WERE UTILISED AS INSTRUMENTS OF ADULT EDUCATION, BOTH BEFORE
THE REVOLUTION, AND ESPECIALLY DURING THE 1ST DECADE OF SOVIET RULE.
