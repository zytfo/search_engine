BIBLIOTEKSTJANST-A PRESENTATION.

CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO PUBLIC LIBRARY SERVICES IN SWEDEN.
BIBLIOTEKSTJANST IS THE SWEDISH LIBRARY SERVICE, WHOSE MAIN TASK IS TO HELP
STREAMLINE THE SWEDISH LIBRARY SYSTEM. IT HAS BEEN SHOWN THAT ALMOST ALL TYPES
OF LIBRARY WORK CAN BE CONDUCTED MORE EFFICIENTLY AND RATIONALLY WITH THE AID
OF CENTRALLY PRODUCED SERVICES AND AIDS. BIBLIOTEKSTJANST, AS THE CENTRAL
SERVICE ORGAN, HAS THUS ACQUIRED A VERY WIDE FIELD OF OPERATION.
