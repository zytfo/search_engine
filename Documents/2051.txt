STUDENT'S GUIDE TO SOME LIBRARIES IN SUSSEX.
     
INTENDED TO HELP THE PURPOSIVE USER TO IDENTIFY THOSE SUSSEX LIBRARIES WHICH
HAVE STOCK RELEVANT TO HIS NEEDS. COMPRISES 3 SECTIONS' RESOURCES-AN
ALPHABETICAL LIST OF SUBJECTS GIVING AT LEAST 1 LIBRARY FOR EACH SUBJECT;
KEY-INDICATES LOCATION OF LIBRARIES; AND DIRECTORY-PROVIDING GENERAL
INFORMATION ABOUT EACH LIBRARY.
