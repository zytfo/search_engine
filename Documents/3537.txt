P.N. BERKOV AND THE BEGINNINGS OF SOVIET RUSSIAN BOOK STUDIES (1923-1935).
     
OUTLINES THE CAREER OF PAVEL N. BERKOV, RUSSIAN TEACHER AND PUBLISHING
SCHOLAR; FOCUSSING ON HIS BIBLIOGRAPHICAL WORK PUBLISHED BETWEEN 1923-1935.
DISCUSSES BERKOV'S MAIN CONTRIBUTIONS AS A BIBLIOGRAPHER, INCLUDING' HIS
ATTEMPT TO APPLY MARXIST-LENINIST METHODOLOGY TO BOOK STUDIES; AND HIS VIEWS
ON THE TECHNIQUES OF BIBLIOGRAPHICAL SEARCHING, OR HEURISTICS.
