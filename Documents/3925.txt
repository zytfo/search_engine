SATELLITE TELECOMMUNICATIONS-SOME POTENTIAL APPLICATIONS STI TRANSFER.
     
DISCUSSES THE USE OF COMMUNICATIONS SATELLITES AS AN EXAMPLE OF POTENTIAL
CHANGES IN METHODS OF DISSEMINATING SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION. IT
BRIEFLY REVIEWS THE BASIC RELEVANT CHARACTERISTICS OF COMMUNICATIONS
SATELLITES (BANDWIDTH, VERY HIGH DATA RATES, MULTI-DESTINATION PROPERTIES AND
SMALL-RECEIVE-ONLY EARTH STATIONS). THE WORK OF COSADOC, SET UP BY THE
EUROPEAN SPACE AGENCY TO REVIEW POSSIBLE SATELLITE APPLICATION AREAS IN
INFORMATION TRANSFER IS BRIEFLY NOTED, INCLUDING DELIVERY OF PICTORIAL DATA
(EARTH RESOURCES PHOTOGRAPHS), FULL-TEXT DOCUMENT DELIVERY WITH ON-LINE
BROWSING AND DATA BASE DISTRIBUTION.
