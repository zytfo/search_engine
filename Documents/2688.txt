EXCERPTS FROM NEW YORK STATE EDUCATION LAW, RULES OF THE BOARD OF REGENTS, AND
REGULATIONS OF THE COMMISSIONER OF EDUCATION PERTAINING TO PUBLIC AND FREE
ASSOCIATION LIBRARIES, LIBRARY SYSTEMS, TRUSTEES AND LIBRARIANS.
     
THIS COLLECTION OF EXCERPTS CONTAINS NEW YORK STATE EDUCATIONAL LEGISLATION,
BOARD OF REGENTS REGULATIONS, AND THE COMMISSIONER OF EDUCATION'S RULES
RELATED TO PUBLIC LIBRARIES, LIBRARY NETWORKS, BOARDS OF TRUSTEES, AND LIBRARY
PROFESSIONALS. IT INCLUDES ALL PERTINENT CHANGES IN EDUCATIONAL LAW ENACTED
THROUGH THE OCTOBER 1979 LEGISLATIVE SESSION AND CHANGES OF REGENTS RULES AND
COMMISSIONER'S REGULATIONS THROUGH THE REGENTS MEETING IN NOVEMBER 1979.
