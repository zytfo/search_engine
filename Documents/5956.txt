THE IFE BOOK FAIR-A REPORT.

REPORT ON THE 6TH IFE BOOK FAIR, LAGOS, MAR 81. THE NORMA AWARD FOR PUBLISHING
IN AFRICA WAS AWARDED TO F. ADI FOR 2HEALTH EDUCATION FOR THE COMMUNITY1.
MAJOR AND MINOR PUBLISHERS FROM AFRICA, USA, UK AND CHINA WERE REPRESENTED.
ORGANISATIONAL DIFFICULTIES PREVENTED 'TEACH-INS' TAKING PLACE, AND BOOKED
SPEAKERS DID NOT APPEAR, WITH THE RESULT THAT IMPROMPTU SPEAKERS ATTEMPTED NO
NEW ANALYSIS OF NIGERIAN AND GENERAL AFRICAN PUBLISHING PROBLEMS.
