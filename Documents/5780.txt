COMPUTER-BASED NATIONAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS. TECHNOLOGY AND PUBLIC POLICY
ISSUES.

PRESENTS THE RESULTS OF A STUDY ON THE USE OF COMPUTER TECHNOLOGY IN NATIONAL
INFORMATION SYSTEMS AND RELATED PUBLIC POLICY ISSUES. THE PURPOSE OF THE STUDY
WAS TO PROVIDE' A GENERAL INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTERISED NATIONAL INFORMATION
SYSTEMS; A FRAMEWORK FOR UNDERSTANDING COMPUTER/INFORMATION POLICY ISSUES; A
STATE OF THE ART SURVEY OF COMPUTER AND RELATED TECHNOLOGIES/INDUSTRIES; AND A
FOUNDATION FOR OTHER RELATED STUDIES.
