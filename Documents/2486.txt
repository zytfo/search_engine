POWERS ON ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING.
     
THE VARIETY OF DIFFERENT FORMS WHICH ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING TAKES IS CONSIDERED
WITH PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO ON-LINE INFORMATION SERVICES, PERSONAL COMPUTERS,
INTERACTIVE TV, ELECTRONIC YELLOW PAGES, BROADCASTING AND CABLE TV,
VIDEOCASSETTES AND VIDEODISCS AND ON-DEMAND PRINTING. BRIEF DETAILS ARE GIVEN
OF EACH OF THESE TECHNOLOGIES.
