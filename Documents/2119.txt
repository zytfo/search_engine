RESOURCES FOR SOVIET, EAST EUROPEAN AND SLAVONIC STUDIES IN BRITISH LIBRARIES.
     
RECORDS LIBRARY COLLECTIONS IN THE UK LIKELY TO BE OF USE FOR ADVANCED STUDY
AND RESEARCH AND DESCRIBES THEIR CONTENTS IN SUFFICIENT DETAIL FOR THE
ENQUIRER TO MAKE AN INFORMED CHOICE BETWEEN THEM. SUBJECT COVERAGE IS ALL
AREAS OF THE HUMANITIES AND SOCIAL SCIENCES. GEOGRAPHICAL COVERAGE IS ALL
TERRITORY OCCUPIED BY THE USSR, POLAND, CZECHOSLOVAKIA, EAST GERMANY (SINCE
1945), HUNGARY, YUGOSLAVIA, RUMANIA, BULGARIA, AND ALBANIA. FOR EACH
COLLECTION, GIVES DETAILS OF CONTENT, ADMISSION, INTERLIBRARY LOANS,
FACILITIES, AND PUBLICATIONS.
