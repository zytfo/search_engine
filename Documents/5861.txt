A PROPOSAL FOR PLANNING AN INTERLENDING SYSTEM FOR THE LIBRARIES OF CAIRO
CITY, EGYPT.

(PHD DISSERTATION-PITTSBURGH UNIVERSITY). A QUESTIONNAIRE SURVEY, INTERVIEWS,
VISITS, AND A LITERATURE ANALYSIS WERE USED TO EXPOSE THE FACTORS RESPONSIBLE
FOR THE PRESENT STATE OF INTERLIBRARY LOAN IN CAIRO. A SYSTEM FOR FORMAL
INTERLIBRARY LENDING WHICH CAN BE MODIFIED FOR OTHER DEVELOPING COUNTRIES IS
PROPOSED.
