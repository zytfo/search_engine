STUDY OF MESSAGE TEXT FORMATS' BIBLIOGRAPHIC SEARCH QUERIES.
     
PRESENTS AN ANALYSIS OF THE SEARCH QUERY ARGUMENTS OF SEVERAL MAJOR
BIBLIOGRAPHIC UTILITIES AND SHOWS THE COMMONALITIES AND DIFFERENCES OF THE
QUERY STRUCTURE AMONG THE VARIOUS SYSTEMS. THE REPORT ILLUSTRATES A SYMBOLIC
META-LANGUAGE THAT WAS DEVELOPED TO DESCRIBE AND COMPARE THE SEARCH ARGUMENTS.
IN ADDITION, STATUS AND ERROR MESSAGES USED IN CONJUNCTION WITH SEARCH QUERIES
THAT SHOULD BE INCORPORATED IN THE NATIONAL COMMISSION ON LIBRARIES AND
INFORMATION SCIENCE (NCLIS), NATIONAL BUREAU OF STANDARDS (NBS) APPLICATIONS
LEVEL PROTOCOL ARE SPECIFIED. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
