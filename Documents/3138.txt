VIDEOCASSETTE SERVICES IN QUEBEC PUBLIC LIBRARIES' THE PRESENT SITUATION.
     
REPORTS THE RESULTS OF A SMALL SURVEY BY INTERVIEW IN 1981 OF VIDEOCASSETTE
SERVICES IN QUEBEC PUBLIC LIBRARIES. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE EQUIPMENT
AVAILABLE, NUMBER OF LIBRARIES INVOLVED, THE SYSTEMS USED, AND CHOICE OF
TITLES. A COMPARISON IS MADE WITH THE U.S.A., AND IN CONCLUSION EXPANSION OF
THIS SERVICE AS AN INTEGRAL PART OF LIBRARY PROVISION URGED.
