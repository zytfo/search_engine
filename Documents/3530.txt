AS I KNEW IT: THE LIBRARY WORLD.
     
OUTLINES THE HISTORY OF THE JOURNAL THE LIBRARY WORLD (LATER TO BECOME NEW
LIBRARY WORLD) DURING THE YEARS OF THE AUTHOR'S EDITORSHIP-1960-71. DISCUSSES
THE JOURNAL'S MAIN FEATURES AND POLICY UNDER THE SUCCESSIVE OWNERSHIP OF:
CLIVE BINGLEY; ANDRE DEUTSCH; AND W.H. SMITH. DESCRIBES BRIEFLY THE BACKGROUND
TO THE REPURCHASE OF THE JOURNAL BY CLIVE BINGLEY IN 1971, AND THE REASONS FOR
THE AUTHOR'S SUBSEQUENT RESIGNATION AS EDITOR.
