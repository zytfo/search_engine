SCHOOL ENTRANCE REQUIREMENTS AND THE TIMING OF LIBRARY EDUCATION IN THE SOUTH
PACIFIC...A FEW THOUGHTS.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT A UNESCO/IFLA PRE-CONFERENCE SEMINAR ON LIBRARY EDUCATION,
MANILA 80. CONSIDERS SCHOOL ENTRANCE REQUIREMENTS AND THE TIMING OF EDUCATION
WITHIN THE CONTEXT OF LIBRARY EDUCATION PROGRAMMES IN DEVELOPING COUNTRIES,
PARTICULARLY THE SOUTH PACIFIC AND OCEANIA.
