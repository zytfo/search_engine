HEBREW MANUSCRIPTS IN NEW YORK LIBRARIES (IN HEBREW).
     
SURVEYS COLLECTIONS OF HEBREW MANUSCRIPTS IN 13 INSTITUTIONAL LIBRARIES AND
PRIVATE COLLECTIONS IN NEW YORK CITY. THE MORE IMPORTANT AND INTERESTING
MANUSCRIPTS ARE DESCRIBED IN BRIEF. A SHORT DESCRIPTION OF THE HEBREW
MANUSCRIPTS IN 5 INSTITUTIONAL LIBRARIES AND 1 PRIVATE COLLECTION IN
WASHINGTON D.C. AND PHILADELPHIA IS ALSO INCLUDED.
