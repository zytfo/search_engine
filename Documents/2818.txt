CLASSIFICATION OF CONNECTORS AND INTERACTIVE SYSTEMS OF AUTOMATIC ABSTRACTING.
     
CONSIDERS HOW TO CREATE AN INTERACTIVE SYSTEM OF AUTOMATIC ABSTRACTING IN
WHICH USERS: INQUIRIES CONCERNING VARIOUS ASPECTS OF DOCUMENT CONTENTS
(RESULTS OF WORK, METHODOLOGY, EXAMPLES, ETC.) CAN BE ANSWERED. OFFERS A
METHOD OF AUTOMATIC 'QUESTIONNAIRE-ASPECT' ABSTRACTING IN WHICH ASPECTS ARE
REPRESENTED BY 'CONNECTEDNESS INDICATORS' (SUCH AS PARENTHESES, CONJUNCTIONS,
PRONOUNS, AND REITERATIONS) WHICH REFLECT SEMANTIC AND LOGICAL-SEMANTIC LINKS
BETWEEN THE SENTENCES OF A CONNECTED TEXT.
