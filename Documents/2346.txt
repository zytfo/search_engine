COLLECTION EVALUATION.
     
ALTHOUGH BOOKS ARE SO EXPENSIVE THAT TO THROW AWAY ANY SEEMS A MAJOR STEP, IT
IS AS IMPORTANT AS EVER TO KEEP A SCHOOL LIBRARY COLLECTION ATTRACTIVE AND
UP-TO-DATE BY REMOVING ALL BOOKS THAT ARE NO LONGER OF VALUE. OFFERS
GUIDELINES FOR EVALUATING AND WITHDRAWING BOOKS AND EXPLAINS WHY DESTRUCTION
IS THE MOST SUITABLE FATE FOR MOST DISCARDED STOCK.
