STRATEGIES FOR AGRICULTURAL INFORMATION REVIEWS AND GREY LITERATURE.

AS A RESULT OF THE CRAIG REPORT (2SEE1 80/4557) AND ITS FOLLOW-UP (2SEE1
81/3454) THE IMPORTANCE OF, AND THE NEED TO DEVELOP BETTER INFORMATION SYSTEMS
FOR, NON-CONVENTIONAL LITERATURE AND REVIEWS WAS HIGHLIGHTED. THE OBJECTIVES
OF THE STUDY WERE TO PRESENT A COMMENTARY ON GREY LITERATURE AND REVIEWS IN
AGRICULTURAL INFORMATION IN THE UK AND TO PROVIDE AN INDEPENDENT ASSESSMENT OF
THE RESEARCH NEEDS IN THE AREA AND MAKE SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS FOR THEIR
IMPLEMENTATION.
