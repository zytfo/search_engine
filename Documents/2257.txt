ENERGY INFORMATION REFERRAL DIRECTORY. FOURTH QUARTER 1980.
     
THIS DIRECTORY PROVIDES THE NAME, ADDRESS, AND PHONE NUMBER OF VARIOUS ENERGY
INFORMATION OFFICES WITHIN THE DOE AND OTHER FEDERAL AGENCIES. THE ARRANGEMENT
IS TOPICAL. EACH ENTRY PRESENTS THE NAME OF THE OFFICE, THE ADDRESS, THE MAIN
CONTACT PERSON, AND A SUMMARY OF THE OFFICE'S PRIMARY ACTIVITIES.
