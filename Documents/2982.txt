ANALYSIS OF THE CHOICE OF JOURNALS FOR PUBLICATION BY TRIDIAN PHYSICISTS.
     
ANALYSES THE DATA REGARDING MEDIA OF PUBLICATION BY INDIAN PHYSICISTS ON THE
BASIS OF 2PHYSICS ABSTRACTS1, 1973, GIVING DISTRIBUTION OF RESEARCH OUTPUT
ACCORDING TO THE INSTITUTIONS, FIELD OF SPECIALISATION AND GEOGRAPHICAL
DISTRIBUTION IN FOREIGN JOURNALS. PROVIDES DISTRIBUTION OF PHYSICS RESEARCH
OUTPUTS IN INDIAN JOURNALS. STRESSES NEED FOR COLLABORATION BETWEEN EDITORS OF
PHYSICS JOURNALS IN INDIA.
