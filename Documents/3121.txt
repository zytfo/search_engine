HOSPITAL ARCHIVES' THE EXAMPLE OF NOTRE-DAME HOSPITAL, QUEBEC.
     
DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE NATURE AND LOCATION OF THE ARCHIVE HOLDINGS OF THE
NOTRE-DAME HOSPITAL, QUEBEC AND HOW THE PROBLEMS OF DEALING WITH LARGE STORES
OF DOCUMENTS DISPERSED OVER NUMEROUS DEPARTMENTS WERE SOLVED. THE INITIAL
TOPOGRAPHICAL INVENTORY BASED ON THE DECENTRALISED SITUATION IS ILLUSTRATED,
AND FULL DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM, CATALOGUING AND
INDEXING PROCEDURES EVOLVED TO MEET USER AND DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS. IN
CONCLUSION RECOMMENDATIONS ARE PRESENTED RELATING TO CENTRALISATION OF ALL
DOCUMENTS AND CONSERVATION WORK, SHOWING THE ADVANTAGES TO BE GAINED FROM A
MORE THOROUGH-GOING ARCHIVAL OVERHAUL.
