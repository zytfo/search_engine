LINGUISTIC SUPPORT IN BUILDING UP MULTI-LINGUAL THESAURI. CHOICE OF
EQUIVALENTS.
     
THE MOST DIFFICULT TASK WHEN COMPILING MULTI-LINGUAL THESAURI IS TO CHOOSE THE
EQUIVALENTS FOR WORDS WHICH ARE PARTIAL SYNONYMS. WITH THIS IN MIND, CONSIDERS
THE MAIN TYPES OF LEXICAL SYSTEMS AND PARADIGMATIC RELATIONS AND GIVES AN
EXAMPLE OF THE SEMANTIC DESCRIPTION OF TERMINAL LEXICAL SYSTEMS IN RUSSIAN AND
ENGLISH.
