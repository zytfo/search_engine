THE VETERANS ADVISOR.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS OF SELECTED
PROFESSIONALS. REVIEW OF THE INFORMATION NEEDS AND USES OF A VETERANS ADVISOR,
BILL WHITE, WHO COUNSELS VETERANS FOR AN EVENING SCHOOL WITHIN THE CONTINUING
EDUCATION DIVISION. INCLUDED IN THE DISCUSSION ARE WHITE'S GENERAL INFORMATION
USE AT HOME, AND ON THE JOB; SPECIFIC INFORMATION NEEDS, USES, AND MEANS OF
ACQUISITION.
