SELECTIVE DISSEMINATION OF INFORMATION SERVICES' TECHNIQUES FOR ELABORATION
AND REFINEMENT OF USER INTEREST PROFILES.
     
DETAILS PROCEDURES FOR IMPROVING RECALL AND PRECISION PERFORMANCE IN USER
INTEREST PROFILES OF AUTOMATED SDI SERVICES, IN 5 STAGES' PRELIMINARY
INFORMATION FOR USERS, OUTLINING THE OBJECTIVES OF THE SYSTEM, HOW IT WORKS,
ITS COVERAGE, AND WHAT IS MEANT BY EXHAUSTIVITY AND RELEVANCE; BASIC PROFILE
DATA, IDENTIFYING THE USER AND HIS INTEREST AREA; ANALYSIS OF ERRORS IN
REFERENCES RECALLED; PROCEDURES FOR REFINEMENT, BY EXCLUDING NOISE, INCREASING
PRECISION OF TERMS, AND IMPROVING SEARCH STRATEGY' EXAMPLES ARE GIVEN IN EACH
CASE.
