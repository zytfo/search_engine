OFF-AIR COPYING UPDATE' GUIDELINES, ADVICE TO EDUCATORS.
     
REPRODUCES THE GUIDELINES FOR OFF-AIR RECORDING OF BROADCAST PROGRAMMING FOR
EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES DRAWN UP BY A NEGOTIATING COMMITTEE OF 19 US EDUCATIONAL
USERS AND COPYRIGHT PROPRIETORS. OUTLINES THE BACKGROUND TO THE DRAWING UP OF
THESE GUIDELINES, AND DISCUSSES SOME OF THEIR IMPLICATIONS.
