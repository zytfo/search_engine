HISTORY OF THE MALAWI LIBRARY ASSOCIATION.
     
A BRIEF OUTLINE OF THE HISTORY OF THE MALAWI LIBRARY ASSOCIATION, DESCRIBING
THE 1ST MEETINGS IN 1975, THE ELECTION OF A STEERING COMMITTEE AND THE
INAUGURATION AT CHANCELLOR COLLEGE ON 30 APR 76 WITH RODERICK MABOMBA AS
CHAIRMAN AND DR. H. KANUZU BANDA AS PATRON. TOUCHES ON MORE RECENT
DEVELOPMENTS.
