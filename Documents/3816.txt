SIXTY YEARS OF 'PHOTOGRAPHIC ABSTRACTS'.
     
THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE JOURNAL IN 1921 TO GIVE A MEANS OF SEARCHING THE
LITERATURE OF PHOTOGRAPHIC SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY IS DISCUSSED, AND ITS
PERFORMANCE OVER THE LAST 60 YEARS SURVEYED. THE NATURE OF THE PROBLEMS MET IS
REVIEWED, AND THE INFLUENCE OF LIMITED FUNDS UPON ITS PROGRESS EMPHASISED.
