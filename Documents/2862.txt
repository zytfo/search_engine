AUTOMATION AND THE SERVICE ATTITUDES OF ARL CIRCULATION MANAGERS.
     
THE SURVEY AIMED TO IDENTIFY THE DEGREE AND STATE OF AUTOMATION OF THE
CIRCULATION FUNCTION OF 98 ACADEMIC LIBRARIES (MEMBERS OF THE ASSOCIATION OF
RESEARCH LIBRARIES), CLASSIFIED AS MANUAL, BATCH AND ON-LINE SYSTEMS. THE
OPINIONS OF CIRCULATION MANAGERS REGARDING THEIR ROLES AND ATTITUDES TO THE
SYSTEMS WERE SOUGHT AND THE RESULTS TABULATED.
