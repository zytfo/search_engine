INFORMATION SYSTEMS FOR NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT-THE SOCIAL RELEVANCE OF
INFORMATION SYSTEMS.
     
EXTRACT FROM THE 6TH GABRIEL A. BERNADO MEMORIAL LECTURE, UNIVERSITY OF THE
PHILIPPINES, MAR 79. CONSIDERS THE ROLE PLAYED BY INFORMATION SYSTEMS OF ALL
TYPES IN REACHING NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT GOALS, PARTICULARLY IN DEVELOPING
COUNTRIES. DEVOTES SPECIAL ATTENTION TO PROBLEMS IN BUILDING THE INFORMATION
INFRASTRUCTURE AND INFORMATION POLICY-MAKING IN A CONSTANTLY CHANGING SOCIETY,
TAKING INTO ACCOUNT ECONOMIC, SOCIAL AND POLITICAL FACTORS. THE USE OF
INFORMATION IN RURAL DEVELOPMENT IS TREATED IN DETAIL AS AN EXAMPLE.
