OCCULTISM AND PARAPSYCHOLOGY' AN ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF SELECTED SERIALS.
     
DEFINES THE DIFFERENCES BETWEEN OCCULTISM AND PARAPSYCHOLOGY AND LISTS 24
SERIALS ABOUT THESE 2 SUBJECTS WHICH APPEAL TO THE GENERAL PUBLIC AND WHICH
ARE OF IMPORTANCE TO LIBRARIES. EACH ENTRY CONTAINS DETAILS OF FULL TITLE,
PLACE, PUBLISHER, EDITOR, BEGINNING DATE, FREQUENCY, AND CONTENTS.
