AASL'S FIRST NATIONAL CONFERENCE.
     
THE FIRST NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE AMERICAN ASSOCIATION OF SCHOOL LIBRARIANS
TOOK PLACE FROM 25-28 SEPT 80 AT LOUISVILLE, KENTUCKY, TAKING AS ITS THEME
''80 AND BEYOND'. REPORTS ON THE ROUTINE MATTERS OF THE CONFERENCE, INCLUDING
REGISTRATION, TICKET SALES, AND RESPONSE TO PROGRAMMES, AND SUMMARISES FORUMS
WORKSHOPS AND SESSIONS.
