POSITION AND DEVELOPMENT OF SPECIAL LIBRARIES WITHIN THE LIBRARIANSHIP OF THE
GDR.

A BRIEF EXPLANATION OF THE TERM SPECIAL LIBRARY AND ITS CONTENTS AND PROBLEMS
WHICH THE SPECIAL LIBRARIES ARE FACING TODAY. MEASURES ARE INDICATED TO
OVERCOME THESE DIFFICULTIES' RESPONSIBILITY OF STATE MANAGEMENT ORGANS FOR
LIBRARIES OF THEIR SPECIAL FIELD AND THEIR CONCENTRATION INTO NETWORKS, THE
ESTABLISHMENT OF SPECIAL LIBRARY NETWORKS AND CENTRALISED SPECIAL LIBRARIES
WITH METHODOLOGICAL AND GUIDING FUNCTIONS; THE COOPERATION IN THE REGIONS AND
THE ORGANS OF THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF EAST GERMANY TO DEVELOP JOINT
ACTIVITIES AND CONTINUED PROFESSIONAL TRAINING.
