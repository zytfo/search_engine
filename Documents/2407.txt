PROBLEMS IN IMPROVING CLASS IN CURRENT AWARENESS BULLETINS WITHIN VINITI'S
INTEGRATED INFORMATION SYSTEM.
     
DESCRIBES THE SUBJECT HEADINGS IN VINITI'S CURRENT AWARENESS INFORMATION
BULLETIN AND ANALYSES THESE FOR AUTOMATICS AND RADIOELECTRONICS, BIOLOGY,
METALLURGY, PHYSICS, AND CHEMISTRY. DISCUSSES THEIR DIFFERENCES AND
SIMILARITIES AND COMPARES THE CLASSIFICATION IN THE BULLETIN AND MAKES
PROPOSALS FOR IMPROVEMENTS.
