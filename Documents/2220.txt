CISS AND GOVERNMENT INFORMATION IN NEW SOUTH WALES.
     
FORMED IN 1976, CISS (COMMUNITY INFORMATION SHARING SERVICE) IS A COOPERATIVE
GROUP OF ALL TYPES OF COMMUNITY INFORMATION WORKERS. MUCH OF ITS WORK HAS BEEN
DIRECTED TOWARDS THE IMPROVEMENT OF THE PROVISION OF AND ACCESS TO GOVERNMENT
INFORMATION. STATES THE UNDERLYING PRINCIPLES INVOLVED AND OUTLINES ACTIVITIES
TO DATE IN THIS AREA IN NEW SOUTH WALES.
