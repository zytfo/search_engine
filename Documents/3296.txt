THE ROLE AND PRACTICE OF REFERENCE STATISTICS (IN CHINESE).
     
REFERENCE STATISTICS, AN INTEGRAL PART OF LIBRARY STATISTICS, IS A SIGNIFICANT
ELEMENT IN PERFORMANCE EVALUATION, BUDGET ALLOCATION, AND SHORT TERM AND
LONG-RANGE PLANNING IN A LIBRARY. HIGHLIGHTS SOME IMPORTANT ASPECTS OF
COLLECTING, ANALYSING AND APPLYING REFERENCE STATISTICAL INFORMATION, FROM A
PRACTICAL POINT OF VIEW.
