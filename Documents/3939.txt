AN OVERVIEW OF COMPUTER GRAPHICS TECHNOLOGY AND APPLICATIONS.
     
THE INTEGRATION OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES' DATA PROCESSING, COMMUNICATIONS,
DISPLAY, AND ERGONOMICS; ARE EXAMINED IN LIGHT OF RECENT DEVELOPMENTS IN
COMPUTER GRAPHICS. A REVIEW OF TECHNICAL IMPROVEMENTS OF THE PAST 2 YEARS IS
GIVEN, FOLLOWED BY A DISCUSSION OF THE APPLICATIONS OF COMPUTER GRAPHICS AND
FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS.
