THE BIRTH OF INDEX MEDICUS.
     
THE BEGINNING YEARS OF 2INDEX MEDICUS1 (IM) AND ITS COMMONLY KNOWN ORIGINATOR,
J.S. BILLINGS, ARE DESCRIBED ALONG WITH IM'S COMPANION PUBLICATION 2INDEX
CATALOG OF THE LIBRARY OF THE SURGEON GENERAL'S OFFICE1 (IC). CONTENT
COMPARISON OF IM AND IC REVEALS THAT R. FLETCHER, WHO JOINED BILLINGS IN 1876,
WAS MOSTLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE PUBLICATION OF 2INDEX MEDICUS1, WHILE BILLINGS
WAS FOR 2INDEX CATALOG1.
