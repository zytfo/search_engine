LIBRARY ACTIVITIES CELEBRATING NATIONAL HERITAGE YEAR IN FRANCE.
     
SURVEY OF THE NUMEROUS DIVERSE ACTIVITIES ORGANISED BY FRENCH LIBRARIES TO
MARK THE OCCASION IN 1980. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF EXHIBITIONS, CULTURAL
ACTIVITIES, PUBLICATIONS, INVENTORIES AND RESTORATION PROJECTS, AND NOTABLE
ACQUISITIONS, WITH PARTICULAR EMPHASIS ON THE PROVINCIAL LIBRARIES WHICH THE
PRESS TENDS TO IGNORE IN FAVOUR OF PARIS EVENTS.
