LIBRARIANSHIP RECRUITS: WHERE DO THEY GO?.
     
TABULATED AND ANNOTATED INFORMATION RELATING TO THE EMPLOYMENT SITUATION FOR
AUSTRALIAN LIBRARY GRADUATES AS AT 30 APR 80. COMPARED WITH PREVIOUS RECENT
SURVEYS THERE IS LITTLE CHANGE IN THE NUMBER OF GRADUATES OF UNIVERSITIES AND
COLLEGES OBTAINING PERMANENT FULL-TIME EMPLOYMENT. A DETAILED ANALYSIS OF DATA
IS MADE.
