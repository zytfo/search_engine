INDEXING A LOCAL PLANNING INQUIRY' THE EXPERIENCE OF BELVOIR.

OUTLINES THE BACKGROUND TO THE VALE OF BELVOIR PLANNING INQUIRY AND DESCRIBES
THE WORK INVOLVED IN INDEXING THIS INQUIRY. TOPICS DISCUSSED INCLUDE' THE
COMPILATION OF THE LIST OF INDEX TERMS; THE INDEXING OF TRANSCRIPTS; INQUIRY
DOCUMENT NUMBERING; LISTING THE INDEXES TO INQUIRY DOCUMENTS; AND INDEXING THE
PROOFS OF EVIDENCE.
