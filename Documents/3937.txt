USER RESPONSE TO TELIDON.
     
STUDIES OF USER RESPONSE TO VIDEOTEX MAY BE AIMED AT 2 BROAD QUESTIONS: 'HOW
WILL THE USER RESPOND TO VARIOUS DESIGN FEATURES OF VIDEOTEX?' AND 'WHAT WILL
BE THE BEHAVIOURAL AND SOCIAL CONSEQUENCES OF VIDEOTEX?' STUDIES OF THE
RESPONSE TO DESIGN OF VARIOUS ASPECTS OF TELIDON HAVE BEEN CONDUCTED IN THE
LABORATORIES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNICATIONS IN OTTAWA. REVIEWS
EXPERIMENTS REGARDING USER RESPONSE TO TREE INDEXES ON TELIDON, TO READING
FROM A TELIDON SCREEN, AND TO GRAPHICS ON TELIDON.
