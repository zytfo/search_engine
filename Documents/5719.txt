MICRO-INFORMATICS AND LIBRARY SCIENCE.

A BRIEF SURVEY DESCRIBING HOW LIBRARIES CAN CAPITALISE ON THE OPPORTUNITIES
OFFERED BY THE AVAILABILITY AT LOW COST OF MICROCOMPUTERS. DETAILS ARE GIVEN
OF HOW MICROCOMPUTERS WORK AND THEIR POTENTIAL APPLICATIONS IN LIBRARY
SITUATIONS; PROGRAMS FOR MANAGING LOANS, ACQUISITIONS, AND PERIODICAL
COLLECTIONS ARE DISCUSSED; AND THE ADVANTAGES OF ACCESSING ON-LINE DATA BASES
CONSIDERED.
