LENIN'S PROGRAMME IN ACTION (ALL-UNION SCIENTIFIC AND PRACTICAL CONFERENCE).
     
PRESENTS SUMMARIES OF PAPERS PRESENTED AT THE CONFERENCE ON THE APPLICATION OF
LENIN'S IDEAS IN LIBRARY PRACTICE OF THE SOCIALIST SOCIETY ORGANISED TO
COMMEMORATE THE 110TH ANNIVERSARY OF LENIN'S BIRTH IN MAY 80. SOME
CONTRIBUTIONS WERE DEVOTED TO VARIOUS ASPECTS OF BIBLIOGRAPHICAL WORK SUCH AS
THE ROLE OF BIBLIOGRAPHY IN THE PROMOTION OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL
LITERATURE, AND BIBLIOGRAPHICAL PROCESSING IN THE ACADEMY OF SCIENCES LIBRARY.
OTHER PAPERS CONCENTRATED ON ACHIEVEMENTS IN BIBLIOGRAPHICAL WORK OF THE STATE
LIBRARIES OF INDIVIDUAL REPUBLICS, THEIR SERVICES TO ALL TYPES OF READERSHIP,
PUBLISHING ACTIVITIES, INTERLIBRARY LOANS, CENTRALISATION OF PUBLIC LIBRARY
NETWORKS AND METHODOLOGICAL GUIDANCE TO LIBRARIES WITHIN THE NETWORK.
