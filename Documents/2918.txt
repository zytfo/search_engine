THE HOME OFFICE' VIDEOTEX AND PERSONAL COMPUTERS FOR OFFICE AUTOMATION.
     
THE USA AND EUROPE HAVE SHIFTED FROM INDUSTRIAL TO INFORMATION ECONOMIES. THIS
HAS HAD CONSEQUENCES FOR OFFICE ACTIVITIES, THE PROLIFERATION OF JOURNALS AND
WORK PATTERNS. EXPLORES THE CONCEPT OF THE REMOTE OFFICE IN THE CONTEXT OF THE
ADVENT OF PERSONAL COMPUTING AND MASS CONSUMER COMPUTING (ESPECIALLY
VIDEOTEX). DETAILED ATTENTION IS GIVEN TO VIDEOTEX APPLICATIONS AND THE USE OF
PERSONAL COMPUTERS AS MULTIFUNCTION WORKSTATIONS.
