INTRODUCING APPLE II.
     
THE TEXT OF A BOOKLET WHICH PROVIDES ELEMENTARY INSTRUCTION IN USING A
COMPUTER, DESIGNED FOR A QUEBEC CHILDREN'S LIBRARY PROJECT TO TEACH COMPUTER
LITERACY. A BRIEF GENERAL INTRODUCTION DESCRIBES WHAT A COMPUTER CAN DO, AND
HOW TO PROGRAMME IT. SPECIFIC INSTRUCTIONS WITH EXAMPLES FOLLOW, DEMONSTRATING
PROCEDURES FOR BASIC OPERATIONS AND THE RANGE OF THE COMPUTER'S FACILITIES.
