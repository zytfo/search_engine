OCLC-MARC TAPES AND COLLECTION MANAGEMENT.
     
OCLC-MARC SUBSCRIPTION SERVICE TAPES CAN BE USED AS A CONVENIENT SOURCE OF
STATISTICAL DATA FOR COLLECTION MANAGEMENT. NEW ACQUISITIONS CAN BE ANALYSED
BY USING MONTHLY AND ANNUAL CUMULATIVE TAPES. INFORMATION OBTAINABLE INCLUDES
GENERAL STATISTICS SUCH AS NUMBERS OF NEW TITLES ADDED BY SUBJECTS, TYPES OF
MATERIALS, LANGUAGES OF THE TEXT, AND INTELLECTUAL LEVELS. USING BOOLEAN
OPERATORS, IT IS POSSIBLE TO GENERATE STATISTICS SUCH AS THE NUMBER OF
CHEMISTRY HANDBOOKS PUBLISHED AFTER 1970, ART HISTORY BOOKS WRITTEN IN THE
SPANISH LANGUAGE FILMSTRIPS TREATING AMERICAN HISTORY SUITED FOR CHILDREN,
ETC.
