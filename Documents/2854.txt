CARING FOR BOOKS AND DOCUMENTS.
     
ADVICE AND GUIDANCE ON THE CARE OF BOOKS, WITH COVERAGE OF THE MATERIALS OF
WHICH BOOKS AND DOCUMENTS ARE MADE, THEORETICAL ASPECTS OF CARE, THE ENEMIES
OF BOOKS AND DOCUMENTS, AND HOW TO CARE FOR BOOKS; A BIBLIOGRAPHY IS APPENDED,
WITH DETAILS ONLY OF GENERAL WORKS-IT IS NOT EXHAUSTIVE, BUT LISTS WORKS WHICH
AID IN A GENERAL UNDERSTANDING OF BOOK CARE.
