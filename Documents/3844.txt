A BIND-AID FOR HURT BOOKS IS INVALUABLE.
     
HIGHLIGHTS THE IMPORTANCE, TO LIBRARIANS AND BINDERS ALIKE, OF CHOOSING THE
APPROPRIATE BINDING, AND GIVES EXAMPLES OF VARIOUS METHODS USED BY SOME US
LIBRARIANS TO ENSURE THAT BOOKS ARE APPROPRIATELY BOUND. PROVIDES A CHECKLIST
WHICH CAN BE USED TO ASSESS WHETHER OR NOT BOOKS NEED TO BE SENT FOR BINDING.
