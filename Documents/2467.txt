PUBLISHING AND BOOKSELLING IN KENYA' PROBLEMS AND PROSPECTS.
     
(MLS DISSERTATION). REVIEWS THE DEVELOPMENT AND CURRENT STATE OF PUBLISHING
AND BOOKSELLING IN KENYA AND HIGHLIGHTS THE NECESSITY FOR A VIABLE INDIGENOUS
PUBLISHING INDUSTRY. DISCUSSES FURTHER OPPORTUNITIES FOR THE PUBLISHING
INDUSTRY AND DISCUSSES MAJOR FACTORS LIKELY TO WIDEN THE BOOK MARKET.
DISCUSSES THE ROLE OF BOOKSHOPS AND THEIR DISTRIBUTION THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY;
ACQUISITION OF STOCK, TRADE DISCOUNTS, CREDIT PERIODS, CURRENCY CONVERSION
RATES; AND BOOKSELLING PROBLEMS AND PROSPECTS. ON THE BASIS OF CONCLUSIONS
THUS DRAWN, MAKES 21 PROPOSALS FOR CHANGE.
