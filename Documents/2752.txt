CITIZEN GROUPS AND THE TRANSFER OF PUBLIC POLICY INFORMATION IN A COMMUNITY.
     
(PHD DISSERTATION-UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN.) EXAMINATION OF THE INFORMATION
SEEKING AND DISSEMINATING ACTIVITIES OF A VARIETY OF US CITIZEN GROUPS,
ATTEMPTING TO DEVELOP RESEARCH KNOWLEDGE ABOUT THE INFORMATION NEEDS OF
CITIZEN GROUPS, THEIR CHARACTERISTICS, AND THEIR PRESENT INFORMATION SEEKING
BEHAVIOUR.
