LIBRARY EDUCATION, TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION IN HAWAII.
     
DESCRIBES THE GRADUATE SCHOOL OF LIBRARY STUDIES, UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII AND
EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR POSITIONS AT THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII AND IN
STATE AND PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN HAWAII. ALSO BRIEFLY LOOKS AT THE SCHOOL LIBRARY
SPECIALIST CERTIFICATE, A 2-YEAR LIBRARY TECHNICIAN COURSE, EXPERIENCE
REQUIREMENTS FOR LIBRARY ASSISTANTS AND CONTINUING EDUCATION.
