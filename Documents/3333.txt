COST RECOVERY' THE CASE FOR.
     
THERE IS WIDESPREAD RECOGNITION THAT THE COSTS OF INTERLENDING ARE UNEQUALLY
DISTRIBUTED, AND SOME NORTH AMERICAN LIBRARIES ARE ALREADY ATTEMPTING FULL OR
PARTIAL COST RECOVERY. PUTS THE CASE FOR RECOUPING OF INTERLOAN COSTS. LOOKS
AT POTENTIAL REVENUE SOURCES, COST RECOVERY, COST ACCOUNTING AND THE IMPACT OF
TECHNOLOGY.
