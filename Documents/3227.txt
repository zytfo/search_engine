ARKANSAS PUBLIC POLICY INFORMATION SYSTEM' ARKANSAS ECONOMETRIC MODELS.
     
REPORTS ON THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE ARKANSAS ECONOMETRIC MODELS AS PART OF AN
OVERALL DESCRIPTION OF THE ARKANSAS PUBLIC POLICY INFORMATION SYSTEM (APPIS).
APPIS IS DESIGNED TO BE A PROTOTYPE FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF PUBLIC POLICY
INFORMATION SYSTEMS BY OTHER STATES. APPIS IS A SET OF ECONOMETRIC MODELS AND
DATA BASES WHICH SPECIFY PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTORS IN THE ARKANSAS ECONOMY.
THE SYSTEM IS DESIGNED TO PROVIDE QUARTERLY FORECASTS OF STATE EMPLOYMENT BY
2-DIGIT STANDARD INDUSTRIAL CLASSIFICATION, SOURCES OF PERSONAL INCOME AND
DETAILED SOURCES OF STATE REVENUES.
