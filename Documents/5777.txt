THE INFORMATION FUTURE AND ITS CHALLENGES TO INFORMATION MANAGERS.

STRESSES THE IMPORTANCE OF INFORMATION AS AN ECONOMIC PRODUCT AND EMPHASISES
ITS VITAL ROLE IN SOCIETY. SINCE THE VALUE OF INFORMATION TO SOCIETY IS
INCREASING, ITS MANAGEMENT SHOULD BE STRENGTHENED-THIS IS A CHALLENGE TO
INFORMATION MANAGERS, PARTICULARLY IN BALANCING REDUCTION OF RESOURCE
INVESTMENTS AND INCREASES IN THE VALUE OF INFORMATION ASSETS. AN ANALYSIS OF
THE INFORMATION CYCLE IN THIS RESPECT IS GIVEN AND A PLEA IS MADE FOR
INFORMATION MANAGERS TO CONTRIBUTE SIGNIFICANTLY TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE
FUTURE OFFICE.
