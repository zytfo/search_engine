SEX MAGAZINES IN THE LIBRARY COLLECTION.
     
COMPRISES 2 INTRODUCTORY ARTICLES AND 10 ARTICLES REPRINTED FROM VARIOUS
SOURCES, COVERING TREATMENT OF SEX MAGAZINES IN US LIBRARIES, THE LEGAL
POSITION, CHILDREN'S RIGHTS IN THE LIBRARY, RELEVANT SERIALS, TREATMENT OF
SEXUAL TOPICS IN VARIOUS SERIALS, A SELECT ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF GAY AND
LESBIAN PERIODICALS, AND A PROPOSAL FOR AN INDEX TO SEX-RELATED PERIODICALS.
