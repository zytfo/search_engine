ANALYSIS OF KEYWORDS IN CHEMISTRY.
     
FROM THE 10 MILLION KEYWORDS IN 3 VOLUMES OF 2CA CONDENSATES 1FOR 1977 AND
1978, 16,000 KEYWORDS WERE ASSEMBLED FROM THE 200 MOST FREQUENTLY OCCURRING
ONES IN EACH OF THE 80 SECTIONS AND ANALYSED FOR CROSS-CORRELATION,
CHARACTERISTIC FEATURES, AND RELATIONSHIP TO 10,000 TERMS IN A SEPARATE
JAPANESE CHEMICAL SOCIETY LIST.
