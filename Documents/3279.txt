MICROPROCESSORS AND WORD PROCESSORS-THEIR USE IN INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS.
     
A BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF MICROPROCESSORS AND WORD PROCESSORS IS PROVIDED WITH
THE OBJECT OF BRINGING THE CAPABILITIES OF THIS EQUIPMENT, TO THE NOTICE OF
INFORMATION SCIENTISTS IN INDIA, SO THAT THEIR UTILITY MAY BE STUDIED IN THE
CONTEXT OF DESIGNING COMPUTER-BASED INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN INDIA.
