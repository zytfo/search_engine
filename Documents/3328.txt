WHERE AND HOW TO GET IT? THE PROPOSED ILL EVALUATIVE STUDY.
     
DESCRIBES THE OBJECTIVES AND METHODOLOGY OF THE PROPOSED EVALUATIVE SURVEY OF
INTERLENDING IN AUSTRALIA. OUTLINES THE NEED FOR SUCH A SURVEY WITH AN
EXPLANATION OF THE KIND OF DATA TO BE COLLECTED AND THE PRINCIPLE SURVEY
INSTRUMENTS TO BE USED. THE SURVEY IS INTENDED TO DEMONSTRATE THE TYPE OF
INTERLENDING ORGANISATIONAL PATTERN BEST SUITED TO THE AUSTRALIAN
BIBLIOGRAPHIC ENVIRONMENT.
