LIBRARIANS FROM ALL OVER THE WORLD TO MEET IN LEIPZIG FOR THE 47TH CONFERENCE
OF THE IFLA'S GENERAL ASSEMBLY.
     
BETWEEN 17-22 AUG 81 THE 47TH CONFERENCE OF THE IFLA'S GENERAL ASSEMBLY WILL
TAKE PLACE IN LEIPZIG. THE MAIN THEME OF THE CONFERENCE WHICH WAS SUGGESTED BY
EAST GERMANY WILL BE 'LIBRARY NATIONAL INSTITUTIONS AND PROFESSIONAL
ORGANISATIONS'. THIS THEME IS CONSIDERED TO BE OF GREAT IMPORTANCE ON AN
INTERNATIONAL LEVEL AS IT PROVIDES AN OPPORTUNITY TO DEAL WITH VARIOUS ASPECTS
OF NATIONAL LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT AND INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION WITH SPECIAL
EMPHASIS ON THE EFFICIENCY OF LIBRARY ORGANISATIONS, PROFESSIONAL METHODICAL
CENTRES AND NATIONAL SERVICE FACILITIES FOR LIBRARIES. ABOUT 1,200 PEOPLE ARE
EXPECTED TO ATTEND THE CONFERENCE WHICH WIL BE THE LARGEST CULTURAL EVENT YET
IN EAST GERMANY. MANY LIBRARIES AND MUSEUMS IN LEIPZIG WILL BE ORGANISING
SPECIAL EXHIBITIONS AT THIS TIME AND A SEMINAR FOR LIBRARIANS FROM DEVELOPING
COUNTRIES WILL BE HELD IN THE WEEK PRECEDING THE CONFERE NCE.
