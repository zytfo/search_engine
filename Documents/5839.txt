LOCAL MAP SOURCES: BARGAINS FROM VARIOUS AGENCIES.

REVIEWS SOME OF THE CARTOGRAPHIC BARGAINS AVAILABLE FROM VARIOUS US AGENCIES
OTHER THAN THE COMMONLY USED NATIONAL GEOGRAPHIC SOCIETY, AND CONTENDING THAT
IN WEST VIRGINIA A CORE COLLECTION OF LOCAL MAPS MAY BE ACQUIRED FOR $50.00 OR
LESS. AGENCIES INCLUDE THE DEPARTMENT OF HIGHWAYS, WEST VIRGINIA GEOLOGICAL
AND ECONOMIC SURVEY, AND THE DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES.
