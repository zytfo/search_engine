MANUAL OF PROCEDURES FOR EVALUATION VISITS UNDER STANDARDS OF ACCREDITATION.
     
THIS FULLY REVISED MANUAL OF PROCEDURES FOR EVALUATION VISITS PRESENTS
GUIDELINES FOR SITE VISITS TO LIBRARY SCHOOLS SEEKING ACCREDITATION FOR THEIR
PROGRAMMES OF STUDY. VISITS TO SUCH SCHOOLS PROVIDE THE COMMITTEE ON
ACCREDITATION WITH DATA TO ASSIST IN REACHING A JUDGMENT WHETHER TO GRANT
ACCREDITED STATUS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
