THE LONG RANGE PLAN FOR STATEWIDE LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT IN CONNECTICUT,
1980-1985.
     
PREPARED TO SATISFY REQUIREMENTS OF THE LIBRARY SERVICES AND CONSTRUCTION ACT
(LCSA) PL 95-123, THIS LONG-RANGE PLAN PRESENTS LIBRARY SERVICE GOALS AND
OBJECTIVES FOR THE CONNECTICUT LIBRARY SYSTEM FOR THE NEXT 5 YEARS. THE
BACKGROUND OF LONG RANGE PLANNING, THE PLANNING PROCESS, AND PROBLEMS TO BE
OVERCOME ARE DEVELOPED WITHIN THE FRAMEWORK OF FEDERAL GUIDELINES FOR
PROGRAMMES AND EXPENSES. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
