POINTS TO THE CONSIDERED IN RELATION TO THE PHYSICAL DESIGN OF A LIBRARY.

A BRIEF SURVEY COVERING POINTS THAT MIGHT BE OVERLOOKED IN DESIGNING A
UNIVERSITY LIBRARY. BASIC REQUIREMENTS TO BE CONSIDERED INCLUDE THE NEED FOR
FLEXIBILITY OF INTERIOR SPACE TO ALLOW FOR INCREASE OF READERS AND SERVICES,
AND THE CREATION OF AN AGREEABLE ATMOSPHERE. A PRELIMINARY ANALYSIS, BY THE
LIBRARIAN AND ARCHITECT IN COLLABORATION, OF ACTIVITIES TO BE ACCOMMODATED
PERMITS CLASSIFICATION OF THE SPACE AVAILABLE IN RELATION TO REQUIREMENTS, FOR
EXAMPLE, FOR BOOKS, READERS, ADMINISTRATION, AND ARCHIVES. DETAILS ARE GIVEN
OF PROCEDURES FOR PLANNING INCORPORATION OF ESSENTIALS SUCH AS SHELVING SPACE,
READER ACCOMMODATION, ILLUMINATION, SOUND INSULATION, COMMUNICATION CHANNELS,
CLEANING SERVICES, AND PROVISION OF EQUIPMENT.
