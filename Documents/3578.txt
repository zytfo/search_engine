PROCEEDINGS OF THE PUBLIC LIBRARIES AUTHORITIES CONFERENCE 1980.
     
THE FOLLOWING PAPERS WERE PRESENTED AT THE PUBLIC LIBRARY AUTHORITIES
CONFERENCE, SOUTHPORT, 6-9 OCT 80: 'THE ROLE OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THE
NATIONAL NETWORK', BY ROYSTON BROWN; 'THE CONSEQUENCES FOR LOCAL AUTHORITIES
OF RECENT LEGISLATION', BY N.P. HEPWORTH; 'THE POLITICAL VIEW', BY D.E. DELL;
'TAKEN FOR GRANTED', BY ALEX HOWSON; 'DIRECTORATES-RIGHT OR WRONG?', BY ALAN
LONGWORTH; 'LIBRARY USER CONSULTATIONS', BY W.J. MURISON; 'THE FUTURE
CHALLENGE FOR PUBLIC LIBRARIES', BY ROY SMITH.
