PROBLEMS OF UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES IN KENYA WITH PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO
STAFFING, ACQUISITIONS AND USE.
     
(MLS DISSERTATION). SURVEYS 3 ACUTE PROBLEMS FACING KENYAN UNIVERSITY
LIBRARIES (STAFFING, ACQUISITIONS, AND USE) AND SUGGESTS SOLUTIONS THAT WOULD
ENABLE THE LIBRARIES TO FULFIL THEIR ROLE EFFECTIVELY IN FURTHERING THEIR
PARENT INSTITUTIONS' GOALS. ACKNOWLEDGES THE EXISTENCE OF OTHER PROBLEMS WHICH
COULD NOT BE COVERED HERE, ESPECIALLY LIBRARY ACCOMMODATION IN GENERAL AND ITS
EFFECTS ON OTHER ASPECTS OF THE LIBRARY, INCLUDING STAFF MORALE.
