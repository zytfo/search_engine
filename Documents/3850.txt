PATTERN OF PUBLIC LIBRARY USE AND FINANCIAL SUPPORT IN PAPUA NEW GUINEA.
     
EXPLAINS THE DECLINING FINANCIAL SUPPORT FOR PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN PAPUA NEW
GUINEA SINCE 1977 AND INVESTIGATES ITS EFFECT ON LIBRARY USE BY LOOKING AT
BOOK LOANS IN 7 LIBRARIES DURING 1977-81. DISCUSSES POSSIBLE OTHER INFLUENCES
ON LOANS IN AN ATTEMPT TO EXPLAIN THE CONFLICTING TRENDS EXPOSED BY THE
INVESTIGATION.
