HUMAN CHOICES IN THE OFFICE OF THE FUTURE.
     
A KEY FACTOR IN THE WIDESPREAD ACCEPTANCE AND USE OF COMPLEX OFFICE
INFORMATION SYSTEMS WILL BE THE HUMAN FACTORS OF THE SYSTEM. MENTIONS SOME OF
THE LIKELY PROBLEM AREAS AND BRIEFLY DISCUSSES THE RELEVANT HUMAN FACTORS
TECHNIQUES FOR SOLVING THESE PROBLEMS. THE USER IS THE MOST IMPORTANT
COMPONENT AND HIS REACTIONS TO THE SYSTEM WILL DECIDE WHETHER ITS INTRODUCTION
IS A SUCCESS OR FAILURE.
