THE FRENCH COMMISSION FOR COORDINATING ADMINISTRATIVE DOCUMENTATION AND THE
DIRECTORY OF FRENCH OFFICIAL PUBLICATIONS.
     
AN ACCOUNT OF THE RESEARCH ACTIVITIES OF THE PUBLICATIONS COMMITTEE OF THE
FRENCH COMMISSION FOR COORDINATING ADMINISTRATIVE DOCUMENTATION SINCE ITS
INCEPTION IN 1971, LEADING TO THE RECENT PUBLICATION OF THE 1ST COMPREHENSIVE
DIRECTORY OF FRENCH OFFICIAL PUBLICATIONS IN 3 VOLUMES. THE PROBLEMS OF
ESTABLISHING BIBLIOGRAPHICAL CONTROL OVER FRENCH ADMINISTRATIVE PUBLICATIONS
ARE DETAILED, AND THE COMMITTEE'S PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING AND IMPLEMENTING
A SUCCESSFUL RESEARCH PROGRAMME DESCRIBED IN FULL. THE CONTENTS OF THE
DIRECTORY, AND PROPOSED ARRANGEMENTS FOR REGULAR UPDATING, ARE OUTLINED.
