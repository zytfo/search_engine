THE ABC OF COPYRIGHT.
     
A PRIMER GIVING AN OVERALL VIEW OF THE ESSENTIALS OF COPYRIGHT, ATTEMPTING TO
PROVIDE PRACTICAL ANSWERS TO QUESTIONS THAT ARE ASKED DAILY IN THIS AREA.
COVERS' COPYRIGHT PAST AND PRESENT; THE MORAL RIGHT OF AUTHORS; ECONOMIC
RIGHTS OF AUTHORS; PROTECTED WORKS; LIMITS TO COPYRIGHT PROTECTION; COPYRIGHT
OWNERSHIP; TRANSFER OF COPYRIGHT; DURATION OF PROTECTION; COPYRIGHT
FORMALITIES; INFRINGEMENT AND REMEDIES; INTERNATIONAL COPYRIGHT; AND COPYRIGHT
AND THE DEVELOPING WORLD.
