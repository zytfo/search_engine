INTERNATIONAL CHILDREN'S CENTRE.
     
DISCUSSES THE FOUNDATION OF THE INTERNATIONAL CHILDREN'S CENTRE IN PARIS IN
1949 AND ENUMERATES ITS AIMS AND OBJECTIVES. DESCRIBES ITS PRESENT ACTIVITIES
WHICH INCLUDE TRAINING AND INFORMATION, DOCUMENTATION AND PUBLICATIONS, AND
RESEARCH. DISCUSSES THE DIFFERENT BODIES WHICH SUPPORT THE CENTRE'S
ACTIVITIES.
