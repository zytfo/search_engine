EVALUATION AND IMPLEMENTATION OF TOPOLOGICAL CODES FOR ONLINE COMPOUND SEARCH
AND REGISTRATION.
     
A TOPOLOGICAL SEARCH CODE HAS BEEN FOUND TO HAVE HIGH DISCRIMINATORY POWER
WITHIN LARGE SETS OF DISPARATE STRUCTURES. THE TECHNIQUE HAS BEEN IMPLEMENTED
IN A PHARMACEUTICAL COMPANY'S COMPUTERISED CHEMICAL INFORMATION SYSTEM, FOR
INTERACTIVE REGISTRATION AND STRUCTURE SEARCH.
