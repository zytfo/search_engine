FIJI'S SECONDARY SCHOOLS LIBRARIES AND LIBRARIANS' A PROFILE.
     
DESCRIBES SECONDARY SCHOOL LIBRARIES AND LIBRARIANS IN FIJI, CONCENTRATING ON'
TRAINING; STAFFING OF LIBRARIES; LENGTH OF TIME THAT LIBRARIANS HAVE HELD
THEIR POSITIONS; AND THE MAJOR SUBJECT AREAS OF TEACHER-LIBRARIANS. ELABORATES
AND EXPANDS FINDINGS OF A SURVEY BY JOHN RAGNI PUBLISHED IN THE 2FIJI LIBRARY
ASSOCIATION JOURNAL1, DEC 79.
