THE AUSTRALIAN LIBRARY SUPPLY SCENE.
     
ATTEMPTS TO HIGHLIGHT ASPECTS OF THE BOOK INDUSTRY INVOLVED IN THE MARKETING
AND DISTRIBUTION OF BOOKS TO AUSTRALIAN LIBRARIES, INCLUDING THE OPERATIONS OF
PUBLISHERS AND THEIR DISTRIBUTORS, AS WELL AS BOOKSELLERS AND LIBRARY
SUPPLIERS. SUCCESSFUL AUSTRALIAN LIBRARY SUPPLIERS MUST TAKE ADVANTAGE OF ANY
LOCAL BOOK SUPPLY ARRANGEMENTS BENEFICIAL TO THE LOCAL LIBRARY MARKET, AND
BYPASS THOSE WHICH DO NOT MEET LIBRARY REQUIREMENTS.
