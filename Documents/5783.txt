MEDICAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS' APPLICATIONS AND EVALUATION. 1973-APRIL, 1982
(CITATIONS FROM THE ENGINEERING INDEX DATA BASE).

BIBLIOGRAPHY CONTAINS CITATIONS CONCERNING THE APPLICATIONS OF COMPUTER BASED
INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN HOSPITALS, CLINICS, LABORATORIES, AND DOCTORS' OFFICES
FOR DATA RECORDING AND REPORTING OF PATIENT MEDICAL AND ACCOUNTING RECORDS.
MICROCOMPUTER SYSTEMS ARE DISCUSSED.
