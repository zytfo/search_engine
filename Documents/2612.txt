LIBRARY SERVICES IN DISTANCE TEACHING' PRESENT REALITIES AND FUTURE
POSSIBILITIES.
     
DISCUSSES THE PROBLEMS OF PROVIDING LIBRARY SERVICES IN DISTANCE TEACHING,
WITH REFERENCE TO THE FINDINGS OF A SURVEY OF THE LIBRARY PROVISION FOR
EXTERNAL STUDENTS OFFERED BY 36 AUSTRALIAN EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS. SUGGESTS
SOME WAYS OF IMPROVING THESE SERVICES, AND DESCRIBES AN EXPERIMENT IN THE USE
OF MICROFICHE BY EXTERNAL STUDENTS CARRIED OUT BY THE TOWNSVILLE COLLEGE OF
ADVANCED EDUCATION. HIGHLIGHTS THE URGENT NEED FOR MORE RESEARCH ON THE
SUBJECT OF PROVIDING QUALITY SERVICES IN DISTANCE EDUCATION.
