THE ACTIVITIES OF THE IFLA SECTION ON BIBLIOGRAPHY BETWEEN THE 46TH AND 47TH
IFLA CONFERENCES.

IN THE PERIOD BETWEEN THE 46TH AND 47TH IFLA CONFERENCES THE SECTION ON
BIBLIOGRAPHY CONTINUED ITS WORK ON THE PROBLEMS OF IMPROVEMENT OF NATIONAL
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL ACCORDING TO PREVIOUSLY ESTABLISHED PLANS. THE WORK
COMPLETED BY THE WORKING GROUP ON CURRENT NATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHIES IS REVIEWED.
THE TASKS OF A NEW WORKING GROUP ON THE 2BIBLIOGRAPHY OF IFLA CONFERENCE
PAPERS AND OTHER DOCUMENTS 1ARE COVERED. PAPERS GIVEN AT SECTION MEETINGS AT
THE 46TH AND 47TH IFLA CONFERENCES ARE LISTED.
