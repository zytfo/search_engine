SIMULATING PRACTICAL PROBLEMS ENCOUNTERED BY THE HANDICAPPED LIBRARY USER.
     
PARTICIPANTS SIMULATING CONDITIONS OF PHYSICAL HANDICAP ATTEMPTED TO LOCATE
AND USE VARIOUS ITEMS OF LIBRARY EQUIPMENT. DIFFICULTIES OF ACCESS AND
INADEQUATE SIGNPOSTING AND INSTRUCTIONS WERE FOUND AS WELL AS THE LACK OF A
FIRE WARNING PERCEPTIBLE TO THE DEAF AND INADEQUATE LIGHTING FOR THE PARTIALLY
SIGHTED.
