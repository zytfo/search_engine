AUDIOVISUAL AIDS IN THE FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF GERMANY.

IN THE PAST 10 YEARS AUDIO-VISUAL AIDS HAVE BECOME AN ESSENTIAL PART OF THE
LIBRARY SUPPLIES OFFERED IN WEST GERMANY. AT THE SAME TIME THE DIFFERENT AID
MEDIA HAVE DEVELOPED IN DIFFERENT DIRECTIONS. DISCUSSES THE DIFFERENT AIDS
AVAILABLE IN SOUND RECORDING MEDIA, VIDEO AND SLIDES AND FILMS. ALSO MENTIONS
THE SERVICES OF THE EINKAUFSZENTRALE FUR OFFENTLICH BIBLIOTHEKEN REVIEWING AND
READING DEPARTMENTS AND THE DIFFICULTIES OF CATALOGUING SOUND RECORDING MEDIA.
