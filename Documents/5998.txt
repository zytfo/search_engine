WHAT'S ON MICROFORM-A REVIEW OF MICROPUBLICATIONS IN THE U.K. TODAY.

THIS REVIEW DEALS WITH THE SOFTWARE OF THE MICROGRAPHICS INDUSTRY RATHER THAN
THE HARDWARE. EXAMPLES OF TYPICAL APPLICATIONS OF MICROFORMS ARE GIVEN, ALONG
WITH A SUMMARY OF NEW TRENDS IN COLOUR MICROFORMS. IN CONCLUSION, A LIST IS
GIVEN OF THE PROJECTS OF THE LEADING MICRO PUBLISHERS, WITH THEIR UK
ADDRESSES.
