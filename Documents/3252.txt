INFORMATION CENTRE'S ROLE IN TRANSFERRING SPECIAL STEELS TECHNOLOGY.
     
DESCRIBES HOW THE INFORMATION CENTRE OF THE SPECIAL STEELS PLANT HELPS THE
ENGINEERS, TECHNOLOGISTS, PLANNERS AND R&D PERSONNEL OF THE PLANT. WITH THE
VIEW THAT TIMELY INFORMATION CAN CHECK WASTAGE AND IMPROVE PRODUCTION AND
QUALITY, THE CENTRE ARRANGES TO OBTAIN WHEN NECESSARY, THE RELEVANT
INFORMATION IN ADVANCE DIRECTLY FROM THE SOURCES.
