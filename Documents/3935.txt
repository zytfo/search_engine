TELIDON BEHAVIOURAL RESEARCH 2' THE DESIGN OF VIDEOTEX TREE INDEXES.
     
4 REPORTS SAID TO BE A FIRST STEP TOWARD DEVELOPING A SET OF GUIDELINES FOR
CONSTRUCTING TREE-STRUCTURED INDEXES ARE PRESENTED. THEY EXAMINE THE
EFFECTIVENESS OF A TREE-STRUCTURED INDEX WHEN THE EXISTENCE OF INFORMATION IS
UNCERTAIN; THE USE OF TREE-STRUCTURED INDEX WHICH CONTAINS 3 TYPES OF DESIGN
DEFECTS; USER SEARCH PERFORMANCE ON A TELIDON INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEM;
AND THE USE OF DESCRIPTORS AND THE ENHANCEMENT OF SINGLE INDEX PAGES.
CONCLUSIONS ARE SUMMARISED AND TENTATIVE GUIDELINES DRAWN.
