BECOMING LITERATE.

FINAL REPORT OF A RESEARCH PROJECT 'ADULT ILLITERACY AND THE SOCIALIZATION OF
ADULT ILLITERATES'. THE STUDY TRIED TO DISCOVER, THROUGH INTERVIEWING A RANDOM
SAMPLE OF ADULT LITERACY STUDENTS AND THEIR TUTORS IN THE NOTTINGHAM AREA, THE
HISTORY OF THEIR READING/WRITING DIFFICULTIES, THE IMPACT OF THESE
DIFFICULTIES ON THEIR CAREERS AT SCHOOL, DOMESTIC AND SOCIAL LIFE AND ANY
INTERACTIONS BETWEEN ILLITERACY AND ANY OTHER HANDICAP OR DEPRIVATION.
