INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENTS IN SCHOOL LIBRARIANSHIP' THE WORK OF THE I.A.S.L.
     
DESCRIBES THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE INTERNATIONAL ASSOCIATION FOR SCHOOL
LIBRARIANSHIP AND PRESENTS BRIEF ACCOUNTS OF SOME RECENT CONFERENCES. SINCE
ITS LAUNCH IN 1970, THE ASSOCIATION HAS BEEN WORKING FOR IMPROVED STATUS AND
RECOGNITION FOR SCHOOL LIBRARIANS AND HAS BEEN SUPPORTING DEVELOPING SCHOOL
LIBRARY ASSOCIATIONS WORLDWIDE.
