THE VIDEOTEX MARKETPLACE. A THEORY OF EVOLUTION.
     
EXAMINES FACTORS WHICH WILL INFLUENCE THE APPLICATION OF VIDEOTEX (PRESTEL,
ETC.) IN BUSINESS. POTENTIAL MARKET SIZE IS ASSESSED AND 4 POSSIBLE MARKET
SCENARIOS ARE OUTLINED. ARGUES THAT A MASS MARKET FOR VIDEOTEX USE BY THE
GENERAL POPULATION WILL EVOLVE FROM A FEW CAREFULLY CHOSEN BUSINESS
APPLICATIONS (INCLUDING PLACING TERMINALS IN PUBLIC PLACES).
