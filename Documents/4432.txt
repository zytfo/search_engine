THE 2ND SOVIET-AUSTRIAN SEMINAR ON LIBRARIANSHIP.
     
REPORTS BRIEFLY ON THE PROCEEDINGS OF THE 2ND SOVIET-AUSTRIAN LIBRARY SEMINAR
HELD IN VIENNA 21-24 OCT 80. SOVIET PAPERS DEALT WITH' PRINCIPLES OF THE
LIBRARY NETWORK ORGANISATION IN THE USSR, THEORETICAL BASIS OF THE
CONSTRUCTION OF THE SOVIET LIBRARY NETWORK AND THE ESTONIAN LIBRARY NETWORK.
THE SUBJECTS OF AUSTRIAN CONTRIBUTIONS WERE' THE SYSTEM OF AUSTRIAN GOVERNMENT
RESEARCH LIBRARIES AND THE ORGANISATION OF A DATA BASE FOR PERIODICAL HOLDINGS
OF AUSTRIAN LIBRARIES. THE SEMINAR PROVIDED AN OPPORTUNITY FOR SOVIET
LIBRARIANS TO GET ACQUAINTED WITH THE WORK OF AUSTRIAN LIBRARIES. COMMENTS ON
LIBRARY VISITS FORM THE CONTENTS OF THE 2ND PART OF THE REPORT.
