NATIONAL LIBRARY FOR BANGLADESH.
     
TRACES THE ORIGIN OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY IN BANGLADESH AND REVEALS THAT ITS
GENESIS GOES BACK TO 1961 WHEN BANGLADESH WAS KNOWN AS EAST PAKISTAN.
DISCUSSES THE REVISED PROJECT FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY IN
THE 2ND 5 YEAR PLAN (1980-85). CONCLUDES THAT, ACCORDING TO THE GOVERNMENT
PLANS AND PROGRAMMES, A FUNCTIONAL NATIONAL LIBRARY WILL NOT BE ESTABLISHED
BEFORE 1985.
