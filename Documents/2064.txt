RURAL LIBRARIES IN WEST MALAYSIA WITH RECOMMENDATIONS.
     
(MLS DISSERTATION.) IN RECENT YEARS, THERE HAS BEEN A GROWING INTEREST IN
RURAL LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT BY VARIOUS AUTHORITIES IN WEST MALAYSIA. DISCUSSES
THE SERVICES OFFERED BY GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND THE STATE PUBLIC LIBRARY
CORPORATIONS AND PROBLEMS. MAKES RECOMMENDATIONS AND HOPES THAT THE
CORPORATIONS WILL ABSORB THE VARIOUS RURAL LIBRARIES ALREADY ESTABLISHED INTO
THEIR OWN SYSTEMS AS SOON AS IT IS FEASIBLE.
