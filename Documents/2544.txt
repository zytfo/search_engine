AN EXPERIMENT IN M LIB SC TEACHING.
     
PRESENTS AN ANALYTICAL VIEW OF THE M LIB SC COURSE WHICH IS BEING OFFERED AT
BOMBAY UNIVERSITY. COMPARES THE COURSE CONTENT AND OTHER ASPECTS OF THIS
COURSE WITH THOSE AT DELHI UNIVERSITY AND BENARAS HINDU UNIVERSITY. ANALYSES
IN DETAIL THE METHOD OF TEACHING, EXAMINATION PATTERN AND OTHER RELATED
ASPECTS OF BOMBAY UNIVERSITY AND COMPARES THEM WITH THE OTHER UNIVERSITIES
OFFERING M LIB SC COURSES. CONCLUDES THAT THE BOMBAY UNIVERSITY COURSE HAS
STARTED A NEW EXPERIMENT WHERE REGULAR CLASSES ARE NOT REQUIRED AND NO
FULL-TIME TEACHERS ARE PROVIDED.
