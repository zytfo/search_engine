TRENDS IN INDUSTRIAL INFORMATION RESOURCE CENTERS.
     
INFORMATION IS AN ESSENTIAL PART OF ANY CORPORATION'S RESOURCES. ONLY
RECENTLY, HOWEVER, HAS EMPHASIS BEEN PLACED ON THE INTEGRATION OF VARIOUS
KINDS OF INFORMATION SERVICES OFFERED IN THE CORPORATE ENVIRONMENT. A TREND
HAS EMERGED TO VIEW THE VARIETY OF INFORMATION PROCESSING TECHNOLOGIES FROM A
GENERAL SYSTEMS PERSPECTIVE, TERMED 'INFORMATION RESOURCE MANAGEMENT' (IRM). A
MAJOR CONCERN OF TOP MANAGEMENT IS TO EDUCATE USERS IN INFORMATION AS A
CORPORATE RESOURCE.
