IFLA AND LIBER SEMINARS ON LIBRARY BUILDINGS.

REPORT OF 2 SEMINARS ON LIBRARY BUILDINGS. THE 1ST, WHICH TOOK PLACE IN
DENMARK, WAS ORGANISED BY IFLA AND TOOK AS ITS THEME LIBRARY INTERIORS; THE
2ND, ORGANISED BY LIBER WAS HELD IN HEIDELBERG IN NOV 80, AND WAS ENTITLED
'NEW PROBLEMS IN LIBRARY ARCHITECTURE'. PAPERS AND DISCUSSION ARE REVIEWED.
