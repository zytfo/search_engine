CONSIDERATIONS ON THE CASE FOR A SPECIFIC STANDARD FOR THE DESCRIPTION OF OLD
BOOKS.
     
EXAMINES IFLA'S DRAFT ISBD(A) OF NOV 78, NOTING DIFFERENCES AND SIMILARITIES
WITH ISBD(M). CONCLUDES THAT A MORE SATISFACTORY SOLUTION WOULD BE TO PRODUCE
AN APPENDIX TO ISBD(M) PROVIDING FOR ALL CASES WHERE A LARGER MEASURE OF
DESCRIPTIVE DETAIL WAS APPROPRIATE.
