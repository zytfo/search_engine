WORD PROCESSING AND OFFICE PRINTING.
     
REPORT ON A TRAVELLING FELLOWSHIP TO THE USA IN JUNE 80, FINANCED BY THE
WINSTON CHURCHILL MEMORIAL TRUST. REPRESENTS A SNAPSHOT OF THE US SITUATION
REGARDING WORD PROCESSING AND OFFICE PRINTING, THOSE ASPECTS OF THE SCENE THAT
APPEARED SIGNIFICANT TO THE AUTHOR, AND SOME SYSTEMS AND EQUIPMENT SEEN.
