RESEARCH IN PROGRESS' STATISTICAL SOURCES OF INFORMATION ON CANADIAN PUBLIC
LIBRARIES.
     
DESCRIBES A RESEARCH PROJECT TO IDENTIFY AND EVALUATE VARIOUS SOURCES OF
STATISTICAL INFORMATION ON CANADIAN PUBLIC LIBRARIES THAT ARE AVAILABLE TO
RESEARCHERS. RESEARCH ON PRE-1921 SOURCES IS COMPLETE AND HAS BEGUN ON THE
NEXT TIME PERIODS. EARLY RESULTS WERE PUBLISHED IN THE 1978 STATISTICS CANADA
ANNUAL REPORT WHILE RESULTS FROM POST-1921 SOURCES SHOULD BE PUBLISHED IN THE
1979 OR 1980 STATISTICS CANADA ANNUAL REPORT ON PUBLIC LIBRARIES. WORK HAS
STARTED ON A LITERATURE REVIEW AIMED AT DEVELOPING A LIST OF SUB-NATIONAL
SOURCES TO BE EVALUATED.
