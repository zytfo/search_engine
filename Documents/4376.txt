VIEWDATA...THREE YEARS LATER.
     
THE 3 YEARS WHICH HAVE ELAPSED SINCE THE FIRST UK TEST TRIAL OF PRESTEL HAVE
NOT SEEN AS FAST A GROWTH IN USAGE AS WAS ESTIMATED. UK EXPERIENCE WITH
PRESTEL IS REVIEWED IN LIGHT OF THE DEVELOPMENT OF COMPETING SYSTEMS BY
CANADA, FRANCE AND JAPAN. THE IMPLICATIONS FOR VIEWDATA SYSTEMS FOR AUSTRALIA
ARE STUDIED.
