THE IAP LIBRARY AT KING ABDULAZIZ INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT.
     
DESCRIBES THE SCOPE AND ACTIVITIES OF THE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORTS PROJECTS
(IAP) OFFICE OF THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE AND AVIATION, SAUDI ARABIA. THE IAP
LIBRARY HAS FAVOURED THE APPLICATION OF TECHNOLOGY-INTENSIVE TECHNIQUES,
INCLUDING THE DEVELOPMENT OF A COMPUTERISED LIBRARY ADMINISTRATION SYSTEM
WHICH MAKES USE OF UK AND LC MARC TAPES. FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS MAY INCLUDE
INTERFACING WITH A COMPUTER-INDEXED AUTOMATED MICROFICHE RETRIEVAL SYSTEM.
SUGGESTS THAT LIBRARY AND INFORMATION PROVISION IN SAUDI ARABIA MAY BE
ENTERING A PERIOD OF RAPID EXPANSION.
