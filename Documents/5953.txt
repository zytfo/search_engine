FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLES FOR CURRICULUM DEVELOPMENT.

THE CURRICULUM MUST REFLECT THE EDUCATIONAL MILIEU, AND RESPOND TO IT.
ACCORDINGLY CONSIDERS IN TURN THE RELEVANCE OF SOCIAL FACTORS, THE PROCESS OF
HUMAN DEVELOPMENT, THE NATURE OF LEARNING, AND THE NATURE OF KNOWLEDGE,
SHOWING THE INFLUENCE OF EACH OF THESE. ONLY AFTER SUCH AN ANALYSIS IS IT
POSSIBLE TO FORM A VIABLE CURRICULUM.
