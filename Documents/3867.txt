REPORT OF THE TASK FORCE ON CATALOG USAGE.
     
AFTER CONSIDERING THE EFFECTS OF AACR2 ON THE PRESENT CATALOGUE SYSTEM, THIS
TASK FORCE ON CATALOGUE USAGE RECOMMENDS THAT THE UNIVERSITY LIBRARY RETAIN
THE PRESENT CATALOGUE BEYOND 1981 AND LATER ADOPT AN ON-LINE CATALOGUE. REPORT
NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
