SOME FACTORS INFLUENCING CHOICE OF HOST SYSTEMS.
     
CHOOSING A SUITABLE ON-LINE HOST SYSTEM IS BECOMING AN INCREASINGLY COMPLEX
PROCEDURE. A SET OF CRITERIA IS SUGGESTED AS A FRAMEWORK TO HELP IN DECISION
MAKING. THE IMPORTANCE OF IDENTIFYING THE SPECIFIC NEEDS OF THE USER IS
STRESSED AND THE MATCHING OF THESE REQUIREMENTS TO CONSIDERATIONS SUCH AS
RANGE OF DATA BASES OFFERED, COMMAND LANGUAGE, METHODS OF ACCESS AS WELL AS
ECONOMICS AND QUALITY OF SERVICE IS EXAMINED.
