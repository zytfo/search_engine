PHOTOGRAPHY & ITS USE IN LIBRARY WORK.
     
PHOTOGRAPHY HAS TAKEN ON AN INCREASINGLY IMPORTANT ROLE IN PUBLIC LIBRARY
WORK. MOST OFTEN THOUGHT OF IN TERMS OF OUTREACH WORK AND PUBLICITY, ITS
EFFECT HAS BEEN EVEN MORE DRAMATIC IN THE IMPACT OF MICROFORMS. AMONG READER
SERVICES THE AVAILABILITY OF PHOTOCOPIERS IS ALREADY TAKEN FOR GRANTED AND IN
THE NEAR FUTURE IMPROVED MODELS CAPABLE OF REPRODUCING TONE ILLUSTRATIONS OR
INSTANT COPIERS USING POLAROID FILM WILL BE EQUALLY COMMON. SUGGESTS SOME
IDEAS AND SOME METHODS FOR CREATIVE APPLICATION OF PHOTOGRAPHY IN LIBRARY
WORK, TOGETHER WITH SUFFICIENT TECHNICAL INFORMATION TO ENABLE THE
NON-PHOTOGRAPHER TO PUT THEM INTO EFFECT.
