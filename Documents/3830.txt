PARTICIPATION OF TEACHING FACULTY IN LIBRARY BOOK SELECTION.
     
ANALYSES THE RESULTS OF A STUDY UNDERTAKEN FOR THE SCHOOL OF EDUCATION OF A US
MIDWESTERN UNIVERSITY DESIGNED TO EXAMINE SELECTED FACTORS WHICH ARE LIKELY TO
INFLUENCE THE ACTIVENESS OF THE TEACHING FACULTY IN THEIR PARTICIPATION IN
LIBRARY BOOK SELECTION. SHOWS THAT THE FOLLOWING FACTORS WERE SIGNIFICANTLY
CORRELATED TO THE DEGREE OF ACTIVITY IN BOOK SELECTION' YEARS OF TEACHING;
AGE; NUMBER OF GRADUATE COURSES TAUGHT; PUBLICATION ACTIVITIES; AND
PROFESSIONAL CONTACTS.
