INTERACTIVE RETRIEVAL SYSTEM ON TECHNIQUES AND TECHNOLOGY OF MINERALS
PROCESSING.
     
DEMONSTRATES THE NECESSITY OF AN INTERACTIVE RETRIEVAL SYSTEM TO EVALUATE THE
PRESENT STATE OF AND TO FORECAST DEVELOPMENTS IN THE TECHNOLOGIES AND
TECHNIQUES OF RAW MINERAL PROCESSING. DESCRIBES THE STRUCTURE OF A SYSTEM
PERFORMING THE FUNCTIONS OF INFORMATION STORAGE, RETRIEVAL, UP-DATING,
GROUPING, AND OUTPUT.
