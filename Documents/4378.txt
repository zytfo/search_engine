ELECTRONIC NEWSPAPER DEBUTS IN CANADA.
     
AN ELECTRONIC EDITION OF LA PRESSE IS BEING OFFERED BY THE VIDEOTRON GROUP
USING THE TELIDON SYSTEM. NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL NEWS WILL BE OFFERED 24
HOURS A DAY TO 551,000 CABLE TELEVISION SUBSCRIBERS IN MONTREAL. ABOUT 50
SEQUENTIAL SCREENS ARE REPEATED, EACH CYCLE TAKING ABOUT 20 MINUTES.
