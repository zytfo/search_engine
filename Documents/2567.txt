CHINESE LIBRARIES DURING AND AFTER THE CULTURAL REVOLUTION.
     
PAPER BASED PRIMARILY ON DATA COLLECTED ON A VISIT IN 1978 TO THE PEOPLE'S
REPUBLIC OF CHINA. PROVIDES A BRIEF ACCOUNT OF THE HISTORY OF CHINESE
LIBRARIES IN CONTEMPORARY CHINA, AND ATTEMPTS TO DESCRIBE HOW THEY ARE MEETING
THE CHALLENGES OF A CULTURE MOVING AWAY FROM ANTI-INTELLECTUALISM TO
SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL RESEARCHES, AND WHAT THEIR SALIENT PROBLEMS ARE.
THE WORK IS IN 3 MAIN SECTIONS' BEFORE THE CULTURAL REVOLUTION, 1949-1966; THE
CULTURAL REVOLUTION, 1966-1976; AND AFTER THE CULTURAL REVOLUTION, 1976-.
