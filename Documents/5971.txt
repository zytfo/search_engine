ORIGIN AND DEVELOPMENT OF PRINTING PRESS IN SIND.

EXAMINES THE DEVELOPMENT OF PRINTING IN THE SIND PROVINCE OF PAKISTAN SINCE
THE PUBLICATION OF THE 1ST NEWSPAPER THERE IN 1843. GIVES BRIEF DETAILS OF
SOME OF THE MANY PRIVATE PRESSES ESTABLISHED DURING THE 19TH AND EARLY 20TH
CENTURY. NOTES THE EFFECT ON PRINTING OF THE ADOPTION OF THE SINDHI ALPHABET
IN 1853, AND DESCRIBES THE WORK DONE BY THE EDUCATION DEPARTMENT'S PRESSES IN
PRINTING SCHOOL TEXTBOOKS IN SINDHI.
