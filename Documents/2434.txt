CHANNEL 2000' DESCRIPTION AND FINDINGS OF A VIEWDATA TEST CONDUCTED BY OCLC IN
COLUMBUS, OHIO OCTOBER-DECEMBER 1980.
     
A REPORT OF A PROJECT BY THE ONLINE COMPUTER LIBRARY CENTER (OCLC), OHIO, TO
TEST THE FEASIBILITY OF DEVELOPING THEIR OWN VIEWDATA SYSTEM. COVERS' THE
VARIOUS FACETS OF NEW CONSUMER ELECTRONIC INFORMATION SERVICES, DETAILS OF THE
CHANNEL 2000 VIEWDATA PROJECT-PROJECT INITIATION, CONDUCT OF THE TEST,
SERVICES OFFERED, USER EQUIPMENT, THE HOST COMPUTER AND SOFTWARE; AND FINDINGS
AND CONCLUSIONS DERIVED FROM THE PROJECT.
