INFORMATION NEEDS AND THE USE OF INFORMATION SYSTEMS BY MEDICAL DOCTORS IN
NIGERIA.
     
(PHD THESIS-CASE WESTERN RESERVE UNIVERSITY.) IDENTIFIES THE KINDS OF
INFORMATION NEEDED BY NIGERIAN MEDICAL DOCTORS AND THE VARIETY OF INFORMATION
SYSTEMS AND CHENNELS THESE DOCTORS USE TO FIND INFORMATION, AND TESTS THE
RELATIONSHIP OF SELECTED VARIABLES AND THE USE OF INFORMATION SYSTEMS BY THE
DOCTORS.
