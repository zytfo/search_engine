NON-PRINT MEDIA-AUDIO HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE.
     
TRACES THE DEVELOPMENT OF AUDIO HARDWARE FOR COMMERCIAL PURPOSES AND THE
COST-EFFECTIVE TRADE-OFFS FROM THE TECHNOLOGY WHICH HAVE BEEN USED IN ITS
ADAPTATION FOR THE PRINT HANDICAPPED. BRIEFLY DISCUSSED ARE METHODS USED IN
SOFTWARE PREPARATION' HIGH VOLUME FOR LIBRARIES, MEDIUM VOLUME FOR ACADEMIC
USE AND LOW VOLUME FOR SPECIAL INFORMATION MATERIALS FOR PRIVATE INDIVIDUALS.
