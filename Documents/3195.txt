RESOURCE NOTEBOOK ON STAFF DEVELOPMENT.
     
FOCUSES ON LIBRARY STAFF DEVELOPMENT. THE INTRODUCTION PROVIDES BACKGROUND AND
A BRIEF CONCEPTUAL OVERVIEW OF STAFF DEVELOPMENT. THE FIRST SECTION OF
DOCUMENTS PROVIDES EXAMPLES OF DATA GATHERING TOOLS AND APPROACHES TO
GATHERING INFORMATION. THE SECOND GROUP OF DOCUMENTS ILLUSTRATES METHODS OF
DESCRIBING AND ANALYZING CURRENT STAFF DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMMES, AND THE FINAL
SECTION CONTAINS EXAMPLES OF RECOMMENDATIONS FOR NEW APPROACHES TO STAFF
DEVELOPMENT WHICH HAVE BEEN MADE BY LIBRARIES.
