AMERICA' CULTURE AND SOCIETY' DEFINING AND DESCRIBING A VISUAL ARCHIVE.
     
TALK DELIVERED AT THE ART LIBRARIES SOCIETY OF NORTH AMERICA 9TH ANNUAL
CONFERENCE, SAN FRANCISCO, FEB 81, BY THE DIRECTOR OF THE UK MICROPUBLISHING
FIRM, CHADWYCK-HEALY LTD. DESCRIBES 5 VISUAL ARTS PROJECTS UNDERTAKEN BY THE
FIRM IN RECENT YEARS, EMPHASISING THE US CONTENT, AND DISCUSSES THE
DEVELOPMENT OF THE FIRM'S PUBLISHING PROGRAMME IN THE REPRODUCTION OF VISUAL
ARCHIVES.
