TOYS AND GAMES IN LIBRARIES.
     
PRESENTS THE VIEWS OF CHILD PSYCHOLOGISTS WHICH JUSTIFY THE CASE FOR TOY
LIBRARIES. GIVES THE HISTORY OF TOY LIBRARIES IN THE UK, CONSIDERS
ADMINISTRATIVE PROBLEMS AND DISCUSSES POSSIBLE SOLUTIONS. ALSO GIVES EXAMPLES
OF TOY LIBRARIES ABROAD, AND SUGGESTS PLANS FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A TOY
LIBRARY SERVICE WITHIN THE UK PUBLIC LIBRARY SERVICE (SEE ALSO 81/5282-5283).
