NEW INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY-SOCIAL ASPECTS, USAGE AND TRENDS.
     
FOLLOWING A DEFINITION OF WHAT IS MEANT BY INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) AND A
BRIEF MENTION OF SOME OF THE APPLICATIONS TO WHICH IT CAN BE PUT, ADVANTAGES
AND DISADVANTAGES OF NEW IT SYSTEMS ARE REVIEWED. THE IMPLICATIONS FOR MASS
UTILISATION OF THE NEW TECHNOLOGY ARE DISCUSSED AND THE NECESSITY FOR
EDUCATION AND TRAINING IN ITS USE IS STRESSED. AN OVERVIEW OF VARIOUS VIDEOTEX
TRIALS IS GIVEN TOGETHER WITH REACTIONS OF USERS. FINALLY SOME TRENDS IN THE
USE AND APPLICATION OF IT ARE NOTED AND A CALL IS MADE FOR ADDITIONAL STUDIES
TO ASCERTAIN THE ACTUAL USE THE PUBLIC MAKES OF THE INFORMATION IT SEEKS.
