AND PLANNING IS STRESSED. TO ATTAIN A BETTER PERFORMANCE MEASURING OF ROUTINE
SERVICE OPERATIONS IS NOT ENOUGH BUT ALSO HAVE TO INCLUDE USER PERFORMANCE IN
