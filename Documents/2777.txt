THE STATUS OF MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEMS (MIS) IN STATE DEPARTMENTS OF
EDUCATION. NATIONAL SURVEY.
     
PREPARED FOR TOP AND MIDDLE MANAGERS, THIS PAPER REPRESENTS A COMPARATIVE
OVERVIEW OF MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT IN 40 STATE DEPARTMENTS
OF EDUCATION. QUESTIONNAIRES ELICITED INFORMATION ABOUT THE BACKGROUND OF
PARTICIPATING AGENCIES AS WELL AS CONCEPTUAL CONCERNS, THE PLANNING AND
IMPLEMENTING OF MIS DATA COLLECTION, DATA PROCESSING, AND INFORMATION
DELIVERY. SPECIFIC CONCLUSIONS DRAWN FROM THIS STUDY AND SOME OF THEIR
IMPLICATIONS ARE DISCUSSED TOGETHER WITH A COMPLETE DESCRIPTION OF THE SURVEY.
