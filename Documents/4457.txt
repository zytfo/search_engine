A PERSONAL TRIBUTE TO JACQUELINE VIAUX ON HER RETIREMENT FROM THE CHAIRMANSHIP
OF THE IFLA SECTION OF ART LIBRARIES.
     
JACQUELINE VIAUX'S WORK HAS ALSO INCLUDED THE POST OF 'CONSERVATEUR EN CHEF'
AT THE BIBLIOTHEQUE FORNEY IN PARIS, ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IN THE 'ASSOCIATION
DES BIBLIOTHECAIRES FRANCAIS' AND CHAIRMANSHIP OF THAT ASSOCIATION'S
'SOUS-SECTION' OF ART LIBRARIANS FROM 1973-80.
