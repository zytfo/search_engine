THE PATENT LIBRARY IN THE INSTITUTE OF TECHNICAL INFORMATION OF CHINA.
     
A BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE PATENT LIBRARY IN THE INSTITUTE OF SCIENTIFIC AND
TECHNICAL INFORMATION OF CHINA (ISTIC). WITHIN THIS LIBRARY RESIDES CHINA'S
LARGEST PATENT LITERATURE COLLECTION, AND PATENT DOCUMENTATION AND INFORMATION
CENTRE. PRESENTED AS A FOLLOW-UP TO AN ARTICLE BY A. WITTMAN (2SEE 182/1221),
THE PAPER OFFERS FURTHER INFORMATION FACILITIES IN CHINA.
