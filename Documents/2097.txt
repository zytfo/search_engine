CONTRIBUTIONS OF BLACK ACADEMIC LIBRARIES IN PROVIDING SERVICES TO THE BLACK
COMMUNITY.
     
(PHD THESIS-FLORIDA STATE UNIVERSITY SCHOOL OF LIBRARY SCIENCE). FOR MANY
YEARS PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN SOUTHERN USA NEGLECTED SERVICES TO BLACK PROSPECTIVE
PATRON. PUBLIC AND PRIVATE BLACK ACADEMIC LIBRARIES OFFERED THE ONLY SOLUTION
TO AN ILLITERATE BLACK SOCIETY. STUDIES COMMUNITY SERVICES FROM BLACK ACADEMIC
LIBRARIES DURING 1940-70. SOME ACHIEVEMENTS WERE MADE DURING THIS PERIOD
DESPITE BUDGET RESTRICTIONS. DESCRIBES THE HISTORICAL DEVELOPMENT OF BLACK
ACADEMIC LIBRARIES, AND CONCENTRATES PARTICULARLY ON TALLADEGA COLLEGE
LIBRARY. ALSO DISCUSSES THE RESULTS OF A QUESTIONNAIRE SENT TO 25 BLACK
ACADEMIC LIBRARIES.
