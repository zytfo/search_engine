A CASE ABOUT LIBRARIES: BRIEF OF 'AMICUS CURIAE'.
     
DETAILS THE ACTION TAKEN BY THE LONG ISLAND LIBRARY ASSOCIATION COALITION
AGAINST THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF ISLAND TREES, NEW YORK. THE CASE ARISES FROM
THE ACTION OF ELECTED MEMBERS OF THE BOARD IN ORDERING THE REMOVAL OF 9 BOOKS
FROM THE SHELVES OF THE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL LIBRARY, ONE BOOK FROM THE JUNIOR
HIGH SCHOOL LIBRARY AND ONE FROM THE SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL CURRICULUM.
