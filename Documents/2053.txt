MEDICAL LIBRARIANS AND THE WHITE HOUSE CONFERENCE' FROM COMPLACENCY, TO
CONCERN, TO COMMITMENT.
     
THE ATTITUDE OF US MEDICAL LIBRARIANS TOWARDS THE WHITE HOUSE CONFERENCE ON
LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SERVICES (HELD IN NOV 79) WAS SLOW TO EVOLVE FROM THE
INITIAL COMPLACENCY TO A STRONG COMMITMENT. DESCRIBES THIS EVOLUTION AND
OUTLINES THE PARTICIPATION OF MEDICAL LIBRARIANS AND THE MEDICAL LIBRARY
ASSOCIATION IN CONFERENCE-RELATED ACTIVITIES.
