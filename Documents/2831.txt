REPORT OF THE COMMITTEE ON THE FUTURE OF THE RUTGERS BIBLIOGRAPHIC RECORD.
     
REPORT OF A COMMITTEE CONVENED TO EXAMINE ALTERNATIVES AND RECOMMEND
PROCEDURES BY WHICH THE RUTGERS UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES WOULD PROVIDE ACCESS TO
THEIR COLLECTIONS, EXAMINING CURRENT PRACTICES FOR BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL, AND
RECOMMENDING ECONOMICALLY FEASIBLE SYSTEMS THAT WOULD BE COMPATIBLE WITH AACR2
AND BE EASILY UTILISED BY LIBRARY STAFF AND USERS.
