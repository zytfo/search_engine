SECURITY IN THE ART LIBRARY.
     
NOTES THAT THE NATURE OF MUCH MATERIAL IN THE ART LIBRARY POSES SECURITY
PROBLEMS. CONSIDERS SOME SOLUTIONS-CLOSED ACCESS, ELECTRONIC SECURITY DEVICES,
OWNER IDENTIFICATION, AND PROVISION OF GOOD PHOTOCOPYING FACILITIES. POINTS
OUT THAT EFFICIENT LIBRARY ADMINISTRATION (INCLUDING REGULAR STOCK CHECKING
AND ENFORCING OF SANCTIONS) IS ESSENTIAL AND CONSIDERS LIBRARY PLANNING FROM
THE SECURITY VIEWPOINT.
