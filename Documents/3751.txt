THE PROJECT LEADER.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS OF SELECTED
PROFESSIONALS. REPORT ON THE INFORMATION NEEDS AND USES OF JOHN L. ROBINSON, A
PROJECT LEADER FOR THE DIVISION FINANCIAL SYSTEMS, HEALTH CARE SYSTEMS OF THE
INFORMATION SERVICES BUSINESS DIVISION WITH A MAJOR COMPANY. ROBINSON REQUIRES
TECHNICAL INFORMATION ON PROGRAMMING LANGUAGES, AND GENERAL AND TECHNICAL
INFORMATION ON COMPUTER HARDWARE AND HOW DEVELOPMENTS IN THE AREA AFFECT
PROGRAMMING LANGUAGES. HE ALSO REQUIRES MATERIAL ON THE CORPORATION AND THE
HOSPITAL HE IS DESIGNING A SYSTEM FOR. DESCRIBES THE USES HE MAKES OF THE
INFORMATION, THE WAYS IN WHICH HE ORGANISES IT, AND DIFFICULTIES IN OBTAINING
INFORMATION.
