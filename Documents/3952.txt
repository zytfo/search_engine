U.S. CONGRESSIONAL INFORMATION' CHARACTERISTICS AND INTERNATIONAL APPLICATIONS.
     
US GOVERNMENT PUBLICATIONS REPRESENT A RICH RESOURCE OF INFORMATION ON A
MULTITUDE OF TOPICS. TYPES OF PUBLICATIONS PRODUCED BY THE US FEDERAL
GOVERNMENT INCLUDE SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL, POLICY-RELATED AND STATISTICAL.
THE PURPOSE OF THIS PAPER IS TO DESCRIBE THOSE POLICY-RELATED PUBLICATIONS,
CONGRESSIONAL PUBLICATIONS, THAT SUPPORT THE LEGISLATION OF THE US. SPECIAL
ATTENTION WILL BE GIVEN TO THE CHARACTERISTICS OF THIS BODY OF INFORMATION AND
APPLICATIONS FOR THE INTERNATIONAL USER AND INFORMATION PROFESSIONAL.
