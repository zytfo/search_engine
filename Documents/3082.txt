PLAYING WITH NUMBERS. COUNTY LIBRARIES AS REFLECTED IN STATISTICS.
     
USING 1978 STATISTICAL DATA FROM THE CENTRE FOR LIBRARY SCIENCE AND
METHODOLOGY, AN ANALYSIS OF HUNGARIAN COUNTY LIBRARIES IS MADE. EXAMINES SUCH
LIBRARIES IN TERMS OF SIZE, STAFF NUMBERS, ACQUISITION FUNDS, YEARLY
ACCESSIONING NUMBERS, ENROLLED READERS AND LENDING STATISTICS. ATTEMPTS TO
ASSESS THE ROLE OF COUNTY LIBRARIES IN RELATION TO CITY LIBRARIES AND
EVALUATES THE LEVEL OF PROVISION FOR USERS.
