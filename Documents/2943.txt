THE USE OF ISBNS COMPARED WITH KEYWORDS AS MEANS OF RETRIEVING BIBLIOGRAPHIC
RECORDS ON-LINE.
     
(PROJECT UNDERTAKEN FOR THE BNB RESEARCH FUND, JAN-NOV 79) REPORT OF AN
EXPERIMENT DESIGNED TO QUANTIFY THE TIMES TAKEN TO RETRIEVE BIBLIOGRAPHIC
RECORDS AT AN ON-LINE TERMINAL USING INTERNATIONAL STANDARD BOOK NUMBERS AND
KEYWORDS. THE OVERALL RATIO IN FAVOUR OF SEARCHING BY ISBN WAS FOUND TO BE
1'1&.912.
