VIDEOTEX AND LIBRARIES.

THE STUDY EXPLORED THE CONCEPT OF INTERACTIVE VIDEOTEX (VIEWDATA) IN A LIBRARY
SETTING AND THE POTENTIAL USEFULNESS FOR THE AUSTRALIAN BUSINESS COMMUNITY. IT
WAS FUNDED JOINTLY BY THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF AUSTRALIA AND TELECOM AUSTRALIA
AND UNDERTAKEN BY INSEARCH LTD. STUDY ALSO UNDERTOOK AN ASSESSMENT OF THE
USEFULNESS OF VIDEOTEX SERVICES 'IN-HOUSE' AS INFORMATION SOURCES FOR THE
NATIONAL LIBRARY, FOR PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND AS A VEHICLE FOR THE LIBRARY'S
SERVICES AND ANNOUNCEMENTS.
