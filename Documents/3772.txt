ACTIVITY REPORT 1978-1980 OF THE AGRICULTURAL WORKING GROUP OF CIDST.
     
THE AGRICULTURAL WORKING GROUP (OF THE COMMITTEE FOR INFORMATION AND
DOCUMENTATION IN SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY) ADVISES THE COMMISSION OF THE
EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES ON COMMUNITY ACTIVITIES IN THE FIELD OF AGRICULTURAL
INFORMATION. PROGRESS MADE FROM 1978 TO 1980 IS DESCRIBED IN THE SUMMARY
ACTIVITY REPORT.
