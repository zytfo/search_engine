ACQUISITIONS IN THE SPECIAL LIBRARY.

ARTICLE BASED ON A PAPER GIVEN AT THE ANNUAL MEETING OF THE SOCIETY FOR
SCHOLARLY PUBLISHING, SAN FRANCISCO, 2 JUNE 81. LIBRARIES IN BUSINESS AND
INDUSTRY ARE SERVICE-ORIENTED. THEY ARE LESS INTERESTED IN THE PHYSICAL
PACKAGE THAN IN THE CURRENCY OF THE INFORMATION AND THE SPEED WITH WHICH IT
CAN BE DELIVERED.
