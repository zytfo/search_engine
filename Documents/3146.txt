A CITATION ANALYSIS OF INTERNAL AND EXTERNAL CONNECTIONS OF A RESEARCH BRANCH'
A CASE STUDY OF SOLAR ENERGY RESEARCH IN THE USSR.
     
DISCUSSES THE INTERNAL AND EXTERNAL CONNECTIONS OF A RESEARCH SPECIALITY IN A
COUNTRY BY TAKING A CASE STUDY OF SOLAR CONCENTRATOR AND COLLECTOR RESEARCH IN
THE USSR. THE VARIOUS FACTORS ARE IDENTIFIED, WHICH ASSESS THE DEVELOPMENT OF
THE SPECIALITY.
