THE NATIONAL CENTRE FOR LETTERS.
     
DESCRIBES ACTIVITIES OF THE FRENCH NATIONAL CENTRE FOR LETTERS SINCE
REORGANISATION IN 1976. STRUCTURE AND RESOURCES ARE OUTLINED, AND DETAILS
GIVEN ON CURRENT POLICY AS IMPLEMENTED BETWEEN 1976 AND 1979 IN PROVIDING
ASSISTANCE FOR THE FOLLOWING' LITERARY CREATION AND AUTHORS; CULTURAL
ACTIVITIES AND JOURNALS; PUBLISHING; PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND BOOK PROMOTION;
TRANSLATION; AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE FOR AUTHORS.
