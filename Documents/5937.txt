QUANTIFIABLE CHARACTERISTICS OF BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATABASES.

(EXTENDED VERSION OF THE LECTURE 'QUANTIFIZIERBARE MERKMALE VON DATENBANKEN',
PRESENTED AT THE DEUTSCHER DOKUMENTARTAG, WEST BERLIN, 1980.) CONSIDERS THE
QUANTIFIABLE CHARACTERISTICS OF BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATA BASES FROM THE ON-LINE
USER'S VIEWPOINT. GIVES A BRIEF OVERVIEW OF DATA BASE PROPERTIES WHICH HAVE
BEEN STUDIED QUANTITATIVELY' COVERAGE, CURRENCY, COSTS, ACCURACY, INDEXING AND
RETRIEVAL EFFICIENCY. INDICATES THE VALUE OF SUCH STUDIES TO THE ON-LINE USER
AND WHERE FURTHER STUDIES WOULD BE USEFUL.
