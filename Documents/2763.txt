TECHNICAL ACTIVITIES 1980, OFFICE OF STANDARD REFERENCE DATA.
     
THE OFFICE OF STANDARD REFERENCE DATA IS 1 OF 6 PROGRAMME OFFICES IN THE
NATIONAL MEASUREMENT LABORATORY, NATIONAL BUREAU OF STANDARDS. THE PROGRAMME
DEVELOPS AND DISSEMINATES DATA BASES OF CRITICALLY EVALUATED PHYSICAL/CHEMICAL
PROPERTIES OF SUBSTANCES. THE OFFICE OF STANDARD REFERENCE DATA IS RESPONSIBLE
FOR MANAGEMENT AND COORDINATION OF THE PROGRAMME. WORK IS CARRIED OUT THROUGH
A DECENTRALISED NETWORK OF DATA CENTRES AND PROJECTS REFERRED TO AS THE
NATIONAL STANDARD REFERENCE DATA SYSTEM.
