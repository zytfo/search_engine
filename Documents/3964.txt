A LOW-COST SYSTEM FOR PRODUCING MACHINE-READABLE ABSTRACTS.
     
DESCRIBES GEO SYSTEMS' PURPOSE BUILT SYSTEM CENTRED ON A WORD PROCESSOR AND
DESIGNED TO ALLOW INPUT FROM DISTANT STATIONS AND OUTPUT OF BOTH CAMERA-READY
COPY AND STANDARD COMPUTER TAPES FOR INDEXES AND RETRIEVAL PURPOSES. THE
EMPHASIS IS ON A RELATIVELY CHEAP AND SIMPLE SYSTEM, AND IT IS CONCLUDED THAT
THE TOTAL COST WILL ADD NO MORE THAN 2.5% TO THE COST OF EACH ABSTRACT.
