A PRESCRIPTIVE MODEL FOR PLANNING A NATIONAL SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL
INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR EGYPT.
     
(PHD THESIS-UNIVERSITY OF PITTSBURGH.) DESCRIBES A STUDY WHICH SURVEYED THE
PREVAILING CONDITIONS OF THE PRESENT SYSTEM FOR HANDLING SCIENTIFIC AND
TECHNICAL INFORMATION IN EGYPT; INVESTIGATED WHETHER THERE IS A NEED FOR A
NATIONAL SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION SYSTEM; AND PROPOSED A MODEL FOR
THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A NATIONAL TECHNICAL INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR EGYPT.
