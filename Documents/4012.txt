FILM RECORD-A RECORD SHEET FOR CURRENT PHOTOGRAPHY.
     
DESCRIBES A FILM RECORD SHEET (DESIGNED BY THE AUTHOR) TO BE COMPLETED BY THE
PHOTOGRAPHER AFTER THE FILM HAS BEEN PROCESSED. EACH ROLL OF FILM IS THE
SUBJECT OF A RECORD SHEET GIVING DETAILS OF CAMERA, LENS AND OTHER TECHNICAL
ITEMS, CONTENT OF PHOTOGRAPHS, AND PLACE.
