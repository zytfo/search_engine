GOTEBORG CITY LIBRARY AND ITS COMPUTERISED LENDING SYSTEM.
     
A DESCRIPTION OF GOTEBORG CITY LIBRARY, SWEDEN FOLLOWING A STUDY TOUR.
DESCRIBES THE STOCK AND SUBJECT SPECIALISATIONS OF THE LIBRARY; ITS
RELATIONSHIP WITH THE SWEDISH LIBRARY SUPPLIER, BIBLIOTEKSTJANST; THE
INTEGRATED PUBLIC LIBRARY SYSTEM, BUMS; THE LIBRARY'S CATALOGUES AND
COMPUTERISED CATALOGUING; AND THE OPERATION OF THE PLESSEY COMPUTERISED
LENDING SYSTEM.
