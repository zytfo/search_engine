LIBRARY HISTORY IN BRITAIN' PROGRESS AND PROSPECTS.
     
LITERATURE REVIEW OF WRITINGS ON THE HISTORY OF LIBRARIES IN BRITAIN, WITH
SOME COMMENTS ON THE FUTURE OF LIBRARY HISTORY STUDY, BASED ON A SURVEY OF
BRITISH PUBLIC LIBRARY ADMINISTRATIVE RECORDS. APPENDED ARE A REPRINTED
QUESTIONNAIRE, AND A TABLE OF THE EARLIEST MATERIALS HELD BY UK LIBRARY
SYSTEMS.
