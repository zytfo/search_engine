HISTORICAL PERSPECTIVE OF THE WORLD'S ANCIENT LIBRARIES.
     
AN ACCOUNT OF THE WORLD'S 2 MOST ANCIENT LIBRARIES, THE LIBRARY AT NINEVEH AND
THE ALEXANDRIAN LIBRARY, WHICH FLOURISHED OVER 4,000 YEARS AGO. DISCUSSES
THEIR ORIGINS AND ORIGINATORS; DESCRIBES THE ARRANGEMENT OF THEIR CONTENTS;
AND ASSESSES HOW MODERN THEIR ORGANISERS WERE IN THEIR THINKING OF THE
PRINCIPLES OF LIBRARIANSHIP.
