EDUCATIONAL MEDIA AND LEARNING THEORIES.
     
EDUCATIONAL MEDIA WILL NOT REPLACE THE TEACHER, BUT THEY DO MAKE TEACHING
EASIER AND RESULT IN GREATER LEARNING. PRESENTS THE VARIOUS OPINIONS
CONCERNING THEIR USE, NOTING THAT THERE IS NOT SO MUCH DISAGREEMENT OR
CONTRADICTION AS THERE ARE DIFFERENCES IN EMPHASIS. REFERS TO RESEARCH STUDIES
THAT HAVE ESTABLISHED THAT THROUGH CAREFUL SELECTION AND USE OF EDUCATIONAL
MEDIA MANY OBSTACLES FOR EFFECTIVE LEARNING CAN BE OVERCOME.
