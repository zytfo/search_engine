UPDATING SCIENTIFIC INFORMATION THROUGH KEY PUBLICATIONS.
     
THIS PRIMER SHOWS HOW TO UPDATE QUICKLY SCIENTIFIC INFORMATION IN A PARTICULAR
AREA, USING A KEY PUBLICATION FROM THAT AREA. THE METHOD PRESENTED HERE, USING
A KEY PUBLICATION AVOIDS THE USUAL FRUSTRATING SEARCH FOR ALL POSSIBLE KEY
WORDS THAT MIGHT HAVE BEEN USED BY THE PERSONS WHO STORED THE DATA.
