SOURCES AND ACQUISITION PROBLEMS OF SERIALS IN NIGERIAN ACADEMIC LIBRARIES
WITH SPECIFIC REFERENCE TO ALVAN IKOKU COLLEGE OF EDUCATION LIBRARY, OWERRI.
NIGERIA.

(MLS THESIS). A REVIEW OF THE USE OF PERIODICALS IN NIGERIAN ACADEMIC
LIBRARIES AND THE SOURCES OF PERIODICALS USED BY THESE LIBRARIES IS FOLLOWED
BY A GENERAL ACCOUNT OF SOME OF THE PROBLEMS WHICH CAN CROP UP IN THEIR
ACQUISITION. GENERAL PROBLEMS INCLUDE BUDGETARY CONTROL, COST OF PERIODICALS,
INTERNAL ADMINISTRATIVE PROBLEMS, CENSORSHIP. SPECIFIC PROBLEMS IN ACQUISITION
OF FOREIGN AND DOMESTIC PERIODICALS ARE DESCRIBED.
