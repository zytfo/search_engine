SERVICES BASED ON THE DANISH NATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHY AND THE SAMKAT SYSTEM.
     
DESCRIBES THE MECHANISATION PLANS OF THE DANISH NATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHY AND THE
WAY TO THEIR REALISATION. OUTLINES THE FAUST PROGRAMME FOR PUBLIC
LIBRARIES-THE DEVELOPMENT, SOURCES AND SERVICES OF THIS DATA BANK. DESCRIBES
HOW SAMKAT, THE COMPUTERISED SYSTEM FOR DANISH RESEARCH LIBRARIES NEEDS TO BE
IN ACCORDANCE AND COOPERATE WITH THE FAUST PROGRAMME.
