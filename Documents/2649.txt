AN ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF SLIDE LIBRARY LITERATURE.
     
INTENDED AS A GUIDE TO THE CURRENT LITERATURE OF NON-BOOK LIBRARIANSHIP
DEALING WITH THE CARE AND ADMINISTRATION OF SLIDE COLLECTIONS, THIS LISTING
SURVEYS POST-1960 IMPRINTS WHICH EMPHASISE ORGANISATION, CONTROL, INDEXING,
CATALOGUING, GENERAL PLANNING, ACQUISITION, AND THE UNIQUE REQUIREMENTS OF
SPECIALISED AREA COLLECTIONS SUCH AS FINE ARTS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM
NTIS.
