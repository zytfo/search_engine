SEARCHES FOR POLYMERS IN THE BASIC FILES DERIVED FROM THE CHEMICAL ABSTRACTS
SERVICE CHEMICAL REGISTRY SYSTEM.
     
THE METHODS USED FOR SEARCHING THE BASIC SUBSTRUCTURE SEARCH SYSTEM FOR
POLYMERS CONTAINING SPECIFIEC MONOMERS ARE DESCRIBED. THE BASIC FILES ARE
BASED ON CAS REGISTRY AND REG/CAN DATA. THE SEARCHES YIELD REGISTRY NUMBERS
WHICH ARE THEN USED TO RETRIEVE CA ABSTRACT NUMBERS.
