IN FRANCE ITS TELETEL.
     
DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE VIDEOTEX TEST CARRIED OUT IN FRANCE, CALLED THE
TELETEL 3V TEST, IN WHICH 2,500 VOLUNTEER HOUSES ARE LINKED TO A NATIONWIDE
NETWORK OF INFORMATION SERVICES. TELETEL IS BASED ON FRANCE'S ANTIOPE STANDARD
AND IN THE TEST THERE ARE 60,000 PRECOMPOSED PAGES WHICH CAN BE ACCESSED BY
THE USER, WHO IS LINKED BY TELEPHONE LINES TO CII-HONEYWELL BULL MINI 6
COMPUTERS IN THE TELETEL COMPUTER CENTRE IN VELIZY. THE REACTION OF SOME OF
THE TEST USERS IS REPORTED.
