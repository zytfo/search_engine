INFORMATION FOR SMALL INDUSTRY' A SURVEY.
     
PRESENTS THE RESULTS OF A SURVEY CARRIED OUT WITH A SAMPLE OF 51 SMALL SCALE
INDUSTRIAL UNITS SITUATED IN AND AROUND THE TWIN CITIES OF HYDERABAD AND
SIKANDERABAD FOR IDENTIFYING (1) ROLE OF INFORMATION AND INFORMATION
ACTIVITIES IN THE SMALL SCALE INDUSTRIES; (2) ROLE OF VARIOUS AGENCIES IN
INDUSTRIAL INFORMATION TRANSFER; (3) SPECIFIC INFORMATION SERVICES AND
PRODUCTS WHICH THEY REQUIRE AND THE SOURCES THEY TAP TO OBTAIN THIS
INFORMATION. THE NEED FOR A MORE EFFECTIVE NATIONAL NETWORK OF INDUSTRIAL
INFORMATION IS EMPHASISED.
