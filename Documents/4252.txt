PLMRU. REPORT OF A DISSEMINATION WORKSHOP TO REVIEW PROGRESS AND DEVELOPMENTS
IN COMMUNITY INFORMATION SERVICES IN THE PUBLIC LIBRARY.
     
THE WORKSHOP, HELD AT HARROGATE 14-15 MAR 80, AIMED TO PROVIDE AN OPPORTUNITY
FOR LIBRARIANS WITH RESPONSIBILITY FOR POLICY MAKING AND FOR MANAGING SERVICES
TO DISCUSS PROGRESS SINCE THE LAST WORKSHOP HELD IN 1979 AND TO DISCUSS FUTURE
DEVELOPMENTS.
