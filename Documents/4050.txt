MR. ACRL' CHARLES HARVEY BROWN (1875-1960).
     
DISCUSSES THE LIFE AND CAREER OF CHARLES HARVEY BROWN WHOSE CHIEF CONTRIBUTION
TO THE LIBRARY PROFESSION WAS THE FOUNDING OF THE ASSOCIATION OF COLLEGE AND
RESEARCH LIBRARIES (ACRL). HOWEVER THIS WAS ONLY 1 OF A SERIES OF MAJOR
CONTRIBUTIONS WHICH HE MADE TO THE PROGRESS OF ACADEMIC LIBRARIANSHIP.
2SCIENTIFIC SERIALS1 WAS HIS RESEARCH CONTRIBUTION AND THE CULMINATION OF A
LIFETIME DEVOTED TO THE STUDY OF LITERATURE. HE ENDED HIS CAREER AS IOWA STATE
LIBRARY DIRECTOR.
