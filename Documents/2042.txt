ALTERNATIVE MODES FOR PROVIDING GRADUATE EDUCATION FOR LIBRARIANSHIP IN OHIO.
FINAL REPORT.
     
REPORTS THE RESULTS OF THE GRADUATE EDUCATION FOR LIBRARIANSHIP IN OHIO
PROJECT'S INVESTIGATION INTO THE NEED FOR AND PROVISION OF GRADUATE LIBRARY
EDUCATION IN THE STATE. PROVIDES INITIAL ASSESSMENT OF THE 4 OPTIONS STUDIED
AND EXPRESSES THE HOPE THAT THIS REPORT WILL FOSTER FURTHER DEVELOPMENT OF
ALTERNATIVE OPPORTUNITIES FOR SUCH EDUCATION THROUGHOUT THE STATE, ESPECIALLY
THOSE WHICH MAXIMISE ACCESS TO STATE RESIDENTS CONVENIENTLY AND
COST-EFFECTIVELY.
