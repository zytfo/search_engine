GENERAL BIBLIOMETRIC MODELS.
     
THEORETICAL BIBLIOMETRIC MODELS WHICH HAVE BEEN DEVELOPED OVER A PERIOD OF
TIME ARE REVIEWED GENERALLY. 3 MODELS, BY B.C. BROOKES (2SEE1 78/173), D. DE
SOLLA PRICE (2SEE1 78/847) AND A. BOOKSTEIN (2SEE1 82/592) ARE ANALYSED BY
CONSIDERING THEIR INTERNAL PROPERTIES, INTERRELATIONSHIPS AND GENERALITY.
