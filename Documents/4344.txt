PROBLEMS IN APPLYING AACR2 TO MUSIC MATERIALS.
     
SUCCESSIVE REVISIONS IN CATALOGUING CODES HAVE POSED SPECIAL PROBLEMS FOR
MUSIC MATERIALS PRIMARILY BECAUSE THE ECONOMICS OF MUSIC PUBLISHING HAVE
DICTATED TRADITIONAL DEPARTURES IN BIBLIOGRAPHIC PRESENTATION. THE ADOPTION OF
AACR2 ADDITIONALLY FOCUSES ON THE UNIQUE PROBLEM OF PARTICIPATORY CREATION IN
MUSIC; THE PERFORMER AS AUTHOR. STUDIES THE IMPACT OF THESE PHENOMENA ON
CERTAIN MATTERS OF DESCRIPTION, ACCESS POINTS, AND UNIFORM TITLES.
