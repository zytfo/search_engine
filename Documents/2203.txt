WISCONSIN SUMMER LIBRARY PROGRAM MANUAL' ALL CREATURES GREAT AND SMALL. 1980.
     
THIS SUMMER LIBRARY PROGRAMME FOR SCHOOL CHILDREN CENTRES AROUND ANIMALS OF
ALL KINDS. PUBLISHED AS AS AID TO LIBRARIANS IN IMPLEMENTING THE PROGRAMME THE
MANUAL IS MADE AVAILABLE ALONG WITH SUCH THEME RELATED ITEMS AS POSTERS,
BALLOONS, READING RECORD FOLDERS, CERTIFICATES, AND BOOKMARKS. INITIAL
SECTIONS IDENTIFY AND EXPAND THE THEME, PROVIDE ADMINISTRATIVE DATA,
PROMOTIONAL GUIDANCE, AND IDEAS FOR LIBRARY DECORATION, AS WELL AS SUGGESTIONS
FOR PROJECTS AND EVENTS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
