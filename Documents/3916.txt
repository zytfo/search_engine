FALKIRK DISTRICT LIBRARIES' ON-LINE INFORMATION RETRIEVAL PROJECT, MARCH
1980-SEPTEMBER 1981.
     
REVIEWS THE ESTABLISHMENT AND OPERATION OF ON-LINE INFORMATION SERVICES AT
FALKIRK DISTRICT LIBRARIES AS PART OF THE PHASE 2 BRITISH LIBRARY RESEARCH AND
DEVELOPMENT PROJECT TO STUDY THE PROVISION OF ON-LINE INFORMATION RETRIEVAL
SERVICES IN PUBLIC LIBRARIES.
