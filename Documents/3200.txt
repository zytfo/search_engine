DOUGLAS MCGREGOR VISITS JAIPUR INFORMATION CENTRE. MORE OF 'Y' AND LESS OF 'X'
IS THE ANSWER. A CASE STUDY.
     
PRESENTS AN ANALYSIS OF MCGREGOR'S THEORIES OF MOTIVATION AND SEEKS TO EXAMINE
THEIR RELEVANCE AND FEASIBILITY IN THEORY AND PRACTICE OF LIBRARY MANAGEMENT
IN INDIA. THE JAIPUR INFORMATION CENTRE WAS CHOSEN FOR A SYSTEMATIC
APPLICATION OF MCGREGOR'S 'X' AND 'Y' THEORIES. CONCLUDES THAT THE RESULTS OF
THE STUDY SHOW THE THEORIES TO BE RELEVANT TO INDIAN LIBRARY MANAGEMENT.
