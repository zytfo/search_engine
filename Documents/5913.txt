EVALUATION OF THE EFFECTIVENESS OF AUTOMATED INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN LIBRARIES.

CONSIDERS CRITERIA FOR THE EVALUATION OF THE EFFECTIVENESS OF AUTOMATED
INFORMATION SYSTEMS IN LIBRARIES, AND PROCEEDS TO INVESTIGATE IN DETAIL THE
SELECTION EFFECTIVENESS OF THE SYSTEM, I.E. THE RELEVANCE OF ITS OUTPUT, ITS
INFORMATION EFFECTIVENESS, AND THE QUALITY, SCOPE AND FORM OF INPUTS AS WELL
AS THE QUALITY OF THE INFORMATION CONTENT OF DOCUMENTS RETRIEVED.
