TECHNICAL INFORMATION AND DATA SERVICES FOR CITY AND COUNTY GOVERNMENTS.
     
THE REPORT DESCRIBES RESEARCH COMMITTED TO HELPING LOCAL GOVERNMENTS IMPROVE
SERVICES AND CUT COSTS THROUGH THE PRACTICAL USE OF APPLIED SCIENCE AND
TECHNOLOGY. THE RESEARCH EFFORT IS BASED ON A NATIONAL PERSPECTIVE. IT IS
POINTED OUT THAT ALTHOUGH CITY AND COUNTY GOVERNMENTS CAN ACCESS OVER 200
TECHNICAL INFORMATION AND DATA SERVICES, LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS ARE UNDER
INCREASING PRESSURE TO MAKE BETTER DECISIONS.
