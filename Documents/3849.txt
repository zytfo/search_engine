WORDS TO THE WISE.
     
REVIEWS THE PROCEEDINGS AT A SEMINAR ON DISASTER PLANNING AND IN-HOUSE
CONSERVATION OF LIBRARY MATERIALS HELD AT RUTGERS UNIVERSITY GRADUATE SCHOOL
OF LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SCIENCE. BRIEFLY SUMMARISES THE ADVICE WHICH WAS
GIVEN AT THE SEMINAR ON' PREPARING A DISASTER RECOVERY PLAN; COPING WITH FLOOD
AND FIRE; AND THE CARE OF LIBRARY MATERIALS.
