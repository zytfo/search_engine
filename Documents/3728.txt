NEW LIBRARY IN VAGAN AUTHORITY.
     
VAGAN, NORWAY, HAS 9,700 INHABITANTS, OF WHOM 4,000 LIVE IN SVOLVAER. SVOLVAER
LIBRARY HAS A BOOKSTOCK OF 39,000 VOLUMES' THERE ARE 5 BRANCHES. A NEW LIBRARY
WAS OPENED IN NOV 79, SHARING A BUILDING WITH DISTRICT MEDICAL AND DENTAL
SERVICES. THE LIBRARY OCCUPIES THE GROUND FLOOR, WHICH HOUSES LENDING
DEPARTMENTS, READING ROOMS, LOCAL COLLECTION AND EXHIBITION AREA, AND PART OF
THE BASEMENT, USED FOR STACKS, STUDY ROOMS, ROOMS FOR MEETINGS AND OTHER
ACTIVITIES, AND ACCOMMODATION FOR A MOBILE LIBRARY SERVICE.
