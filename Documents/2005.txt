VISIT TO ORDNANCE SURVEY-11TH JUNE, 1980.
     
REPORT OF A VISIT BY MEMBERS OF THE MICROFILM ASSOCIATION OF GREAT BRITAIN
(MAGB) TO THE ORDNANCE SURVEY HEADQUARTERS, 11 JUNE 80. GIVES A BRIEF HISTORY
OF THE ORDNANCE SURVEY, AND DESCRIBES ITS WORK, WITH PARTICULAR ATTENTION TO
THE MICROFILMING OF MAPS, WHICH IS UNDERTAKEN ON BOTH 35 MM AND 70 MM ROLL
FILM.
