THE NEXT DECADE: WHAT WILL IT MEAN TO LIBRARIES? FACSIMILE: THE LONG DISTANCE
COPYING MACHINE.
     
OUTLINES THE FEATURES AND CAPABILITIES OF FACSIMILE TRANSMISSION EQUIPMENT AND
IDENTIFIES SOME DEVELOPMENTS NEEDED TO MAKE THE EQUIPMENT'S USE ECONOMIC IN
LIBRARIES (FLAT-BED MACHINES WITH STAFF-SAVING DEVICES, CONSIDERABLE DROP IN
PRICE, AND BASING OF COMMUNICATION COSTS ON USE RATHER THAN DISTANCE).
