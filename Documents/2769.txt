INFORMATION REQUIREMENTS AND POSSIBILITIES OF DISPENSING PHARMACISTS.
     
SPECIAL LIBRARIES OF THE HUNGARIAN PHARMACEUTICAL CENTRES ARE PRESENTLY
INTEGRAL PARTS OF THE MEDICAL LIBRARY NETWORK, AND ALL REQUIRE INFORMATION
QUICKLY AND DAILY. DISCUSSES THE AVAILABILITY OF INFORMATION AND THE USE MADE
OF IT, WITH ATTENDANT LANGUAGE PROBLEMS, AND THE DIFFICULTIES OF THE SUPPLY OF
MATERIALS WHICH ONLY PARTIALLY SATISFY INFORMATION DEMANDS.
