A GLANCE AT ALMA PUBLIC LIBRARY, QUEBEC.
     
AN ACCOUNT OF HOW SERVICES AT ALMA PUBLIC LIBRARY, QUEBEC WERE EXPANDED ON
RELOCATION IN MORE SPACIOUS QUARTERS, WITHIN THE LIMITATIONS OF SEVERE BUDGET
RESTRICTIONS. PRIORITIES WERE IMPROVEMENT OF THE REFERENCE SERVICE, CREATION
OF SUBJECT DEPARTMENTS, CATALOGUING NEW AND EXISTING STOCK, INTRODUCTION OF
READER SERVICES, COLLABORATION WITH SCHOOLS, AND ORGANISATION OF EXHIBITIONS.
FUTURE PROJECTS ARE OUTLINED.
