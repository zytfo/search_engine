INTEGRATING THE DATA ENCRYPTION STANDARD INTO COMPUTER NETWORKS.
     
THE NBS DATA ENCRYPTION STANDARD MAY BE INTEGRATED INTO COMPUTER NETWORKS TO
PROTECT PERSONAL (NONSHARED) FILES, TO COMMUNICATE SECURELY BOTH ON- AND
OFF-LINE WITH LOCAL AND REMOTE USERS, TO PROTECT AGAINST KEY SUBSTITUTION, TO
AUTHENTICATE DATA, AND TO PROVIDE DIGITAL SIGNATURES USING A NONPUBLIC KEY
ENCRYPTION ALGORITHM.
