TRAVEL POLICIES OF TWENTY-ONE COLLEGE AND UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES.
     
IN A RECENT SURVEY, ACRL FOUND THAT COLLEGE AND RESEARCH LIBRARIES ARE
DEVELOPING FORMAL WRITTEN POLICIES FOR STAFF TRAVEL AND ARE UPGRADING EXISTING
POLICIES. PRESENTS 21 REPRESENTATIVE POLICIES WHICH DISPLAY A WIDE VARIETY OF
INSTITUTIONAL SOLUTIONS TO THE ALLOCATION OF TIME AND FUNDS FOR LIBRARY
EMPLOYEES' PARTICIPATION IN MEETINGS AND OTHER CONTINUING EDUCATION
ACTIVITIES.
