TELECOMMUNICATIONS SERVICES REQUIRED BY DISTRIBUTED AND INTERCONNECTED OFFICE
CENTRES.
     
ANALYSES TRENDS IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF INFORMATION TRANSFER SYSTEMS OF THE
FUTURE, SUCH AS ELECTRONIC MAIL TRANSFER, TELECONFERENCING, COMMUNICATING WORD
PROCESSORS, FACSIMILE, MICROGRAPHICS, AND OTHER TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCES WHICH
ALLOW THE RAPID EXCHANGE OF MULTIMEDIA INFORMATION OVER COMMUNICATIONS
NETWORKS TO GEOGRAPHICALLY SEPARATED LOCATIONS.
