PERIODICALS FOR CHILDREN' A SELECTION.
     
AN EXAMINATION OF SOME OF THE CURRENT PERIODICALS FOR CHILDREN WHICH ARE FOUND
MOST FREQUENTLY IN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND PUBLIC LIBRARY COLLECTIONS. THE
INFORMATION INCLUDES SOME VALUE JUDGEMENTS PERTAINING TO THEIR QUALITY, THEIR
POPULARITY WITH CHILDREN, AND THEIR POTENTIAL USE IN THE SCHOOL, THE LIBRARY,
AND THE HOME.
