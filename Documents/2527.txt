OSCAR G.T. SONNECK: ARCHITECT OF THE 'NATIONAL MUSIC COLLECTION'.
     
DISCUSSES THE WORK OF OSCAR GEORGE THEODORE SONNECK, (BORN IN NEW JERSEY IN
1873), WHO AS CHIEF OF THE MUSIC DIVISION, LIBRARY OF CONGRESS, PRODUCED A
CLASSIFICATION SCHEME FOR THE LIBRARY'S MUSIC COLLECTIONS, ASSEMBLED A VAST
GENERAL COLLECTION AND SPECIALISED COLLECTIONS, AND MAY JUSTLY BE DESCRIBED AS
THE FATHER OF AMERICAN MUSIC LIBRARIANSHIP.
