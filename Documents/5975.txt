WHY COPYRIGHT?.

ARTICLE ADAPTED FROM A PAPER PRESENTED AT A SEMINAR ON COPYRIGHT SPONSORED BY
THE ASSOCIATION OF CANADIAN PUBLISHERS AND THE CANADIAN BOOK PUBLISHERS
COUNCIL, TORONTO, ONTARIO, 19 JUNE 81. COPYRIGHT LAW BEGAN IN RESPONSE TO
TECHNOLOGICAL CHANGE AND HAS OFTEN DEMONSTRATED ITS ADAPTIBILITY TO FURTHER
CHANGE. THE CONTROL IT ENSURES THE CREATOR INVOLVES MORAL AS WELL AS PROPERTY
RIGHTS AND MUST NOT BE ABANDONED LIGHTLY.
