PROBLEMS OF ARCHAEOLOGICAL INDEXING.
     
DISCUSSES IN SOME DETAIL SOME OF THE PROBLEMS ENCOUNTERED IN ARCHAEOLOGICAL
INDEXING. MENTIONS SOME CONVENTIONS CUSTOMARY IN UK ARCHAEOLOGICAL INDEXING,
SETS OUT SOME DILEMMAS, AND CITES SOME GOOD AND BAD EXAMPLES. HIGHLIGHTS THE
FACT THAT ARCHAEOLOGY IS A HIGHLY COMPLEX SUBJECT COVERING A VERY WIDE RANGE
OF ACADEMIC DISCIPLINES AND DEMANDING GREAT FLEXIBILITY AND KNOWLEDGE FROM ITS
INDEXERS.
