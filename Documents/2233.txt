THE ROLE OF BRANCH SYSTEMS IN THE STATE SYSTEM OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL
INFORMATION.
     
BASED ON A PAPER PRESENTED AT THE ALL-UNION MEETING ON THE PLACE OF BRANCH
SYSTEMS IN THE STATE SYSTEM OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL INFORMATION, APR 78.
SECTORAL SYSTEMS ARE COMPONENTS OF THE STATE SYSTEM. DESCRIBES THEIR
DEVELOPMENT; INFORMATION SERVICES; THE ROLE OF THE CENTRAL BODIES IN CREATING
AUTOMATED BRANCH SYSTEMS, IN SCIENTIFIC-INDUSTRIAL PUBLICITY, AND
INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION SYSTEMS; AND THE ATTENTION BEING PAID TO RAISING THE
QUALIFICATION LEVELS OF INFORMATION PERSONNEL.
