VIDEOTEX: WRIT LARGE OR SMALL?.
     
A SURVEY OF VIDEOTEX SYSTEMS BEING DEVELOPED AROUND THE WORLD, CONCENTRATING
ON THE UK'S PRESTEL AND CANADA'S TELIDON, IN ADDITION TO THE GATEWAY FACILITY
FROM WEST GERMANY'S BILDSCHIRMTEXT, WHICH HAS BEEN PURCHASED BY PRESTEL. SHOWS
HOW PRESTEL HAS FALLEN SHORT OF ITS TARGET FOR SUBSCRIBERS.
