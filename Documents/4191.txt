TOWARDS LIBRARY UNIFICATION.
     
A TEXT PREPARED BY THE PUBLIC LIBRARIES SECTION OF THE ASSOCIATION DES
BIBLIOTHECAIRES FRANCAIS (ABF) TO SUPPORT THE 1981 CONFERENCE MOTION ON
LIBRARY UNIFICATION. A PRELIMINARY DISCUSSION BRIEFLY INTRODUCES THE ISSUES
INVOLVED IN ESTABLISHING AN INTEGRATED NATIONAL NETWORK, IN THE FACE OF A
GOVERNMENT POLICY OF DECENTRALISATION. THE ABF PROGRAMME FOR ACHIEVING
UNIFICATION IN SERVICE PROVISION, PERSONNEL ALLOCATION, AND PERSONNEL
TRAINING, IS THEN ELABORATED. IN EACH CASE DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE EXISTING
ORGANISATION OF RESOURCES, THE ADVANTAGES OF UNIFICATION OUTLINED, AND THE
MEANS OF IMPLEMENTING A POLICY OF INTEGRATION INDICATED.
