PROGRAMMING AND SERVICES STRATEGIES FOR TWO-WAY TV AND VIEWDATA SYSTEMS-THE
TELEPHONE VS CABLE TV (PROSPECTUS ONLY).
     
A MARKET SURVEY OF 18 DIFFERENT TYPES OF PROGRAMMING AND SERVICES FOR THE HOME
IS PRESENTED. PAST, PRESENT AND POSSIBLE FUTURE METHODS OF IMPLEMENTING 2-WAY
COMMUNICATION ARE REVIEWED. THE PROBABLE MARKET ACCEPTANCE OF THE VARIOUS
KINDS OF SERVICE IS CONSIDERED IN DETAIL. A SURVEY OF THE SUPPLIERS INVOLVED
IS INCLUDED AND SOME RECOMMENDATIONS ARE MADE FOR VENDORS. RESEARCH AND TRIALS
IN THIS AREA ARE CRITICALLY EXAMINED.
