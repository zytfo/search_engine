A REMARKABLE MATHEMATICIAN ON THE ACTIVITY OF INFORMATION AND DOCUMENTATION'
HOMAGE TO GRIGORE C. MOISIL.
     
RECALLS THE MEMORY OF THE RUMANIAN MATHEMATICIAN, LOGICIAN AND PHILOSOPHER,
GRIGORE C. MOISIL, MEMBER OF THE ACADEMY OF SCIENCES OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC
OF RUMANIA. PAYS HOMAGE TO HIS ACTIVITIES IN PROMOTING AND POPULARISING
SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, AND DESCRIBES HIS INITIATIVES, ACTIVITIES AND
INNOVATIONS IN THE PROMOTION OF INFORMATION AND DOCUMENTATION IN RUMANIA.
