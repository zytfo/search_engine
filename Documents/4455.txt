INTERVIEW: LENYRA CAMARGO FRACCAROLI.
     
THE FOUNDER OF BRAZIL'S 1ST CHILDREN'S LIBRARY, NOW IN HER 70S, DISCUSSES HER
INVOLVEMENT WITH CHILDREN'S LIBRARIES AND LITERATURE, ANSWERING QUESTIONS
ABOUT THE HISTORY AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE SAO PAULO CHILDREN'S LIBRARY NETWORK,
PLANNING STRATEGIES, LIBRARIAN TRAINING PROGRAMMES, AND STANDARDS IN
CONTEMPORARY CHILDREN'S LITERATURE. A SUMMARY IS GIVEN OF HER CAREER.
