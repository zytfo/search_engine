ULRICH'S REFERENCES TO MICROFORM AVAILABILITY.

ATTEMPTING TO USE ULRICH'S INTERNATIONAL PERIODICALS DIRECTORY AS A GUIDE TO
THE AVAILABILITY OF MICROFORM OF MANY JOURNALS CAN BE MISLEADING. A QUICK
SURVEY OF 25 TITLES LISTED IN THE GUIDE TO MICROFORMS IN PRINT SHOWS THAT
ULRICH'S LISTED AVAILABILITY FOR ONLY 30.76%. THE SURVEY ALSO BROUGHT OUT THAT
SOME OUTSTANDING US GOVERNMENT TITLES ARE NOT LISTED IN ULRICH'S.
