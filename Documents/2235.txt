PUBLICATIONS ON COMPLEX PROBLEMS OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL COOPERATION OF
THE COMECON COUNTRIES.
     
NOTES SOME FEATURES OF THE PRESENT DEVELOPMENT STAGE OF SCIENTIFIC AND
TECHNICAL COOPERATION AMONG THE COMECON (EASTERN BLOC) COUNTRIES. THE
SUCCESSFUL SOLUTION OF THE PROBLEMS INVOLVED AND THE SUCCESSFUL COMPLETION OF
COOPERATIVE TASKS DEPEND TO A GREAT EXTENT ON GOOD INFORMATION PROVISION.
CONSIDERS THE PREPARATION OF PUBLICATIONS CONCERNING PROBLEMS AS PART OF THE
TOTAL SYSTEM OF INFORMATION PROVISION FOR THE COOPERATIVE VENTURE. DESCRIBES
THE TYPES OF PUBLICATION ISSUED AND THEIR CHARACTERISTICS.
