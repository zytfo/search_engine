BRITISH LIBRARY SUPPORTED RESEARCH IN AGRICULTURAL, BIOLOGICAL AND MEDICAL
INFORMATION.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 54TH ASLIB ANNUAL CONFERENCE, OXFORD UNIVERSITY, UK,
22-25 SEPT 81. DESCRIBES THE WORK OF THE BIOLOGICAL, AGRICULTURAL AND MEDICAL
INFORMATION REVIEW PANELS OF THE BRITISH LIBRARY RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT
DEPARTMENT AND EXAMINES THEIR IMPACT ON THE INFORMATION SCENE IN THESE SECTORS
WITHIN THE UK. THE PANELS CONSISTED OF EMINENT INFORMATION USERS BROUGHT
TOGETHER TO REVIEW THE RESEARCH ALREADY COMPLETED IN THEIR SUBJECT AREAS, TO
IDENTIFY THE GAPS AND TO PROPOSE WHAT RESEARCH COULD BE DONE TO FILL IN THESE
GAPS.
