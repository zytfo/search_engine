QUESTION MARKS SURROUNDING THE CURRENT BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL OF FOREIGN
PUBLISHED HUNGARICA LITERATURE.
     
DESCRIBES THE CURRENT BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL OF FOREIGN PUBLISHED HUNGARICA
LITERATURE IN BOTH HUNGARIAN AND FOREIGN LANGUAGES COMMENCING WITH HUNGARIKA
KULFOLDI FOLYOIRATSZEMLE (HUNGARICA FOREIGN PERIODICALS REVIEW) IN 1970. BOOKS
HAVE ONLY BEEN REGISTERED SINCE 1977. IN ITS PRESENT FORM, BIBLIOGRAPHIC
CONTROL UNDERTAKES MORE THAN IT CAN RELIABLY PERFORM. THE FUNCTION OF SUCH
BIBLIOGRAPHY IS NOT CLEAR AND USER DEMANDS DO NOT APPEAR TO BE MET.
