COMPARATIVE EVALUATION OF INFORMATION RESOURCES FOR CHEMICAL TOXICOLOGY
SEARCHING: A COLLABORATIVE STUDY.
     
A COLLABORATIVE PROJECT TO COMPARE AND EVALUATE INFORMATION RESOURCES IN
CHEMICAL TOXICOLOGY HAS BEEN CARRIED OUT BY 14 EUROPEAN ORGANISATIONS. THE
METHODOLOGY INVOLVED INDEPENDENT SEARCHING OF 'TEST QUERIES', WITH SUBSEQUENT
EXTENSIVE EVALUATION OF RESULTS AND FAILURE ANALYSIS. A SUMMARY OF THOSE
FINDINGS OF DIRECT RELEVANCE TO ON-LINE SEARCHING IS PRESENTED.
