RESOURCE NOTEBOOK ON ORGANIZATION.
     
FOCUSES ON IMPROVEMENT OF LIBRARY ORGANISATION. THE INTRODUCTION PROVIDES
BACKGROUND AND A REVIEW OF ANALYTICAL METHODS. THE FIRST SECTION PROVIDES
EXAMPLES OF DATA GATHERING TOOLS AND APPROACHES TO GATHERING INFORMATION. THE
SECOND GROUP OF DOCUMENTS ILLUSTRATES METHODS OF DESCRIBING AND ANALYSING
LIBRARY ORGANISATIONAL STRUCTURES AND RELATIONSHIPS, AND THE FINAL SECTION
CONTAINS EXAMPLES OF RECOMMENDATIONS FOR CHANGE AND IMPROVEMENT WHICH HAVE
BEEN MADE BY LIBRARIES. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
