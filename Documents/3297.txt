USER-LIBRARIAN NEGOTIATIONS AND INFORMATION SEARCH PROCEDURES IN PUBLIC
LIBRARIES.
     
DESCRIBES HYPOTHESES, EXPERIMENTAL SITUATIONS AND DESIGN, ANALYSING
METHODOLOGY, AND FINDINGS AND CONSIDERS THEORETICAL APPROACHES TO THE
USER-LIBRARIAN INFORMATION TRANSFER PROCESS. DESCRIBES A PROJECT ON THIS
TOPIC, UNDERTAKEN DURING 1976-79, CONCURRENTLY WITH THE TRAINING OF STUDENTS
IN INFORMATION RETRIEVAL AT THE ROYAL SCHOOL OF LIBRARIANSHIP, COPENHAGEN,
DENMARK.
