THE BIRTH OF A NATION, MICROFICHE, AND FILM SCHOLARSHIP.
     
DESCRIBES THE USE OF MICROFICHE FOR THE STORING OF INDIVIDUAL MOTION PICTURE
FRAMES-A SORT OF 'VISUAL SCORE' OF THE FILM-AND THE AUTHOR'S WORK ON BIRTH OF
A NATION: A FORMAL SHOT-BY-SHOT ANALYSIS TOGETHER WITH MICROFICHE, THE 1ST
OFFERING IN RESEARCH PUBLICATIONS, INC.'S CINEMA EDITIONS ON MICROFICHE
(CINE/FICHE). OUTLINES HOW SUCH PUBLICATIONS WILL AID THE STUDY OF FILMS AND
DESCRIBES THE STRUCTURE OF THE PUBLICATION'S HARD COPY TEXT COMPONENT.
