EXPERIMENTAL USE OF ON-LINE TERMINALS IN PUBLIC LIBRARIES. THE ESTABLISHMENT
OF AN ON-LINE INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SERVICE IN BEDFORDSHIRE, JAN 80-SEPT 81.

INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SERVICE BASED ON LUTON CENTRAL REFERENCE LIBRARY. DEALS
WITH THE SETTING UP AND RUNNING OF THE SERVICE WITH EMPHASIS ON STAFF
TRAINING, INTERNAL AND EXTERNAL PROMOTION/PUBLICITY, FINANCE AND OTHER
PROBLEMS.
