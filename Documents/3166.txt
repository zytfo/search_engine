TOWARDS PROBLEM-ORIENTED INFORMATION SYSTEM' A NEW PERSPECTIVE FOR INFORMATION
ORGANISATION IN U.S.
     
DISCUSSES A RECENT REPORT PUBLISHED IN THE USA THAT SUGGESTS A NEW PERSPECTIVE
FOR ORGANISING INFORMATION SYSTEMS FOR PROBLEM-SOLVING. THE ORGANISING
PRINCIPLE IS SCIENCE, TECHNICAL AND SOCIETAL INFORMATION (STSI) FOR THIS
PURPOSE. EXAMINES THE RELEVANCE OF THIS STUDY TO THE SITUATION OBTAINING IN
INDIA.
