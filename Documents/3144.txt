SUBJECT SOURCE ANALYSIS' ACCESS CHARACTERISTICS OF OBSCENITY LAW RESEARCH
MATERIALS.
     
ILLUSTRATES, THROUGH THE EXPLORATION OF ONE SUBJECT AREA, THE STRENGTHS AND
FLAWS OF SOME OF THE STANDARD TOOLS IN LEGAL RESEARCH. WHILST WORKING THROUGH
THE SUBJECT OF OBSCENITY THROWS LIGHT ON ONLY ONE SUBJECT SPECIALITY, IT
REVEALS PARADIGMS APPLICABLE TO ALL RESEARCH ENDEAVOURS.
