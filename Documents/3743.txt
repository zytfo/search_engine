TH CONFERENCE OF THE DIRECTORS OF THE STATE LIBRARIES OF THE UNION REPUBLICS.R
     
EPORTS ON PROCEEDINGS OF THE 9TH ALL-UNION CONFERENCE OF THE DIRECTORS OF THE
STATE LIBRARIES OF THE UNION REPUBLICS WHO MET TO DISCUSS THE ROLE OF
LIBRARIES IN THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE COMMUNIST PARTY CENTRAL COMMITTEE'S
RESOLUTION 'ON FURTHER IMPROVEMENT OF THE IDEOLOGICAL AND POLITICAL EDUCATION
WORK AND THE CURRENT PROBLEMS OF INFORMATION AND REFERENCE WORK IN THE STATE
LIBRARIES'. PAPERS CONCENTRATED MAINLY ON THE QUESTION OF THE IMPROVEMENT OF
EFFECTIVENESS OF INFORMATION AND REFERENCE SERVICES PROVIDED BY THE STATE AND
ON THE IMPORTANCE OF INFORMATION SERVICES FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF NATIONAL
AGRICULTURE.
