THE QUESTION OF THE VALUE OF INFORMATION IS PARTIALLY ADDRESSED IN THIS
REPORT, BUT A DEFINITIVE ANSWER IS NOT FOUND. THE ATTITUDE OF CLASSIFIED USERS
IS THAT THE INFORMATION THEY RECEIVE IS WELL WORTH ITS PRESENT COSTS. FOR THIS
REASON, A COMPLETELY UNCLASSIFIED ON-LINE SYSTEM IS NOT RECOMMENDED AS A
RESULT OF THIS STUDY.
