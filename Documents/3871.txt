APPLYING AACR2 IN PAPUA NEW GUINEA LIBRARIES.
     
REVIEWS SOME OF THE DEBATE OF THE PAST FEW YEARS CONCERNING THE ANGLO-AMERICAN
CATALOGUING RULES (2ND EDITION); EXPLAINS SOME OF THE NEW RELATED TOOLS THAT
HAVE BEEN INTRODUCED AND ACCEPTED IN LIBRARIES THROUGHOUT THE ENGLISH-SPEAKING
WORLD; STRESSES THE IMPORTANCE OF AACR 2 FOR PAPUA NEW GUINEAN LIBRARIANS AND
LIBRARY USERS; AND URGES ALL LIBRARIES (ESPECIALLY THOSE WITH LARGE AND VERY
SPECIALISED COLLECTIONS) TO ADOPT AACR 2.
