STATE OF THE ART IN LIBRARY TRAINING AND EXTENSION TRAINING OF LIBRARIANS.
     
AN ABRIDGED VERSION OF THE PROPOSALS PREPARED BY A WORKING COMMITTEE OF THE
HUNGARIAN COUNCIL OF LIBRARIES RELATING TO THE CONTENT, ORGANISATIONAL AND
QUANTITATIVE DEVELOPMENT OF LIBRARY TRAINING, WHICH IN THE PAST HAS NOT KEPT
PACE WITH THE INCREASE IN STAFF NUMBERS WORKING IN THE FIELD NOR WITH THE
DEVELOPMENT AND GROWTH OF KNOWLEDGE NECESSARY FOR BOTH THE LIBRARIAN AND THE
INFORMATION SPECIALIST.
