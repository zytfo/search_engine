BUSINESS PROGRAMMING' LIBRARY AND GOVERNMENT COOPERATION PAYS DIVIDENDS.
     
SINCE 1975, KITCHENER PUBLIC LIBRARY IN ONTARIO HAS BEEN RUNNING A MARKETING
CAMPAIGN TO INCREASE THE AWARENESS OF BUSINESSMEN ABOUT THE WIDE RANGE OF
UP-TO-DATE INFORMATION AVAILABLE THROUGH THE LIBRARY. PROGRAMMING HAS BEEN A
KEY ELEMENT IN THE GROWTH OF THE LIBRARY'S BUSINESS SERVICE. DESCRIBES THE
PROGRAMMES ON PATENTS AND COPYRIGHT, STARTING A NEW BUSINESS, AND
FRANCHISING-ALL WERE DEVELOPED IN RESPONSE TO PUBLIC INTEREST AND WITH THE
COOPERATION OF PROVINCIAL AND FEDERAL GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS.
