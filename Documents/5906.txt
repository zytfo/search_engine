LC CLASSIFICATION KEY' A COMPARISON OF CLASSES A-P WITH THEIR Z COUNTERPARTS.

THE BIBLIOGRAPHY SECTION (Z CLASS) OF THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS SYSTEM IS OFTEN
CONFUSING TO REFERENCE LIBRARIANS AND TO USERS. A KEY HAS BEEN PREPARED
SHOWING A COMPARISON OF THE MAIN CLASSES AND SUBCLASSES FOR A TO P WITH THEIR
CORRESPONDING Z NUMBERS. THE METHOD FOR PREPARING THIS KEY AND THE PROBLEMS
ENCOUNTERED ARE EXPLAINED.
