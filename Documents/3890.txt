THESAURUS FOR URBAN ROADS.
     
THE THESAURUS WAS CONSTRUCTED FOR USE IN THE LIBRARY OF THE UNIVERSITY OF SAO
PAULO FACULTY OF ARCHITECTURE AND URBAN STUDIES. IN A FULL INTRODUCTION, THE
TREATMENT OF TERMINOLOGY AND THE STRUCTURAL BASIS ARE DISCUSSED, WITH NOTES ON
THE STYLE OF PRESENTATION. THE THESAURUS ITSELF IS GIVEN IN FULL, IN FIVE
SECTIONS' A HIERARCHICAL PLAN, ALPHABETICAL LIST OF TERMS, SYSTEMATIC LIST OF
DESCRIPTORS, HIERARCHICAL INDEX OF DESCRIPTORS, AND GLOSSARY.
