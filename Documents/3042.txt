COMPARATIVE STUDY OF LIBRARY SCIENCE EDUCATION IN THE UNITED STATES AND INDIA.
     
MAKES A CRITICAL STUDY OF LIBRARY SCIENCE EDUCATION IN INDIA AND THE USA.
REVEALS THAT THE COURSE CONTENT OF LIBRARY SCIENCE IN US LIBRARY SCHOOLS IS
COMPARABLE TO THAT OF INDIAN LIBRARY SCHOOLS. REVIEWS AND COMPARES THE VARIOUS
COURSES THAT ARE OFFERED IN THE 2 COUNTRIES. ANALYSES THE ROLES OF THE
AMERICAN LIBRARY ASSOCIATION AND THE UNIVERSITY GRANTS COMMISSION IN PROMOTING
LIBRARY SCIENCE EDUCATION IN THE US AND INDIA RESPECTIVELY.
