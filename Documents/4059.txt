WILLIAM C. MILNER AND MARITIME LIBRARIES.
     
DESCRIBES THE FORMATIVE YEARS OF CANADA'S MARITIME LIBRARY ASSOCIATION AND ITS
EFFORTS TO ORGANISE PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THE MARITIME STATES 1918-24.
CONCENTRATES ON THE WORK OF ITS 2ND PRESIDENT WILLIAM C. MILNER, WHO
ULTIMATELY FAILED TO ESTABLISH LIBRARIES IN NOVA SCOTIA OR NEW BRUNSWICK.
REASONS FOR THE FAILURE WERE ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL CIRCUMSTANCES THOUGH SOME
REASONS WERE DUE TO MILNER HIMSELF.
