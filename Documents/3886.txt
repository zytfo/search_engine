COUPLING OF COMPUTER PROCESSED INDEXES TO A PHOTOCOMPOSING UNIT TO IMPROVE THE
READABILITY.
     
DESCRIBES THE DETAILS OF A PROJECT UNDERTAKEN TO EXPLORE THE POSSIBILITY OF
IMPROVING THE READABILITY OF A COMPUTER ASSISTED INDEX (PRESENTLY BROUGHT OUT
BY A LINE PRINTER) BY TAKING THE OUTPUT ONTO A PAPER TAPE IN A COMPATIBLE
MANNER THAT CAN BE FED TO A FILM SETTER DIRECTLY TO PROVIDE LOWER-CASE LETTERS
AND ITALIC FOUNTS, WITH A FLEXIBILITY OF VARYING THE POINT SIZE.
