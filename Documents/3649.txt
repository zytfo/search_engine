THE WORKING PARTY ON UNIVERSITY ARCHIVES IN QUEBEC (QUEBEC CONFERENCE OF
UNIVERSITY RECTORS AND PRINCIPALS).
     
A PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 CONGRESS OF THE ASSOCIATION OF CANADIAN
ARCHIVISTS, BY A MEMBER OF THE WORKING PARTY ON UNIVERSITY ARCHIVES IN QUEBEC,
ONE OF SEVERAL SPECIALIST COMMITTEES CREATED BY CREPUQ. DESCRIBES HOW THE
GROUP CAME INTO BEING IN 1977, AND ITS INITIAL MANDATE. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF
PARTICIPANT REPRESENTATION, AND ACTIVITIES TO DATE AT 12 MEETINGS. TOPICS
COVERED ARE SUMMARISED, WITH SPECIAL REFERENCE TO THE LAW ON PHOTOGRAPHS AND
RECORDS MANAGEMENT. IN CONCLUSION THE ADVANTAGES OF THE GROUP AS COMPARED TO A
PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATION ARE DISCUSSED.
