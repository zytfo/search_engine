DENMARK'S SCHOOL LIBRARY ASSOCIATION BOOKRACK AWARD 1981.
     
THE BOOKRACK AWARD FOR 1981 WAS AWARDED TO AASE BREDSDORFF, A FORMER INSPECTOR
OF LIBRARIES FOR HER OUTSTANDING WORK IN MAKING LITERATURE ACCESSIBLE TO
CHILDREN AND IN PROMOTING THE ASSOCIATION'S AIM OF PROVIDING CHILDREN AND
YOUNG PEOPLE WITH ACCESS TO EXPERIENCE AND INFORMATION THROUGH LIBRARIES. THE
PERSON AWARDED THE BOOKRACK HOLDS IT FOR THE YEAR AND RECEIVES A COPY OF THE
BOOK NOMINATED, WHICH FOR 1981 WAS 2STEMPLERNE1 BY JOHN NEHM.
