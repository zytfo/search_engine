EXPERIENCES OF THE REGIONAL CENTRE OF SCIENTIFIC, TECHNICAL AND ECONOMIC
INFORMATION IN GDANSK.
     
PRESENTS A BRIEF OUTLINE OF THE FUNCTION OF THE REGIONAL (VOIVODSHIP)
INFORMATION CENTRES WITHIN POLAND'S STATE INFORMATION SYSTEM, SINTO (SYSTEM
INFORMACJI NAUKOWEJ, TECHNICZNEJ I ORGANIZACYJNEJ); DESCRIBES THE WORK OF SUCH
CENTRES, WITH REFERENCE TO THE CENTRE IN GDANSK; AND LISTS THE FORMS AND
METHODS OF INFORMATION SERVICES USED IN THEIR WORK.
