PREVENTIVE CONSERVATION FOR MAP COLLECTIONS.
     
THE PHYSICAL CARE OF MAPS AND PLANS IS A PROBLEM FOR MANY PERSONS USED TO
WORKING WITH MORE TRADITIONAL FORMS OF LIBRARY AND ARCHIVAL MATERIALS. THE MAP
CURATOR, AS WELL AS BEING FAMILIAR WITH BROAD CONSERVATION MEASURES SUCH AS
PROPER ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS, NEEDS TO DEVELOP MORE SPECIALISED KNOWLEDGE
OF STORAGE POSSIBILITIES AND TECHNIQUES FOR HANDLING OVERSIZED MATERIAL.
CONSERVATION METHODS THAT HAVE PROVEN USEFUL IN MAP COLLECTIONS AND GUIDELINES
FOR RESEARCHERS AND STAFF ARE DESCRIBED.
