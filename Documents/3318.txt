AN INTERDEPARTMENTAL ALLOCATION FORMULA FOR THE MAXIMIZATION OF USE OF LIBRARY
MATERIALS.
     
DEVELOPS A MATHEMATICAL FORMALISATION OF THE PROBLEMS OF MAXIMISING USE OF
LIBRARY MATERIALS IN TERMS OF THE IDEAS DEVELOPED BY T.J. PIERCE AND W.E.
MCGRATH (78/2422) IN THEIR WORK OF ALLOCATION, AND SOLVES THIS PROBLEM TO
PRODUCE THE ALLOCATION FORMULA THAT MAXIMISES THE USE OF MATERIALS. BRIEFLY
DISCUSSES THE FEASIBILITY OF APPLYING THE FORMULA.
