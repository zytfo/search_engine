ANALYSIS OF INFORMATION FLOW AND OBSOLESCENCE IN THE LITERATURE OF BIOLOGICAL
MEMBRANES.
     
SURVEYS INFORMATION TRANSFER AND OBSOLESCENCE IN THE LITERATURE OF
MEDICO-BIOLOGICAL RESEARCH ON MEMBRANES IN THE PERIOD 1962-79. A STATISTICAL
ANALYSIS OF LITERATURE ON INTERNATIONAL RESEARCH RELYING UPON THE 2INDEX
MEDICUS 1HAS SHOWN THAT THE 1ST PROGRESSIVE STAGE OF RESEARCH ON MEMBRANES
FALLS BETWEEN 1966 AND 1972. AFTER A RELATIVE STAGNATION IN 1972-75
INTERNATIONAL LITERATURE SHOWS DEVELOPMENT TRENDS AGAIN. DETAILED PROFESSIONAL
ANALYSIS INDICATES DYNAMIC DEVELOPMENT OF SOME BASIC RESEARCH FIELDS (E.G.
ARTIFICIAL MEMBRANES) FROM 1972 ON.
