THE HOME TV AS AN INFORMATION TERMINAL-THE AUSTRALIAN SCENE.
     
DISCUSSES THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF AUSTRALIA'S INVOLVEMENT IN A MARKET TRIAL OF
AN INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION NETWORK USING PRESTEL. ALSO LOOKS AT A PILOT
PROJECT OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY AND THE STATE LIBRARY OF NEW SOUTH WALES TO
PROVIDE DATA FOR ATN 7'S TELETEXT SYSTEM SEVENTEXT REGARDING THE SERVICES THEY
OFFER.
