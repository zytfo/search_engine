EXISTING COLLECTIONS FOR HANDICAPPED USERS.
     
RESULTS OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF AUSTRALIA 1978 SURVEY 2NATIONAL SERVICES
FOR HANDICAPPED PEOPLE1 ARE ANALYSED. THE SURVEY FOUND THAT SOME 70% OF PUBLIC
LIBRARIES PROVIDED SPECIAL COLLECTIONS IN PRINT, AUDIO AND OTHER FORMS. THE
RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE WORKING PARTY ON LIBRARY SERVICES FOR THE HANDICAPPED
ARE DESCRIBED, AND THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THOSE RECOMMENDATIONS IS DISCUSSED.
