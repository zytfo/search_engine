THE EFFECT OF AACR2 ON SSAL.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA CATALOGUERS'
SECTION NATIONAL CATALOGUING SEMINAR, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. COMMENTS
ON THE CHANGES IN SSAL (SCIENTIFIC SERIALS IN AUSTRALIAN LIBRARIES) AS A
RESULT OF IMPLEMENTING THE ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING RULES (2ND EDITION) IN
1981. IT IS HOPED TO KEEP THE 2SSAL SUPPLEMENT1 COMPLETELY CONSISTENT AS
REGARDS ENTRY POINT. THE MINOR CHANGES IN FORMS OF EXPRESSION SHOULD NOT
AFFECT CONTRIBUTORS OR USERS.
