GENERAL ASPECTS OF TECHNICAL AND SCIENTIFIC INFORMATION IN RESEARCH WORKS DONE
IN HEALTH INSTITUTIONS.
     
IN THE BANSKA BYSTRICA COUNTRY HEALTH INSTITUTE OF SLOVAKIA LIBRARY AND
INFORMATION ACTIVITIES HAVE BEEN TAKING PLACE FOR YEARS. WISHING TO IMPROVE
THEIR SERVICES A SURVEY BY QUESTIONNAIRE WAS MADE TO REVEAL USER DEMAND ON
LITERATURE. FROM THE 250 QUESTIONNAIRES SENT OUT, 60 WERE ANSWERED. BESIDES
USUAL ANSWERS IT DESERVES MENTION THAT 31.7% OF RESPONDENTS VOTED FOR BOOKS,
43.3% FOR PERIODICALS. USUALLY THERE IS A BIGGER SHIFT TOWARD PERIODICALS THAN
IN THIS SURVEY.
