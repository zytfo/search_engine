SOME ARE MORE EQUAL..
     
EDITED VERSION OF AN ADDRESS TO THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA VICTORIAN
SCHOOL LIBRARIES SECTIONAL GROUP, MELBOURNE, 10 APR 81. RECORDS THE AUTHOR'S
IMPRESSIONS OF SCHOOL LIBRARIES THROUGHOUT ENGLAND AND WALES, GAINED DURING A
RECENT VISIT. DESCRIBES THE BLEAK PROSPECTS FACING THE SCHOOL LIBRARIANS AND
WARNS THAT AUSTRALIAN STANDARDS IN THIS AREA MAY WELL BE IN DANGER OF EROSION.
