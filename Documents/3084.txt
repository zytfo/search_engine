QUO VADIS? FUNDAMENTAL REFLECTIONS ON THE FUTURE OF ACADEMIC LIBRARIES.
     
REVIEWS SOME FACTORS WHICH ARE LIKELY TO AFFECT THE ROLE AND DEVELOPMENT OF
ACADEMIC AND RESEARCH LIBRARIES DURING THE IMMEDIATE FUTURE. DISCUSSES' THE
IMPROVEMENT OF THE LIBRARY SERVICE IN THE CONTEXT OF ACCESS TO INFORMATION AND
MATERIALS; RESOURCE SHARING THROUGH INFORMAL COOPERATION AND THROUGH NETWORKS;
THE INCREASING IMPORTANCE OF DOCUMENT DELIVERY; AND THE EFFECTS OF NEW
DEVELOPMENTS ON COLLECTION GROWTH AND MANAGEMENT AND ON THE RELATIONSHIP OF
THE ACADEMIC LIBRARY WITH THE PARENT BODY AND WITH OTHER LIBRARIES. MAKES
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR' INCREASED ACCESS TO LIBRARY HOLDINGS THROUGH DISTRIBUTED
NETWORKS; THE IMPROVEMENT OF INTERLIBRARY LOAN; THE IMPLEMENTATION OF
COOPERATIVE COLLECTION MANAGEMENT; A REAPPRAISAL OF COLLECTION EVALUATION
CRITERIA; AND THE APPROPRIATE UTILISATION OF MANAGEMENT SKILLS AND TECHNOLOGY.
