THE ATTORNEY.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO INFORMATION SEEKING PATTERNS OF SELECTED
PROFESSIONALS. RELATES THE SOURCES USED IN ACQUIRING INFORMATION AND THE
IMPORTANCE OF EACH TYPE OF SOURCE TO AN ATTORNEY, ALBERT RAINES. THE
INFORMATION NEEDS WERE GROUPED IN 4 BROAD CATEGORIES' (1) CURRENT AWARENESS;
(2) PREPARATION OF DOCUMENTS; (3) DOMESTIC RELATIONS CASES; AND (4) PERSONAL
INJURY CASES.
