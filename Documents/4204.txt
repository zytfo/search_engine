LEVELS OF OUTPUT RELATED TO COST OF OPERATION OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL
LIBRARIES: PART 2. A CAPACITY MODEL OF THE AVERAGE COST FORMULA.
     
AN ECONOMETRIC ANALYSIS HAS BEEN MADE OF COST AND SERVICE DATA REPRESENTING 65
SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNICAL LIBRARIES. THE DATA INCLUDE LIBRARIES IN A VARIETY OF
INSTITUTIONAL ENVIRONMENTS, RANGING IN OPERATING BUDGET FROM $20,000 TO
$2,000,000 PER YEAR. ALL THE DATA ARE WELL DESCRIBED BY A SINGLE FORMULA,
WHICH EXHIBITS ECONOMIES OF SCALE, AND FIXED RELATIVE UNIT COST OF THE VARIOUS
DIMENSIONS OF SERVICE. (FOR PART 1 SEE PREVIOUS ABSTRACT).
