PROJECT PROGRESS' A STUDY OF THE CANADIAN PUBLIC LIBRARIES.

THE FINAL REPORT OF A STUDY SPONSORED BY THE CANADIAN LIBRARY ASSOCIATION IN
ORDER TO PROVIDE PRACTICAL INFORMATION AS A BASE FOR PLANNING AND DECISIONS.
THIS INCLUDES BOTH SPECIFIC MATTERS OF INTERLIBRARY COOPERATION, COST-BENEFIT
ANALYSIS, PERSONNEL AND DEMOGRAPHY AND MORE GENERAL QUESTIONS OF SOCIAL,
POLITICAL AND TECHNOLOGICAL CHANGE.
