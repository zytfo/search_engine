ICSSR-SSDC' BIBLIOGRAPHICAL ACTIVITY IN THE SOCIAL SCIENCES IN INDIA.
     
DESCRIBES THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE INDIAN COUNCIL OF SOCIAL SCIENCE RESEARCH
AND ITS SOCIAL SCIENCE DOCUMENTATION CENTRE; EXAMINES THE CENTRE'S PROJECTS
(MAINLY CATALOGUES, INDEXES, AND BIBLIOGRAPHIES OF VARIOUS SOCIAL SCIENCE
DISCIPLINES); AND MENTIONS PLANS FOR THE FUTURE.
