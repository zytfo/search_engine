REPRESENTATION OF KNOWLEDGE IN A LEGAL INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEM.
     
THE LEGAL RESEARCH SYSTEM IS A KNOWLEDGE-BASED COMPUTER RETRIEVAL SYSTEM FOR
USE BY LAWYERS AND LEGAL ASSISTANTS AND CONTAINING INFORMATION ABOUT 200 COURT
DECISIONS AND 200 STATUTES CONCERNING US NEGOTIABLE INSTRUMENTS LAW (CHEQUES
AND PROMISSORY NOTES). 4 KINDS OF KNOWLEDGE ABOUT LEGAL CONCEPTS AND RELATED
COURT CASES ARE REPRESENTED-FUNCTIONAL, STRUCTURAL, SEMANTIC, AND FACTUAL.
DISCUSSES THE MOTIVATION FOR INCLUDING EACH KIND OF KNOWLEDGE. DESCRIBES THEIR
COMPUTER REPRESENTATION AND GIVES EXAMPLES OF THEIR USE. DESCRIBES HOW THE
SYSTEM'S STRUCTURE IS USED TO UNDERSTAND AND INTERPRET USER QUERIES.
