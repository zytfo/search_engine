LC'S CONCLUDING AACR2 INSTITUTE.
     
REPORTS ON AN INSTITUTE ON THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS'S INTERPRETATION AND
APPLICATION OF THE ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING RULES, 2ND EDITION, SAN
FRANCISCO, CALIFORNIA, JUNE 81. THIS WAS THE LAST OF 14 'ROADSHOWS' ON THE
SUBJECT DESIGNED AND HELD BY THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS THROUGHOUT THE USA. THE
INSTITUTE COMPRISED 2 GENERAL SESSIONS AND 6 GROUP SESSIONS COVERING SPECIFIC
CHAPTERS OF AACR 2.
