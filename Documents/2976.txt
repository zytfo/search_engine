POLAND' THE DAM BREAKS.
     
OUTLINES THE HISTORY OF SEVERE CENSORSHIP RESTRICTIONS IN POLAND AND EVENTS
LEADING TO THE NEW CENSORSHIP LAW WHICH CAME INTO FORCE ON 1 OCT 81. THIS LAW
GIVES PUBLISHERS AND WRITERS MORE FREEDOM AND THE RIGHT OF APPEAL AGAINST
CENSORSHIP DECISIONS. BRIEFLY DISCUSSES HOW THE LAW WILL AFFECT PUBLISHING.
