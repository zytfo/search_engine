EURONET-DIANE' HOW TO ACHIEVE FURTHER HARMONIZATION.
     
BASED UPON THE EXPERIENCE GAINED THROUGH OPERATION OF EURONET-DIANE, DESCRIBES
THE DIFFERENT HARMONISATION EFFORTS UNDERTAKEN AND PROPOSED IN ORDER TO MAKE
EURONET-DIANE LOOK LIKE ONE SYSTEM TO THE USER. IT DESCRIBES VARIOUS PROPOSALS
PUT FORWARD, BOTH ON THE TECHNICAL AND ON THE ADMINISTRATIVE SIDE, AND HOW FAR
THESE HAVE BEEN ACCEPTED AND IMPLEMENTED.
