INTERVIEW: ALFREDO AMERICO HAMAR.
     
INTERVIEW WITH ALFREDO AMERICO HAMAR, A BRAZILIAN PROFESSOR OF LIBRARY
SCIENCE, CHIEF LIBRARIAN AT THE UNIVERSITY OF SAO PAULO SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING,
AND COORDINATOR OF BICENGE (BIBLIOTECA COMPLEMENTAR DE ENGENHARIA), WHO
DESCRIBES THE ORIGIN AND OBJECTIVES OF THIS PROJECT, INTENDED TO DEVELOP AND
INTEGRATE DOCUMENTATION AND INFORMATION SERVICES IN ENGINEERING THROUGHOUT
BRAZIL. A COMMITTEE OF EXPERTS IS RESPONSIBLE FOR ITS DIRECTION, UNDER
GOVERNMENT SPONSORSHIP. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF THE PROPOSED STRUCTURE AND
OBJECTIVES, RESOURCES, AND PROGRESS IN IMPLEMENTATION TO DATE.
