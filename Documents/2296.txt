INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES FOR THE 1980'S' LASERS AND MICROPROCESSORS.
     
THIS DISCUSSION OF THE DEVELOPMENT AND APPLICATION OF LASERS AND
MICROPROCESSORS TO INFORMATION PROCESSING STRESSES LASER COMMUNICATION IN
RELATION TO CAPACITY, RELIABILITY, AND COST AND THE ADVANTAGES OF THIS
TECHNOLOGY TO REAL-TIME INFORMATION ACCESS AND INFORMATION STORAGE. THE
INCREASED CAPABILITIES OF MICROPROCESSORS ARE REVIEWED, AND A GENERAL
ASSESSMENT OF THE IMPACT OF THESE TECHNOLOGIES IS PROVIDED. REPORT NOT
AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
