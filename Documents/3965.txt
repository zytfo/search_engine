WHAT LIBRARIANS HAVE WANTED TO KNOW ABOUT REMARC AND WERE NOT AFRAID TO ASK.
     
CARROLLTON PRESS, INC., IS CREATING A NEW DATA BASE (REMARC) FROM AN ESTIMATED
5.2 MILLION NON-MARC LIBRARY OF CONGRESS (LC) CATALOGUE RECORDS. ANSWERS SOME
QUESTIONS PUT BY LIBRARIANS CONCERNING ACQUISITION OF REMARC RECORDS,
RELATIONSHIP OF THE RECORDS WITH LC FILES, COSTS, AND WHY CARROLLTON EMBARKED
ON THE REMARC DATABASE PROJECT.
