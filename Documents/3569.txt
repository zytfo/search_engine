INFORMATION PRODUCTS, FROM 15 MAIN ABSTRACT JOURNALS IN 1939 TO 27 MAIN
ABSTRACT JOURNALS PLUS 16 SPECIALIST ABSTRACT JOURNALS IN 1981 IS REPORTED.
