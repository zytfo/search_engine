LIBRARY TRAINING BASED ON INFORMATICS.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO A THEMATIC ISSUE ON LIBRARY TRAINING. INVESTIGATES THE
THEORETICAL FOUNDATION OF UNIVERSITY LIBRARY TRAINING AND PROPOSES TRAINING
BASED ON INFORMATICS. PRESENTS THE MOST IMPORTANT THEMES OF INFORMATICS AS
SHANNON'S COMMUNICATION THEORY; SCIENTOMETRICS; DOCUMENT TYPOLOGY; ORGANISING
LIBRARY WORK; INFORMATION MANAGEMENT TECHNOLOGY; AND INFORMATION ORGANISATION.
