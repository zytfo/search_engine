THE IMPACT OF AACR2 ON SERIALS CATALOGUING IN THE NATIONAL LIBRARY.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA CATALOGUERS'
SECTION NATIONAL CATALOGUING SEMINAR, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. A BRIEF
ACCOUNT OF THE EFFECT OF IMPLEMENTING THE ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING RULES,
2ND EDITION, IN THOSE SECTIONS OF THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF AUSTRALIA PRODUCING
BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATA FOR SERIALS AND ITS IMPACT ON THE 2AUSTRALIAN NATIONAL
BIBLIOGRAPHY1, SALSSAH (SERIALS IN AUSTRALIAN LIBRARIES' SOCIAL SCIENCES AND
HUMANITIES), AND THE AUSTRALIAN MARC RECORD SERVICE.
