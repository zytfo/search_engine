CAS77 AND CAS7276' A REVIEW.
     
DESCRIBES THE CONTENT, ORGANISATION, SPECIFICATIONS, AND METHODS OF USE OF THE
CAS77 AND CAS7276 ON-LINE FILES OF WORLDWIDE CHEMICAL LITERATURE, PRODUCED BY
CHEMICAL ABSTRACTS SERVICE AND AVAILABLE FROM SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION
(SDC). THE SCOPE OF THE DATA BASES, THEIR UNIT RECORD, DATA ELEMENTS, MODES OF
SUBJECT SEARCHING, AVAILABLE USER AIDS, AND CHARGES TO USERS ARE DETAILED.
TABLES IDENTIFY DIRECTLY SEARCHABLE DATA ELEMENTS PROVIDED BY SDC AND DATA
BASE SPECIFICATIONS FOR THE CAS77 FILE. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
