NEW TECHNIQUES IN THE TEACHING OF ONLINE SEARCHING. AN INSTITUTE FOR LIBRARY
EDUCATORS (SEATTLE, WASHINGTON, MARCH 20-24, 1978). EXHIBITS AND SUMMARIES OF
PRESENTATIONS.
     
USING DATA BASES OF THE DIALOG SYSTEM NEW TEACHING TECHNIQUES AND MATERIALS
ARE PRESENTED TO AN AUDIENCE OF LIBRARY EDUCATORS. INCLUDED ARE SEARCH
DEMONSTRATIONS, TEACHING APPROACHES, SAMPLE EXERCISES AND INSTRUCTIONAL
PROJECTS, MULTI-DATE BASE SEARCHING METHODS, UTILISATION OF SEARCH AIDS AND
OFF-LINE RESOURCES, THE CONDUCT OF PRESEARCH INTERVIEWS, INFORMATION SEARCH
TACTICS, AND EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMME PLANING. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
