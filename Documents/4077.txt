WORKSHOP ON NEW TECHNOLOGY AND LIBRARY/INFORMATION SCIENCE EDUCATION,
NEWCASTLE UPON TYNE POLYTECHNIC, 13-16 APRIL 1981.
     
SESSIONS COVERED THE ASPECTS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY TO BE INCLUDED IN THE
SYLLABUS, PRESTEL AND ITS APPLICATIONS IN PUBLIC LIBRARIES, STAFF DEVELOPMENT
AND COURSE ORGANISATION AND EXPECTATIONS OF THE LIBRARY PROFESSION. REVIEWS
THE WORK OF THE MICROELECTRONICS EDUCATION PROGRAMME AND ITS CENTRE IN
PROVIDING FOR THE FAMILIARISATION OF SCHOOLCHILDREN WITH COMPUTER TECHNIQUES.
