ASPECTS OF CO-OPERATION, FUNDING PRODUCTION, COPYRIGHT, TALENT-SHARING UNION
LISTS, INTERLENDING.
     
ARGUES THE CASE FOR COOPERATION BETWEEN PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND OTHER
INSTITUTIONS SPECIFICALLY SERVING THE PRINT-HANDICAPPED. A SYSTEM FOR
PRODUCING AUDIO CASSETTES IS PROPOSED AS AN EXAMPLE, PRESUMING ONLY THE
EXISTING RESOURCES AND FUNDING, WITHOUT RELYING ON GOVERNMENT GRANTS.
