A LOW COST TERMINAL FOR THE 1980'S' PROJECT GREEN THUMB.
     
THE USA GOVERNMENT IS FUNDING A VIDEOTEXT FARM INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEM
(FIRS) USING INEXPENSIVE HOME TERMINALS. A DETAILED ACCOUNT IS GIVEN OF THE
HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE INVOLVED. THE FUNCTIONS OF THE STATE AND THE COUNTY
COMPUTERS ARE EXPLAINED. THESE ARE ACCESSED THROUGH A TERMINAL OR GREEN THUMB
BOX. THE ORGANISATION OF DATA IN THE SYSTEM IS DESCRIBED. THE FIRS SYSTEM IS
CURRENTLY BEING TESTED AND PRELIMINARY DATA INDICATES THAT THE SYSTEM IS BEING
USED ON A DAILY BASIS.
