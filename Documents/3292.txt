LITERATURE REQUIREMENTS OF RESEARCH WORK.
     
INFORMATION ON RESEARCH TOPICS MUST BE UP TO DATE. FOR TOPIC RETRIEVAL, A
RESEARCHER CAN MAKE A PERSONAL CARD INDEX OF JOURNAL ARTICLES, OR CHECK
2CURRENT CONTENTS1. ALTERNATIVELY HE MAY RELY ON 1 OR 2 COMPREHENSIVE WORKS
AND THEIR REFERENCES. HOWEVER, PAPERS IN 2PHARMACOLOGICAL REVIEW1 OR
2PHYSIOLOGICAL REVIEW1, FROM ENGLISH-SPEAKING COUNTRIES, DO NOT REPRESENT A
CROSS-SECTION OF THE IMPORTANT PUBLICATIONS ON A PARTICULAR TOPIC. THE
LITERATURE BASE SHOULD BE ENLARGED.
