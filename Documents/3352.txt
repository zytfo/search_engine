ALTERNATIVES FOR LIBRARY CATALOGUES' TOOLS FOR CATALOGUE PLANNING.
     
MANY LIBRARIES ARE EXAMINING THE CURRENT STATE OF THEIR CATALOGUES AND THE
IMPACT OF VARIOUS ALTERNATIVES FOR THEIR FUTURE. PRESENTS 2 TOOLS WHICH MAY BE
USEFUL IN SUCH STUDIES' (1) A CHECKLIST OF THE WIDE VARIETY OF THINGS THAT
SHOULD BE TAKEN INTO ACCOUNT WHEN LOOKING AT THE CATALOGUE AS A SYSTEM AND AS
THE RESULT OF A COMPLEX SET OF ONGOING ACTIVITIES AND (2) A TABLE SETTING OUT
SOME POSSIBLE OPTIONS THAT COULD BE CONSIDERED IN THE FACE OF THE
IMPLEMENTATION OF AACR2 AND SOME CRITERIA AGAINST WHICH TO MEASURE THESE
OPTIONS.
