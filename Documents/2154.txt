THE DECORATIVE ARTS PHOTOGRAPHIC COLLECTION' AN AID IN IDENTIFICATION AND
COMPARATIVE STUDY.
     
DESCRIBES THE WORK OF THE DECORATIVE ARTS PHOTOGRAPHIC COLLECTION OF THE
WINTERTHUR MUSEUM, DELAWARE, FOUNDED TO SUPPORT THE EARLY AMERICAN CULTURE
GRADUATE PROGRAMME BY STORING PHOTOGRAPHS OF US DECORATIVE ARTIFACTS PRODUCED
PRIOR TO WORLD WAR I.
