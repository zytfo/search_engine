THE PUBLIC LIBRARY IN A FREE ENTERPRISE SOCIETY.
     
STATES THAT THE FREE PUBLIC LIBRARY IN ITS PRESENT FORM IS A UNIQUELY US
INSTITUTION, REFLECTING THE NATION'S COMMITMENT TO INDIVIDUAL INITIATIVE AND
FREE ENTERPRISE-READY ACCESS TO BOOKS FOR SELF-HELP AND SELF-IMPROVEMENT HAS
BEEN PERCEIVED AS ESSENTIAL TO BUILDING CHARACTER AND SELF-RELIANCE FOR
GENERATIONS. TRACES THE VITAL ROLE OF PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THE LIVES OF LEADING
US INVENTORS, BUSINESSMEN, AND INDUSTRIES AND CALLS FOR SUPPORT FOR LIBRARIES
(THROUGH THE PROPOSED NATIONAL LIBRARY AND INFORMATION SERVICES ACT) IN THEIR
PRESENT FINANCIALLY-STRAINED CIRCUMSTANCES.
