LIBRARY SKILLS.
     
THIS WORKBOOK DESIGNED FOR A BASIC LIBRARY SKILLS COURSE AT THE UNIVERSITY OF
MISSOURI ALSO SERVES AS A COMPONENT FOR OTHER LIBRARY USE AND RESEARCH
COURSES. IT IS STRUCTURED TO INTRODUCE THE STUDENT TO THE EFFICIENT AND
EFFECTIVE USE OF THE LIBRARIES AND THEIR RESOURCES AND TO PROVIDE ELEMENTARY
INSTRUCTION IN THE RESEARCH PROCESS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
