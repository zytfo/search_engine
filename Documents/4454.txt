JEAN BLETON.
     
BRIEF ACCOUNT OF THE WORK OF THE FRENCH LIBRARY ARCHITECTURE SPECIALIST JEAN
BLETON WHOSE PROFESSIONAL LIFE STARTED IN 1943 AT THE BIBLIOTHEQUE NATIONALE.
FROM 1945-72 HE WORKED IN THE DIRECTORATE FOR LIBRARIES AND TOOK PART IN 110
UNIVERSITY AND PUBLIC LIBRARY PROJECTS INCLUDING BOTH NEW BUILDINGS AND
RECONSTRUCTIONS. AS THE GENERAL LIBRARY INSPECTOR (FROM 1972) HE IS ALSO
RESPONSIBLE FOR PROJECTS IN AFRICA. HIS PUBLICATIONS AND HIS WORK IN IFLA ARE
REVIEWED AND A LIST OF HIS PUBLISHED WORKS APPENDED.
