CLASSIFICATION LITERATURE IN INDIA' ANALYSIS AND TREND.
     
AN ATTEMPT HAS BEEN MADE TO ANALYSE THE CLASSIFICATION LITERATURE PRODUCED IN
INDIA FROM 1932-80. SOME OF THE FACETS CONSIDERED ARE' TYPE OF LITERATURE
(PERIODICAL, CONFERENCE/SEMINAR, BOOK, THESIS); PERIODICALS PUBLISHING
CLASSIFICATION LITERATURE; PRODUCTIVE AUTHORS; YEARWISE DISTRIBUTION OF
CLASSIFICATION LITERATURE; AND SUBJECT-WISE ANALYSIS. THE 160 DEPTH SCHEDULES
PRODUCED BY RANGANATHAN AND HIS SCHOOL ARE LISTED IN CHRONOLOGICAL SEQUENCE.
SOME CONCLUSIONS ARE DRAWN BASED ON THE ANALYSIS.
