BIBLIOTHERAPY' LIBRARY SERVICE IN THE TREATMENT OF THE SICK.

AN INTRODUCTION TO BIBLIOTHERAPY-THE USE OF BOOKS AND RELATED MATERIALS IN THE
TREATMENT OF THE SICK. THROUGH SELECTED ACTIVITY INVOLVING PLANNED, CONDUCTED,
CONTROLLED READING, BIBLIOTHERAPY MAY BE USED TO DEAL WITH EMOTIONAL AND OTHER
PROBLEMS. DESCRIBES THE INSTITUTIONS IN WHICH BIBLIOTHERAPY MAY BE USED, AND
THE LIBRARIAN'S ROLE ON THE TREATMENT TEAM WITHIN SUCH AN INSTITUTION.
