LIBRARY USE EDUCATION' CURRENT PRACTICES AND TRENDS.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO A THEMATIC ISSUE ON LIBRARY USE INSTRUCTION. DESCRIBES AND
EVALUATES THE WORK OF THE LIBRARY ORIENTATION/INSTRUCTION EXCHANGE (LOEX)
OFFICE AT EASTERN MICHIGAN UNIVERSITY, WHICH FUNCTIONS AS A CLEARING HOUSE AND
CENTRAL EXCHANGE FOR LIBRARY USER INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIAL IN THE USA. INCLUDES
A NUMBER OF STATISTICS WHICH ILLUSTRATE THE VARIETY OF INSTRUCTIONAL
APPROACHES, METHODS, MATERIALS, AND PROJECTS USED IN A WIDE RANGE OF LIBRARY
INSTRUCTION PROGRAMMES IN 1979, AND COMPARES THEM WITH THE FIGURES AVAILABLE
FOR SUCH ACTIVITIES IN 1973. SUGGESTS AN OUTLINE PLAN OF ACTION FOR BASIC
LIBRARY INSTRUCTION.
