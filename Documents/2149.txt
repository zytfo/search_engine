INTRODUCTION TO REFERENCE SOURCES IN THE HEALTH SCIENCES.
     
WRITTEN WITH THE LIBRARY SCHOOL STUDENT IN MIND, DISCUSSES VARIOUS TYPES OF
BIBLIOGRAPHIC AND INFORMATION SOURCES AND THEIR USE IN HEALTH SCIENCES
REFERENCE WORK. SOME MAJOR SPECIALISED TOOLS HAVE BEEN INCLUDED, BUT THERE IS
NO ATTEMPT TO GO INTO SUBJECT SPECIALISATIONS IN GREAT DETAIL. THE EMPHASIS IS
ON US PUBLICATIONS AND LIBRARIES.
