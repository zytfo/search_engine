MYSTERIES OF THE DEEP' MODELS OF THE UNIVERSE OF KNOWLEDGE.
     
EVIDENCE OF THE USE, BY SCHOLARS AND OTHERS, OF MENTAL CONSTRUCTS OR MODELS OF
SPATIAL AND TEMPORAL RELATIONSHIPS IS PRESENTED TO ILLUSTRATE A NON-VERBAL
KNOWLEDGE STRUCTURE. THE RELATIONSHIP OF THIS KIND OF STRUCTURE TO EFFORTS TO
CONSTRUCT MODELS OF THE UNIVERSE OF KNOWLEDGE IS STATED, NOTING THE DANGER IN
SUCH MODELS.
