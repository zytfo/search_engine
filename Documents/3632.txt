ON THE AIR OR ON THE SHELF?.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO EDUCATIONAL BROADCASTING AND LIBRARIES.
EXAMINES WHAT CAN BE DONE TO ENSURE THE COMPLEMENTARY RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN
LIBRARIANS AND EDUCATIONAL BROADCASTERS, INCLUDING THE POSSIBILITY OF DEPOSIT
COLLECTIONS OF BBC EDUCATIONAL PUBLICATIONS IN DESIGNATED LIBRARIES ACROSS THE
COUNTRY, ORGANISATION OF STUDY GROUPS USING LIBRARY'S TELEVISIONS, AND THE USE
OF VIDEO AND PRESTEL. DESCRIBES THE WORK OF CAMDEN PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THIS
SPHERE.
