CONTINUING EVOLUTION OF ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING.
     
ADVANCES IN DIGITAL PREPRESS THAT HAVE OCCURRED DURING 1980-81 ARE REVIEWED,
AND THE CONTINUING PROGRESS IN ELECTRONIC PUBLISHING TECHNIQUES IS DESCRIBED.
AREAS IN WHICH INNOVATIONS ARE NOTED AND DISCUSSED INCLUDE ELECTROPHOTOGRAPHY,
RUN LENGTH, TYPOGRAPHY, SCANNING, FACSIMILE, NEWSPAPER ELECTRONIC PRODUCTION,
AND LASER PLATEMAKING.
