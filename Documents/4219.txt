SHOULD LIBRARIES BE UNIONISED?.
     
REPORT OF THE PROCEEDINGS AT A 1982 MEETING OF QUEBEC PUBLIC LIBRARIANS. THE
SPEECHES FOR AND AGAINST THE MOTION ARE SUMMARISED' THE MAIN TOPICS RAISED
WERE THE IMPORTANCE OF UNION SUPPORT IN NEGOTIATING SALARIES AND CONDITIONS OF
EMPLOYMENT; WAYS OF ORGANISING UNIONS TO SERVE DIVERSE INTERESTS; AND
CONSTRAINTS ON ADMINISTRATIVE FLEXIBILITY. POINTS RAISED IN THE ENSUING DEBATE
ARE SELECTIVELY REPORTED.
