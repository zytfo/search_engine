INTERLENDING IN THE 80'S' PROCEEDINGS OF A NATIONAL CONFERENCE HELD AT THE
UNIVERSITY OF QUEENSLAND, BRISBANE. 27TH-29TH AUGUST, 1980.
     
PROCEEDINGS OF THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA QUEENSLAND BRANCH AND
COLLEGE LIBRARIES SECTION, UNIVERSITY OF QUEENSLAND, BRISBANE, 27-29 AUG 80.
ITS AIM WAS TO FOCUS THE ATTENTION OF BOTH POLICY-MAKERS AND PRACTITIONERS ON
THE ROLE OF INTERLENDING IN THE PROVISION OF INFORMATION SERVICES DURING THE
NEXT FEW YEARS. THE PROGRAMME WAS DIRECTED 1ST AT THE CURRENT SITUATION WITH
REGARD TO PATTERNS OF INTERLENDING IN AUSTRALIA, AND 2ND, TO WAYS IN WHICH
FUTURE SERVICES MIGHT BE IMPROVED. FOR ABSTRACTS OF THE INDIVIDUAL PAPERS SEE
