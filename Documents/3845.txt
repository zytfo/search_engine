ADHESIVES FOR LIBRARY BINDING.
     
DISCUSSES THE DIFFERENT TYPES OF ADHESIVES WHICH ARE AVAILABLE TO BINDERS FOR
USE IN PUTTING THE SEQUENCE OF PRINTED PAGES TOGETHER TO FORM A BOOK.
DESCRIBES THE USE, AND APPROPRIATE APPLICATIONS OF' ANIMAL GLUE; STARCH
PASTES; SYNTHETIC RESIN EMULSIONS; AND HOT METAL ADHESIVES.
