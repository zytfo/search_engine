CHANGES AND EXCHANGES' AUSTRALIAN VIEWPOINTS.
     
COLLECTED PAPERS BY THE LIBRARIANS-IN-RESIDENCE FROM THE SCHOOL OF LIBRARY AND
INFORMATION STUDIES, PRESENTED BETWEEN 1978 AND 79. THE SUBJECT MATTER IS
DISPARATE, RANGING FROM THE AUSTRALIAN LIBRARY SCENE TO LIBRARY PROBLEMS IN A
PERIOD OF FINANCIAL STRINGENCY. ALSO INCLUDED IS THE PROBLEM OF PROFESSIONAL
KNOWLEDGE IN LIBRARIANSHIP BY W. BOYD RAYWARD, VISITING LECTURER TO THE
COLLEGE.
