PRIVATE VIDEOTEX SYSTEMS AND ACCESS VIA PUBLIC SERVICES.
     
WHILE THE INITIAL CONCEPT OF VIDEOTEX WAS THAT OF A HOME INFORMATION RETRIEVAL
SERVICE, IN PRACTICE VIDEOTEX SERVICES ARE OF MUCH INTEREST TO BUSINESS USERS.
PRIVATE VIDEOTEX SYSTEMS ARE BEING INTRODUCED TO COMPLEMENT PUBLIC SERVICES.
THEY MAY BE IMPLEMENTED IN SEVERAL DIFFERENT WAYS-ON DEDICATED COMPUTERS
ACCESSED VIA THE PUBLIC TELEPHONE OR DATA NETWORKS, OR VIA A GATEWAY FACILITY
ON A PUBLIC SERVICE. EXAMINES THESE APPROACHES, AND IN PARTICULAR THEIR
RELATIVE COSTS.
