PRINTED MATTER IN THE FACE OF NEW MEDIA.
     
THE EVOLUTION OF MEANS OF COMMUNICATION BASED ON THE USE OF TELEVISION
APPARATUS IS DISCUSSED, WITH PARTICULAR EMPHASIS ON SATELLITE TV, FIBRE
OPTICS, DIGITISATION OF SIGNALS, VIDEOTEX AND CABLE TV. THE IMPLICATIONS OF
THESE NEW TECHNOLOGIES FOR THE PRINTING INDUSTRY ARE ASSESSED. IN PARTICULAR,
AN ATTEMPT IS MADE TO IDENTIFY THOSE MARKETS WHICH ARE DIRECTLY THREATENED BY
ELECTRONIC MEDIA AND THOSE WHICH WILL BE ENHANCED AND EXPANDED.
