BOOK COLLECTOR, BIBLIOGRAPHER, AND BENEFACTOR OF LIBRARIES: SIR WILLIAM OSLER.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT LIBRARY HISTORY SEMINAR VI 'LIBRARIES & CULTURE', AUSTIN,
TEXAS, 19-22 MAR 80. PRESENTS SOME DETAILS OF WILLIAM OSLER (1849-1919), A
CANADIAN TEACHER AND PHYSICIAN, AS A BOOK COLLECTOR AND BENEFACTOR OF
LIBRARIES, AND FOCUSES ON OSLER AS A BIBLIOGRAPHER.
