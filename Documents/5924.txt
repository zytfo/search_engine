GATEWAY-AN ENHANCEMENT OF PRESTEL.

THE INTRODUCTION OF THE GATEWAY FACILITY IN MAR 82 WILL ENABLE USERS TO BE
LINKED VIA PRESTEL AND THE PACKET SWITCHED SERVICE TO EXTERNAL COMPUTERS,
ALLOWING A RANGE OF NEW SERVICES TO BE OFFERED. EXAMINES THE SIGNALS COMING
FROM THE VIEWDATA INDUSTRY AND POTENTIAL USER ORGANISATIONS TO ANALYSE THE
DIRECTIONS IN WHICH THIS MORE SOPHISTICATED FORM OF PRESTEL MAY BE CHANNELLED.
OUTLINES' THE BACKGROUND TO GATEWAY'S UK INTRODUCTION; HOW THE GATEWAY
FACILITY OPERATES; APPLICATIONS AND THE INDUSTRIES LIKELY TO BE AFFECTED; AND
GATEWAY'S LONGER TERM INFLUENCE.
