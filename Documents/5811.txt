INFOPASS' SOME ORGANIZATIONAL CONCERNS OF A LOCAL COOPERATIVE REFERENCE
PROGRAM.

A 3-MONTH TRIAL OF THE DEARBORN AREA LIBRARIES INFOPASS SYSTEM (DALIS), A
LOCAL LIBRARY COOPERATIVE PROGRAMME COORDINATING THE REFERENCE SERVICES OF 6
LIBRARIES (SPECIAL, PUBLIC, AND ACADEMIC), SUGGESTS THAT FURTHER STUDY IS
WARRANTED TO DEVELOP COST BENEFIT DATA. ANALYSIS INDICATES THAT DOCUMENT
DELIVERY AND TELECOMMUNICATION USE SHOULD BE INVESTIGATED TO OVERCOME LOW
PROGRAMME ACCEPTANCE BY PATRONS. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
