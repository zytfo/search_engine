AACR2 AND MAPS.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA CATALOGUERS'
SECTION NATIONAL CATALOGUING SEMINAR, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. THE
ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING COMMITTEE FOR CARTOGRAPHIC MATERIALS WAS FORMED IN
1979 AND IS PREPARING AN INTERPRETATIVE MANUAL OF MAP CATALOGUING, BUILT ON
CHAPTER 3 OF THE ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING RULES (2ND EDITION) AND INCLUDING
ADDITIONAL GUIDELINES WHERE NECESSARY. OUTLINES THE MOTIVATION, PHILOSOPHY,
AND FORMAT OF THE MANUAL AND INDICATES THE MAJOR PROBLEM AREAS IN AACR 2 WHICH
HAVE REQUIRED AMPLIFICATION, INTERPRETATION, OR CORRECTION.
