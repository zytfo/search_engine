NOTATION IN CLASSIFICATION.
     
(MLS DISSERTATION.) EXAMINES THE FUNCTION OF THE NOTATIONAL SYSTEM IN A
CLASSIFICATION SCHEME, THE MANNER IN WHICH IT OPERATES, AND THE FEATURES WHICH
DETERMINE THE MEASURE OF ITS SUCCESS OR FAILURE. CONSIDERS THE 5 MAJOR GENERAL
CLASSIFICATION SCHEMES (UNIVERSAL DECIMAL, DEWEY DECIMAL, COLON, LIBRARY OF
CONGRESS, AND BIBLIOGRAPHIC).
