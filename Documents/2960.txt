COMPUTERIZED TEXT MANAGEMENT.
     
SOME APPLICATIONS OF COMPUTERISED TEXT MANAGEMENT (CIM) ARE CONSIDERED, WITH
PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO THE ADVANCED TEXT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM, A SOFTWARE
PACKAGE SUPPLIED BY IBM. A DESCRIPTION IS GIVEN OF HOW DATA BASES WERE SET UP
AT ONE TRAFFIC ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT IN THE US AND AT THE DEPARTMENT OF
SOCIAL SERVICES. THE BENEFITS OF CTM ARE SUMMARISED.
