INTERDISCIPLINARY STUDIES' A CLASSIFIER'S PROBLEM.
     
THE GROWTH OF INTERDISCIPLINARY SUBJECTS SUCH AS ENVIRONMENTAL STUDIES,
ENERGY, COMPUTERS AND WOMEN'S STUDIES HAVE PRESENTED A GREAT CHALLENGE TO
ESTABLISHED CLASSIFICATION SCHEMES. EXAMINES THE CASE FOR ONE PLACE
CLASSIFICATION AND EVALUATES THE BROWN CLASSIFICATION IN THE LIGHT OF THIS
PRINCIPLE.
