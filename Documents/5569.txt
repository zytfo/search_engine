INTER-LIBRARY COMPARISONS: A REPORT ON PROGRESS WITH PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO
PUBLIC LIBRARIES.

INTERLIBRARY COMPARISONS AS A MANAGEMENT TOOL ARE DESCRIBED. A GENERAL OUTLINE
OF INTERFIRM COMPARISONS AS A TOOL IN MANAGEMENT IS FOLLOWED BY A DESCRIPTION
OF THE WORK CARRIED OUT BY THE CENTRE FOR INTERFIRM COMPARISON, FUNDED BY
BRITISH LIBRARY R&D, INTO INTERLIBRARY COMPARISONS. THE FINAL PART DESCRIBES
THE SCOPE AND CONTENT OF THE MAIN COMPARISON SO FAR UNDERTAKEN FOR PUBLIC
LIBRARIES.
