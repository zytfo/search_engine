TELECOMMUNICATIONS IN THE ELECTRONIC OFFICE.
     
THE WORK STATION OF THE EXECUTIVE OF THE FUTURE WILL EVOLVE FROM THE
COMMUNICATING WORD PROCESSOR (CWP) AND WILL INCORPORATE THE BASIC FACILITIES
PROVIDED BY THE CWP FOR INPUT, TEXT DISPLAY, TEXT MANIPULATION, HARD COPY
OUTPUT AND COMMUNICATION. THE ENHANCEMENT OF THESE FACILITIES WILL RESULT FROM
THE ADDITION OF FEATURES CURRENTLY FOUND IN STAND ALONE DEVICES. DESCRIBES
SOME OF THE MORE LIKELY DEVELOPMENTS THAT WILL EMERGE FOR INPUT, STORAGE AND
OUTPUT. ALSO DISCUSSES COMMUNICATION AND NETWORK SERVICES AND STANDARDISATION.
