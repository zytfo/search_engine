USING VIEWDATA AS A COMPLEMENTARY MEDIUM (IN GERMAN).
     
13 WEST GERMAN PUBLISHERS ARE SURVEYED ABOUT THEIR VIEWS ON BILDSCHIRMTEXT,
THE WEST GERMAN VIEWDATA SYSTEM. EACH IS ASKED FOR REASONS FOR PARTICIPATING
(OR NOT) IN THE SYSTEM'S PILOT TESTS. OPINIONS ON THE POSSIBLE BENEFITS OF THE
SYSTEM AND ITS THREAT TO THE PRINTED MEDIA ARE SUMMARISED AND THE PUBLISHERS
ARE ASKED FOR THEIR OPINIONS ON FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS OF THE SERVICE.
