READERSHIP CHARACTERISTICS AND ATTITUDES. SERVICE TO BLIND AND PHYSICALLY
HANDICAPPED USERS.
     
PRESENTS FINDINGS BASED ON A SURVEY OF USERS OF THE US NATIONAL LIBRARY
SERVICE FOR THE BLIND AND PHYSICALLY HANDICAPPED (NLS) IN SEPT-OCT 79. THE
SURVEY'S OBJECTIVES WERE TO DEVELOP A PROFILE OF THE NLS READERSHIP AND TO
HELP NLS IN ITS FUTURE PROGRAMME PLANNING BY IDENTIFYING READERS' INTERESTS
AND CONCERNS.
