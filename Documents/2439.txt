ONLINE BIBLIOGRAPHIC SERVICE' A COMPARISON.
     
A GRAPHIC COMPARISON IS OFFERED OF THE COMMANDS, COSTS, AND DATA BASE
AVAILABILITY OF 4 ON-LINE BIBLIOGRAPHIC RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS' SDC ORBIT, LOCKHEED
DIALOG, BIBLIOGRAPHIC RETRIEVAL SERVICES BRS, AND DEFENSE TECHNICAL
INFORMATION CENTER DROLS. SOME PARTICULAR FEATURES OF THE INDIVIDUAL SYSTEMS
ARE ALSO DISCUSSED. THE COMMAND CHARTS WERE CONSTRUCTED AS 1 HORIZONTAL GUIDE
TO BE POSTED NEAR A TERMINAL FOR THE OPERATOR'S USE. THEY ARE NOT INTENDED AS
A SUBSTITUTE FOR MANUALS OR TRAINING SESSIONS.
