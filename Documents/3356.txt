STATUS REPORT ON THE MAP CATALOGING PROJECT AT THE ILLINOIS STATE LIBRARY.
     
ILLINOIS STATE LIBRARY BEGAN ITS MAP CATALOGUING PROJECT ON 1 AUG 80. ALL
FORMERLY UNCATALOGUED SHEET MAPS ARE BEING CATALOGUED IN MARC MAP FORMAT AND
THOSE ALREADY CATALOGUED BY VARIOUS METHODS ARE BEING RECATALOGUED, USING
OCLC. BECAUSE THE STATE LIBRARY IS A REGIONAL GOVERNMENT PRINTING OFFICE
DEPOSITORY, THE FEDERAL MAPS ARE BEING CLASSIFIED ACCORDING TO THE SUDOC
(SUPERINTENDENT OF DOCUMENTS) SYSTEM. ALL OTHER MAPS ARE BEING CLASSIFIED BY
THE LIBRARY OF CONGRESS G SYSTEM. DESCRIBES PROGRESS ON THE PROJECT AND
DISCUSSES PROBLEMS ENCOUNTERED AND THEIR SOLUTION.
