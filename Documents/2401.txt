PERMUTED MEDICAL SUBJECT HEADINGS, 1982.
     
PERMUTED MEDICAL SUBJECT HEADINGS, 1982, IS A COMPUTER-GENERATED DISPLAY OF
ALL TERMS, I.E. DESCRIPTORS AND CROSS-REFERENCES, IN THE ALPHABETIC LIST OF
THE NATIONAL LIBRARY OF MEDICINE'S MEDICAL SUBJECT HEADINGS (MESH) FOR 1982,
AS WELL AS ALL CITATION TYPE, CHECK TAG, AND GEOGRAPHIC DESCRIPTORS IN THE
ANNOTATED ALPHABETIC MESH, 1982. THE PERMUTED MESH TAKES EACH SIGNIFICANT WORD
THAT APPEARS IN EACH MESH TERM AND THEN LISTS ALL THE MESH TERMS IN WHICH THAT
WORD APPEARS.
