TELEVISION AND CHILDREN: A PRO/CON READING LIST.
     
PRESENTS AN ANNOTATED BIBLIOGRAPHY OF MATERIAL ON TELEVISION AND CHILDREN
WHICH LIBRARIANS CAN GIVE TO BOTH CHILDREN AND ADULTS. ONLY ITEMS PUBLISHED
SINCE 1970 ARE INCLUDED. OF THE 53 TITLES, 24 ARE PRO, 22 CON, 3 PRO/CON, AND
4 'ON THE FENCE'.
