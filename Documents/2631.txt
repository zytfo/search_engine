A PAPERBACK LIBRARY AT SAINT-EUSTACHE, QUEBEC.
     
DESCRIBES A PROJECT TO PROVIDE LENDING FACILITIES AT LOW COST WITH MINIMUM
DELAY AT A PAPERBACK 'DEPOT' IN SAINT-EUSTACHE, QUEBEC. DETAILS ARE GIVEN OF
COSTS, REGISTRATION AND LOAN PROCEDURES, ACQUISITION AND PREPARATION OF STOCK,
AND SHELF ARRANGEMENT. THE SUCCESS OF THE PROJECT TO DATE IS ASSESSED, AND THE
ADVANTAGES OF PAPERBACK COLLECTION IN GENERAL DISCUSSED.
