THE SITUATION IN THE UNITED KINGDOM.
     
CONTRIBUTION TO A THEMATIC ISSUE ON PUBLIC LENDING RIGHT (PLR). OUTLINES THE
CASES FOR AND AGAINST PLR IN THE UK; THE ORIGIN AND EVOLUTION OF THE PLR
CAMPAIGN; ATTEMPTS TO INTRODUCE PLR LEGISLATION IN PARLIAMENT; PROVISIONS OF
THE PUBLIC LENDING RIGHT ACT PASSED IN 1979; AND MOVES TOWARDS THE ACT'S
IMPLEMENTATION.
