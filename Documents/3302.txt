A DALA PROJECT 25 YEARS AGO.
     
DESCRIBES THE 1ST DALA PROJECT CARRIED OUT IN 1956-58 BY THE AUTHOR AND
FINANCED INITIALLY BY LOTTERY MONEY GAINED BY DALARNA LIBRARY ASSOCIATION. THE
AIM WAS TO REACH NEW LIBRARY USERS. AFTER A SCANT EXAMINATION OF THE AVAILABLE
LITERATURE THE AUTHOR BEGAN SEEKING POTENTIAL USER GROUPS SUCH AS SOCIETIES
FOR HOUSEWIVES, FARMERS, PENSIONERS, AND SCHOOLS. CONTACTS WERE MADE AND
LETTERS WRITTEN. BOOK EXHIBITIONS AND BOOK TALKS WERE ARRANGED AT MEETINGS OF
THE SOCIETIES. A PROPER EVALUATION OF THE PROJECT WAS NOT MADE, ALTHOUGH SOME
FIGURES ARE AVAILABLE IN UPPSALA UNIVERSITY'S SOCIOLOGICAL SURVEY ON LEISURE
HABITS IN DALARNA PUBLISHED IN 1959. THE AUTHOR LEFT IN 1958, BUT THE PROJECT
WAS CARRIED ON BY OTHERS IN A LIMITED FORM UNTIL 1966, AND A NEW PROJECT IS
DUE TO START IN AUTUMN 81.
