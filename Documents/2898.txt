TEST OF THE EQUIFREQUENT CHARACTER STRING TEXT SEARCH TECHNIQUE.
     
REPORT DESCRIBES THE DESIGN AND IMPLEMENTATION OF SOFTWARE, BASED ON THE
VARIETY GENERATION METHOD, FOR A SEARCH SYSTEM TO MATCH RETRIEVAL PROFILES
AGAINST DOCUMENT TEXTS IN THE INSPEC DATABASE, AND THE EVALUATION OF THE
SYSTEM RELATIVE TO THAT USED BY INSPEC FOR ITS SDI SERVICE IN TERMS OF
COMPUTER RESOURCES AND COSTS. THE EVALUATION FAVOURS THE VARIETY-GENERATION
METHOD. USING INSPEC'S CHARGING ALGORITHM, ITS COSTS ARE APPROXIMATELY 75% OF
THOSE OF THE OPERATIONAL SYSTEM, WHILE THE CENTRAL PROCESSOR TIME IS REDUCED
TO 1/2.
