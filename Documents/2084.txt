EARLY CHILDHOOD LIBRARY PROGRAMMING' MEASUREMENT AND EVALUATION.
     
THIS RESEARCH AND DEMONSTRATION PROJECT WAS UNDERTAKEN TO STUDY STYLES AND
TECHNIQUES OF EVALUATION WHICH ARE APPLICABLE TO EARLY CHILDHOOD PROGRAMMING
IN SCHOOL AND PUBLIC LIBRARIES, AND TO IDENTIFY SOME METHODS OF MEASURING
OUTCOMES IN ORDER TO IMPROVE AND JUSTIFY LIBRARY PROGRAMMES FOR YOUNG
CHILDREN. REPORT NOT AVAILABE FROM NTIS.
