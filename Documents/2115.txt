A SHORT ACCOUNT OF THE LIBRARY AT LONGLEAT HOUSE, WARMINSTER, WILTS.
     
A COPIOUSLY-ILLUSTRATED ACCOUNT OF THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE LIBRARY AT LONGLEAT.
IT IS CLAIMED TO BE THE FINEST PRIVATE LIBRARY IN ENGLAND AND CONTAINS A FULL
SHELF OF BOOKS PRINTED BY WILLIAM CAXTON, MANUSCRIPTS OF GREAT ANTIQUITY, AND
BOOKS OF WHICH NO OTHER COPIES ARE KNOWN.
