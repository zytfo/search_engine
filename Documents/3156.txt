INVESTIGATION OF A COMPUTERIZED NETWORK TO FACILITATE RESOURCE SHARING WITHIN
THE VA LIBRARY NETWORK (VALNET). VOLUME 1.
     
THE PURPOSE OF THIS PROJECT IS THE INVESTIGATION OF A COMPUTERISED NETWORK TO
FACILITATE RESOURCE SHARING WITHIN THE VA LIBRARY NETWORK. THE PROJECT
CONSISTS OF 3 PHASES' (1) INFORMATION GATHERING, ANALYSIS, AND SYNTHESIS; (2)
PROBLEM DEFINITION; AND (3) REQUIREMENTS FORMULATION.
