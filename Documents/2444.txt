DOE'S ENERGY DATA BASE (EDB) VERSUS OTHER ENERGY-RELATED DATA BASES' A
COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS.
     
THE RELEASE OF THE DOE ENERGY DATA BASE TO COMMERCIAL SERVICES IN 1980 RAISED
AN IMMEDIATE QUESTION IN THE MINDS OF SOME SEARCHERS' HOW DOES THIS NEW DATA
BASE FIT INTO THE SPECTRUM OF ALREADY-AVAILABLE DATA BASES. THE INVESTIGATION
ATTEMPTS TO RESOLVE THIS QUESTION.
