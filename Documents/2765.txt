CONSUMER HEALTH INFORMATION NEEDS AND ACCESS THROUGH EXISTING INDEXES.
     
A SOLUTION TO THE DEMAND FOR CONSUMER HEALTH INFORMATION IN PUBLIC LIBRARIES
IS PROPOSED; USE OF MATERIALS ALREADY IN MOST COLLECTIONS AND ACCESSIBLE
THROUGH POPULAR JOURNAL INDEXES SUCH AS 2READERS' GUIDE1 AND 2MAGAZINE INDEX1.
THE RESULTS OF A STUDY OF THE RESPONSIVENESS OF THE 2 INDEXES TO PATRONS'
REQUESTS FOR HEALTH-RELATED INFORMATION IN 1 PUBLIC LIBRARY ARE PRESENTED
ALONG WITH A COST ANALYSIS OF THE METHOD.
