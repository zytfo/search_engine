MODERN TECHNIQUES IN PERIODICAL PUBLISHING (IN ITALIAN).
     
NEW TECHNOLOGIES AFFECTING PERIODICAL PUBLISHING ARE EXAMINED, WITH PARTICULAR
REFERENCE TO THEIR EFFECTS ON EDITORIAL AND PRODUCTION PROCEDURES. THE
TECHNOLOGIES DISCUSSED INCLUDE VDU TERMINALS, OCR, PHOTOTYPESETTING WITH CRT
TERMINALS, INK JET PRINTING AND REMOTE PRESS CONTROL. COMPETITIVE TECHNOLOGY
AND ITS IMPLICATIONS ARE ALSO DISCUSSED WITH PARTICULAR REFERENCE TO TELETEXT,
VIEWDATA AND FACSIMILE TRANSMISSION.
