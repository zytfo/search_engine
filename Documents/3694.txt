WORD PROCESSOR (HERMES WP-6000) AND ITS APPLICATION TO LIBRARY WORK.
     
A WORD PROCESSOR, HERMES WP-6000, WAS INSTALLED IN TOKYO METROPOLITAN
GERONTOLOGY CENTER LIBRARY IN FEB 80. DETAILED DESCRIPTIONS ARE PROVIDED FOR
THE SYSTEM COMPONENTS, WHICH INCLUDE A CRT DISPLAYER, DISK DRIVE, KEYBOARD, A
PRINTER AND A FLOPPY DISK. THE LIBRARY APPLICATIONS OF WORD PROCESSORS ARE
EXPLORED IN THE AREA OF CATALOGUE CARD PRODUCTION, BOOK CATALOGUE PRODUCTION,
AND CURRENT FOREIGN SERIAL RECORD MAINTENANCE.
