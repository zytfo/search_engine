USER NEEDS IN DOCUMENTATION AND INFORMATION. 1977-MAY 1981 (CITATIONS FROM THE
NTIS DATA BASE).
     
REPORTS ON MEETING THE NEEDS OF THE INFORMATION SCIENCES USER IN THE MOST
DIRECT AND SPECIFIC WAY ARE CITED, INCLUDING METHODS USED TO DETERMINE THOSE
NEEDS AND MEASURES TAKEN TO FULFILL THEM IN APPLICATION TO EDUCATIONAL
RESOURCES, DATA BASES, DATA MANAGEMENT, LIBRARY NETWORKS, PHOTOGRAPHIC
RECORDING SYSTEMS, TECHNICAL INFORMATION CENTRES, AND CONSULTING SERVICES.
