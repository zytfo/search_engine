CHANGING WORLD OF LIBRARY BINDING PART 1.
     
DISCUSSES DEVELOPMENTS IN THE WORLD OF LIBRARY BINDING, PARTICULARLY IN LIGHT
OF THE WORK CARRIED OUT AT THE TESTING LABORATORY OF THE LIBRARY BINDING
INSTITUTE OF THE ROCHESTER INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY. (THE PAPER WAS 1ST
PUBLISHED IN 2PRESERVATION OF LIBRARY MATERIALS1, ED. BY JOYCE R. RUSSELL, NEW
YORK, SLA, 1980).
