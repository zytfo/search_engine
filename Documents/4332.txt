ON THE ACCESS TO CARTOGRAPHIC COLLECTIONS IN GDR LIBRARIES.
     
SUITABLE CATALOGUING AND CLASSIFICATION OF CARTOGRAPHIC COLLECTIONS OFFERS THE
USER EASIER ACCESS TO THESE MATERIALS AND OFFERS THE LIBRARIAN A FUNDAMENTAL
BASIS FOR PROVIDING INFORMATION AND PROMOTING OPTIMAL USE OF HIS COLLECTIONS.
HERE, THE METHODS OF CLASSIFICATION WHICH ARE USED IN EAST GERMANY ARE
LINEATED USING EXAMPLES FROM THE MOST SIGNIFICANT MAP COLLECTIONS.
