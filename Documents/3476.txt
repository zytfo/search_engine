JUSTIFYING THE ELECTRONIC OFFICE-THE NEED FOR AN 'ADDED VALUE' APPROACH.
     
SUGGESTS THAT EMPHASIS ON THE COST-SAVING ELEMENTS OF OFFICE AUTOMATION CAN
HAVE AN OVERALL NEGATIVE EFFECT ON ENTERPRISE OR ORGANISATION, AND DISCUSSES
SOME APPROACHES TO AN 'ADDED VALUE' APPROACH, INCLUDING REQUIREMENTS OF
ANALYSIS AND JUSTIFICATION, ANALYSIS OF MANAGERIAL WORK, CONCEPT OF PLANNED
SUPPORT LEVELS AND 'DELIVERED SERVICE' JUSTIFICATION.
