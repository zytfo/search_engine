THE USBC AND CONTROL OF THE BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATABASE.
     
A NUMBER OF PROBLEMS HAVE ARISEN BY THE USE OF ISBN FOR COMPUTERISED
BIBLIOGRAPHIC RECORDS. THE NEED FOR A CONTROL NUMBER THAT IS NOT SUBJECT TO
MISAPPLICATION EITHER BY HUMAN ERROR OR THROUGH FAULTS IN ITS STRUCTURE HAS
LED TO THE CONCEPT OF THE UNIVERSAL STANDARD BOOK CODE (USBC). THE STRUCTURE
AND FUNCTION OF USBC IS DESCRIBED BRIEFLY AND THE ABILITY OF USBC TO DETECT
DUPLICATES IN DATA BASES IS HIGHLIGHTED.
