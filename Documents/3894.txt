A WORKSHOP ON QUALITATIVE INFORMATION RETRIEVAL, NOVEMBER 18-20, 1980.
     
WORK IN THE OFFICE OF ADMINISTRATION ON THE DEVELOPMENT OF A NON-INTERMEDIATED
INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEM LED TO CONSIDERATION OF THE PROBLEM OF
INFORMATION RETRIEVAL WHEN CONTROLLED VOCABULARY FAILS. DISCUSSION OF THAT
PROBLEM LED TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF A TYPOLOGY OF SUCH FAILURES AND TO AN
EXAMINATION OF METHODS OF RESOLVING THE PROBLEMS.
