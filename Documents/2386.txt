PLANNING FOR THE ADOPTION OF AACR2 AT THE NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF SINGAPORE
LIBRARY (WITH SPECIAL REFERENCE TO THE SINGAPORE/MALAYSIA COLLECTION).
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE LAS/PPM JOINT CATALOGUING SEMINAR ON AACR2, 8-10 DEC
80. DISCUSSES THE RESPONSE OF THE NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF SINGAPORE LIBRARY TO
THE 2ND EDITION OF THE 2ANGLO-AMERICAN CATALOGUING RULES1, AND THE NEED FOR
MORE DETAILED PLANNING BEFORE FULL IMPLEMENTATION CAN BE ACHIEVED. A
QUANTITATIVE STUDY WAS CARRIED OUT TO DETERMINE THE NUMBER OF HEADING CHANGES
AFFECTING THE NUSL'S SINGAPORE/MALAYSIA COLLECTION CATALOGUE SAMPLE. DESCRIBES
THE IMPLICATIONS OF THE RESULTS OF THE STUDY IN THE CONTEXT OF THE
RETROSPECTIVE CATALOGUE CONVERSION OF THE SINGAPORE/MALAYSIA COLLECTION
RECORDS INTO MACHINE-READABLE FORM.
