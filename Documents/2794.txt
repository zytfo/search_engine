DEVELOPING A FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR LOCAL GOVERNMENTS'
THE KEY ISSUES.
     
A SYMPOSIUM TO DISCUSS THE KEY ISSUES LOCAL GOVERNMENTS MUST CONSIDER IN
DEVELOPING A COMPUTER-BASED FINANCIAL INFORMATION SYSTEM, INCLUDING SOFTWARE
AND HARDWARE, WAS HELD AT CLEVELAND STATE UNIVERSITY IN OHIO. ITS PRIMARY
FOCUS WAS TO ASSIST THE CITY OF CLEVELAND WHICH WAS IN THE MIDST OF PLANNING A
MAJOR REVISION OF ITS SYSTEM.
