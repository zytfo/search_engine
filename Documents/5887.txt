BIBLION' THE ON-LINE BIBLIOGRAPHIC SYSTEM OF THE BARR SMITH LIBRARY.

DESCRIBES THE DEVELOPMENT, FEATURES AND USE OF BIBLION-THE ON-LINE
BIBLIOGRAPHIC SYSTEM OF THE BARR SMITH LIBRARY OF THE UNIVERSITY OF ADELAIDE.
BIBLION IS USED FOR THE PRODUCTION CATALOGUE OF THE UNDERGRADUATE COLLECTION,
THE CATALOGUE OF MONOGRAPHS SELECTED FOR STORAGE, AND THE MEANS OF ACCESS TO
ABN (AUSTRALIAN BIBLIOGRAPHIC NETWORK) FOR THE USE OF ALL ABN FUNCTIONS
CURRENTLY AVAILABLE.
