HMSO CATALOGUE DEVELOPMENTS' A PERSONAL ACCOUNT.

DISCUSSES SOME OF THE DEVELOPMENTS IN HMSO CATALOGUING SERVICES THAT HAVE
TAKEN PLACE SINCE THE AUTHOR TOOK OVER AS HEAD OF BIBLIOGRAPHICAL SERVICES IN
1979. DESCRIBES BRIEFLY' IMPROVEMENTS THAT HAVE BEEN MADE IN THE TIMETABLING
OF CATALOGUES; THE INTRODUCTION OF THE DAILY PRESTEL INFORMATION SERVICE; AND
THE COMPUTERISATION OF CATALOGUING. LISTS ALL PUBLICATIONS CURRENTLY PRODUCED
UNDER HMSO'S BIBLIOGRAPHICAL PROGRAMME, AND CONSIDERS THE POSSIBILITY OF ITS
CATALOGUE RECORDS BEING MADE AVAILABLE ON-LINE IN THE NEAR FUTURE.
