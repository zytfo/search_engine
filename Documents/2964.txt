WORD PROCESSING EQUIPMENT AND APPLICATIONS. JANUARY, 1976-SEPTEMBER, 1981
(CITATIONS FROM THE PAPER AND BOARD, PRINTING, AND PACKAGING INDUSTRIES
RESEARCH ASSOCIATIONS DATA BASE).
     
WORD PROCESSING AND KINDS OF EQUIPMENT AND APPLICATIONS ARE CONSIDERED IN THIS
BIBLIOGRAPHY FROM GLOBAL SOURCES. TOPICS DISCUSSED INCLUDE HOW TO CUT OFFICE
COSTS, TRENDS IN PHOTO-TYPESETTING, LASER PRINTERS, INK JET PRINTING, FULLY
AUTOMATED RECORDS MANAGEMENT, AND DIGITAL EQUIPMENT IN CONJUNCTION WITH WORD
PROCESSING.
