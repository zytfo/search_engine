COMPUTER-AIDED INDEXING IN THE ARTS' THE CASE FOR A THESAURUS OF ART TERMS.
     
PAPER DELIVERED TO THE ART LIBRARIES SOCIETY OF NORTH AMERICA CONFERENCE, SAN
FRANCISCO, 22 FEB 81. DESCRIBES HOW SUBJECT INDEXING ACTIVITIES IN ART ARE
BEING AIDED BY THE USE OF COMPUTERS; NOTES THE CURRENT STATE OF DISARRAY AMONG
ART SUBJECT INDEXING SYSTEMS; AND SUGGESTS THAT THE AVAILABILITY OF A
STANDARDISED VOCABULARY WILL PROVIDE AN IMPORTANT ADJUNCT TO COMPUTERISED
INDEXING.
