PIRA SEMINAR S13. AN INTRODUCTION TO DATABASE PUBLISHING.
     
THE TEXT OF TALKS BY M.Y. GATES ON THE BASICS OF DATA BASE PUBLISHING, AND BY
D.H. BARLOW ON IDENTIFYING DATA BASES AND ON FINANCIAL AND MARKETING
STRATEGIES FOR DATA BASE PUBLISHING IS GIVEN. A PRESENTATION BY D.H. BARLOW
AND P. BARWISE AT A NFAIS CONFERENCE ON MODELLING TECHNIQUES AS AN AID IN
MARKETING DECISIONS IS ALSO INCLUDED. A LEAFLET ON LANGTON ELECTRONIC
PUBLISHING SYSTEMS IS ENCLOSED.
