TATTLE TAPE SECURITY SYSTEM.
     
A BRIEF ACCOUNT OF THE INSTALLATION OF 3M TATTLE TAPE SECURITY SYSTEMS BY THE
YORK REGIONAL LIBRARY IN LIBRARIES IN OROMOCTO AND FREDERICTON (NEW
BRUNSWICK). OUTLINES REASONS FOR CHOOSING THIS PARTICULAR SYSTEM, DISCUSSES
ITS LOCATION IN THE LIBRARIES, AND VOICES CONCERN OVER DESIGN DEFECTS
(ALTHOUGH THESE ARE FEW).
