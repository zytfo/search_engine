ANTIOPE TELETEXT CAPTIONING.
     
DIGITAL CAPTIONING CAN BE USED IN TELETEXT SYSTEMS AND THE ADVANTAGES OF THIS
TECHNIQUE OVER CONVENTIONAL CAPTIONING ARE EXPLAINED. DIDON IS USED BY ANTIOPE
FOR CAPTIONING. THE USER SELECTS THE PAGE REQUIRED AND THE CAPTIONS CAN BE
TRANSMITTED IN A VARIETY OF FORMATS AND LANGUAGES. THE CAPTIONS MAY BE
SITUATED ANYWHERE ON THE TELETEXT PAGE AND MAY APPEAR IN DIFFERENT PLACES ON
THE SCREEN AT DIFFERENT TIMES.
