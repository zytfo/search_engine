SELECTIVE DISSEMINATION OF INFORMATION IN ACADEMIC LIBRARIES.

PRESENTS A HISTORICAL REVIEW OF SELECTIVE DISSEMINATION OF INFORMATION AS A
CURRENT AWARENESS SYSTEM, THE NEED FOR IT, AND SOME PRACTICAL SUGGESTIONS FOR
ITS INSTIGATION. EXAMPLES ARE DRAWN FROM BOTH SPECIAL AND ACADEMIC LIBRARIES.
