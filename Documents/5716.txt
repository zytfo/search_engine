ISSUES AND APPLICATIONS OF MICROCOMPUTERS FOR LIBRARIES.

CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO PAPERS PRESENTED AT THE CANADIAN LIBRARY
ASSOCIATION ANNUAL CONFERENCE, JUNE 81. EXAMINES THE IMPACT OF MICROCOMPUTERS
ON LIBRARIES, AND ARGUES THAT THEIR INTRODUCTION REPRESENTS A CRITICAL POINT
FOR INFORMATION PROFESSIONALS. DISCUSSES THE APPLICATIONS OF MICROCOMPUTERS AS
PART OF A REFERENCE SERVICE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM, INFORMATION RETRIEVAL SYSTEMS,
AND FOR EXTERNAL ON-LINE ACCESS.
