THE CONCEPTION OF THE UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF PUBLICATIONS PROGRAMME AND ITS
PRACTICAL RESULTS.
     
BRIEFLY DESCRIBES THE MOST IMPORTANT PHASES OF THE UNIVERSAL AVAILABILITY OF
PUBLICATIONS (UAP) PROGRAMME. ANALYSES THE CONNECTION BETWEEN THE PROGRAMME
AND INTER-LIBRARY LENDING. OUTLINES THE CONCEPTUAL PROBLEMS OF A NATIONAL
INTER-LIBRARY LENDING SYSTEM MODEL AND PRESENTS 4 BASIC MODELS SPECIFYING
THEIR ADVANTAGES AND DISADVANTAGES. FINALLY DESCRIBES SOME PRACTICAL RESULTS
OF THE UAP PROGRAMME IN RELATION TO BRAZIL, BULGARIA, FRANCE, EAST GERMANY,
MALAYSIA, NORWAY, UK AND USA.
