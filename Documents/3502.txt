DOLLARS-AND-CENTS VALUE OF EFFICIENT PRESENTATION. A PLEA FOR THE RETENTION OF
THE WELL-PRODUCED PRIMARY JOURNAL.
     
ATTEMPTS TO DEMONSTRATE, WITH THE HELP OF A SIMPLE ECONOMIC MODEL, THAT THE
PRESENT TREND TOWARD LOW-COST AND LOW-QUALITY PUBLICATION OF SCIENTIFIC WORK
MAY BE BENEFICIAL FOR THE INDIVIDUAL PUBLISHER BUT HARMFUL TO THE COMMUNITY AS
A WHOLE.
