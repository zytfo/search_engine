THE COMMUNICATIONS REVOLUTION.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AS THE 14TH ANNUAL LECTURE AT THE ROYAL AERONAUTICAL SOCIETY,
LONDON, 9 JUN 81. DISCUSSES SOME OF THE CHARACTERISTICS OF THE COMMUNICATIONS
AND INFORMATION REVOLUTION' THE PROCESSES AND PHENOMENA WHICH WE VAGUELY BUT
DEFINITELY FEEL ARE CHANGING SOCIETY AND OUR OUTLOOK ON SOCIETY, OUR RELATIONS
TO WORK AND LEISURE, OUR RELATIONS TO EACH OTHER AND OURSELVES.
