CONTRIBUTION TO AN ISSUE DEVOTED TO PAPERS PRESENTED AT THE CANADIAN LIBRARY
ASSOCIATION ANNUAL CONFERENCE, JUNE 81. DISCUSSES THE DEVELOPMENT OF UNIONS ON
CANADIAN LIBRARIES, AND ADVISES LIBRARY BOARD MEMBERS ON DEALING WITH UNIONS.
HIGHLIGHTS THE NEED FOR BOARD MEMBERS TO BE FAMILIAR WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF
THEIR PROVINCES'S LABOUR CODE, AND FOR CHIEF LIBRARIANS AND THEIR ASSISTANTS
TO BE WELL TRAINED IN CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION. ALSO CONSIDERS BARGAINING AND
NEGOTIATING TACTICS.
