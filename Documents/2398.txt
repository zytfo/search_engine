THE DEWEY DECIMAL CLASSIFICATION AND MUSIC.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 LIBRARY ASSOCIATION OF AUSTRALIA CATALOGUERS'
SECTION NATIONAL CATALOGUING SEMINAR, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED.
DESCRIBES THE DEWEY DECIMAL CLASSIFICATION PHOENIX MUSIC SCHEDULE, DUE FOR
PUBLICATION IN LATE 1980. THE AUTHOR HAS BEEN USING THE NEW SCHEME'S 1975
DRAFT SINCE THAT YEAR FOR CLASSIFYING THE BOOKS AND PERIODICALS (BUT NOT
SCORES) IN SYDNEY OPERA HOUSE LIBRARY.
