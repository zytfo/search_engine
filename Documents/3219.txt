THE STATUS OF IRANIAN INFORMATION SCIENCE.
     
PROVIDES A CONCISE HISTORY AND ASSESSMENT OF IRANIAN INFORMATION SCIENCE IN
THE PAST 2 DECADES. TRACES PROGRESS FROM EARLY INDEXING PROJECTS TO
INTERACTIVE DATA BASE SEARCHING. ASSESSES THE IMPORTANCE OF EARLY COURSE WORK,
AN EARLY SOPHISTICATED TECHNICAL INFORMATION CENTRE, AND THE IRANIAN
DOCUMENTATION CENTRE'S EXTENSIVE ACTIVITIES.
