FIRST IMPRESSIONS OF THE CHIEF CONSERVATION OFFICER' THE CHALLENGE IS IMMENSE.
     
DISCUSSES BRIEFLY THE MAIN CONSERVATION NEEDS OF THE HUMANITIES RESEARCH
CENTER AT THE UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS, AUSTIN, AND DESCRIBES WHAT HAS BEEN DONE SO
FAR TO MEET THEM, INCLUDING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A CONSERVATION COMMITTEE
COMPRISED OF VARIOUS DEPARTMENTS AT THE CENTER, AND THE INITIATION OF A
TRAINING PROGRAMME FOR THE CONSERVATION OF LIBRARY AND ARCHIVAL MATERIALS.
