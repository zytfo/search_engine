NATIONAL BOOK WEEK REPORT.
     
HOHOLA PUBLIC LIBRARY, PORT MORESBY, PAPUA NEW GUINEA, ORGANISED MANY
ACTIVITIES FOR CHILDREN DURING NATIONAL BOOK WEEK, AUG 81, INCLUDING STORY
SESSIONS AND FILM SHOWS. THE CHILDREN'S RESPONSE WAS ENTHUSIASTIC AND IT IS
HOPED THAT SUCH EVENTS WILL ENCOURAGE THEM TO GET TO KNOW AND USE LIBRARIES.
