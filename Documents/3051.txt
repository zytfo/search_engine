TRAINING IN ON-LINE CATALOGUING IN AN ACADEMIC SETTING.
     
IN AN EFFORT TO DETERMINE THE MOST EFFECTIVE MEANS OF TRAINING LIBRARY SCHOOL
STUDENTS FOR LATER PERFORMANCE AS ON-LINE CATALOGUERS, EXAMINES THE METHODS OF
SIMULATION, DIAL-UP ACCESS TO ESTABLISHED BIBLIOGRAPHIC NETWORKS, AND THE USE
OF A CRT TERMINAL DEDICATED TO ONE SUCH NETWORK. ANALYSIS OF THE PROBLEM
INDICATED THAT 2 MODES OF TRAINING ARE REQUIRED' DEVELOPMENT OF THE
INTELLECTUAL CAPACITY FOR ORIGINAL CATALOGUING; AND INCULCATION OF
USER-COMPUTER INTERACTIVE FACILITY.
