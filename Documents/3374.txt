A PERFORMANCE EVALUATION OF SIMILARITY MEASURES, DOCUMENT TERM WEIGHTING
SCHEMES AND REPRESENTATIONS IN A BOOLEAN ENVIRONMENT.
     
REPORTS ON THE RESULTS OF A STUDY WHICH AIMED TO EVALUATE RANKING ALGORITHMS
SO THAT GENERALISATIONS ABOUT THEIR EFFECTIVENESS COULD BE MADE. A SINGLE DATA
BASE, COMMON USER POPULATION, AND COMMON SETS OF QUERIES AND RELEVANCE
JUDGEMENTS WERE USED. ALSO REPORTS ON SOME UNEXPECTED FINDINGS RELATING TO THE
PERFORMANCE OF DOCUMENT REPRESENTATIONS AND SEARCHER DIFFERENCES.
