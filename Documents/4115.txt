DESCRIBES THE LIBRARIES IN TOMMERUP MUNICIPAL DISTRICT, FUNEN. UNLIKE OTHER
AUTHORITIES AFTER THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT REORGANISATION IN 1966, TOMMERUP
RETAINED ITS DECENTRALISED STRUCTURE AND STRENGTHENED EACH OF ITS 4 URBAN
CENTRES. THUS THE 4 PART-TIME LIBRARIES HAVE BEEN OR ARE BEING EXPANDED. AT
PRESENT 2 SHARE PREMISES WITH SCHOOL LIBRARIES. EXPENDITURE PER INHABITANT IS
HIGH COMPARED EVEN TO FULL-TIME LIBRARIES. A HIGH STANDARD FOR CULTURAL
ARRANGEMENTS IS SET. INTERVIEWS THE CHAIRMAN OF THE CULTURAL COMMITTEE AND THE
CHIEF LIBRARIAN. THE PRESENT PART-TIME LIBRARIANS ARE HIGHLY APPRECIATED,
ALTHOUGH IT IS ENVISAGED THAT A QUALIFIED LIBRARIAN WILL BE EMPLOYED WHEN THE
NEW MAIN LIBRARY IS BUILT IN 1983. A STRONG LOCAL CULTURAL LIFE IS ENCOURAGED.
