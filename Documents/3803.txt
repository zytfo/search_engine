EXAMINATION OF THE MARKETING IMPLICATIONS FOR INFORMATION PRODUCTS AND
SERVICES.
     
INFORMATION NEEDS THE APPLICATION OF GOOD MARKETING PRINCIPLES IN ORDER TO
HAVE SATISFIED CUSTOMERS AND CREATE A FINANCIALLY SOUND INDUSTRY. INFORMATION
HAS HOWEVER SEVERAL MARKETING PROBLEMS' LACK OF SKILL WITHIN THE INDUSTRY;
ABSENCE OF MARKET RESEARCH; NO DIRECT CONTACT BETWEEN SUPPLIER AND USER; POOR
PACKAGING; DIFFICULTIES OF MAINTAINING QUALITY; AND LITTLE APPRECIATION OF
VALUE BY THE CUSTOMER. PROMOTION ACTIVITY OF PERSONAL SELLING, MAILING,
ADVERTISING AND EXHIBITING HAS TO BE PLANNED AND MONITORED.
