GIVING BIRTH TO A JOURNAL.

ARTICLE ADAPTED FROM A PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 5TH ANNUAL SPRING CONFERENCE OF
THE LIBRARIANS' ASSOCIATION AT THE UNIVERSITY OF NORTH CAROLINA AT CHAPEL
HILL, MAR 81. MAKES GENERAL POINTS ABOUT STARTING UP A NEW JOURNAL, BASED ON
THE EXPERIENCES OF A SPECIFIC PUBLICATION. A PERCEIVED NEED LED TO THE BIRTH
OF A PUBLICATION, ATTENDED BY COMPLICATIONS-SOME EXPECTED, OTHERS NOT-BUT
RESULTING IN AN INCREASINGLY HEALTHY JOURNAL THAT DISSEMINATES DATA AND IDEAS.
