HAMPSHIRE SOCIAL SERVICES DEPARTMENT ON-LINE INFORMATION SYSTEM.
     
DESCRIBES THE BACKGROUND TO THE SETTING UP OF THE ORIGINAL BATCH SYSTEM IN
1974 AND REVIEWS PROGRESS IN DEVELOPING AN ON-LINE VERSION. THIS NOT ONLY
IMPROVES LEVELS OF ACCURACY IN CLIENT RECORDS BUT HAS ALSO ALLOWED THE
INTRODUCTION OF NEW FIELDS AND FACILITIES SUCH AS VACANCY SEARCHING. IT IS
PLANNED TO INSTALL VISUAL DISPLAY UNITS IN AREA OFFICES IN 1982.
