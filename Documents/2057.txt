REPORT OF THE INTERDEPARTMENTAL COMMITTEE TO CONSIDER LIBRARY AND INFORMATION
SERVICES TO GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS AND INSTRUMENTALITIES.
     
APPOINTED IN MAY 76, THE COMMITTEE TO CONSIDER LIBRARY AND INFORMATION
SERVICES TO GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS AND INSTRUMENTALITIES WAS CHARGED TO MAKE
RECOMMENDATIONS TO THE TASMANIAN MINISTER FOR EDUCATION ON THE EFFECTIVENESS
OF EXISTING SERVICES, HOW THEY COULD BE IMPROVED, AND HOW THEY COULD BE
EXTENDED TO MORE GOVERNMENT OFFICES.
