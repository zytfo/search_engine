THE USE OF HOSPITAL IN-PATIENT DATA IN THE ANALYSIS OF THE INJURIES SUSTAINED
BY ROAD ACCIDENT CASUALTIES.
     
THIS STUDY AIMED AT COMPILING FROM EXISTING HOSPITAL CASUALTY RECORDS AND
POLICE ACCIDENT RECORDS A DATA SET CONTAINING INFORMATION ON THE NATURE OF
INJURIES TO ROAD ACCIDENT CASUALTIES AND ON THE CIRCUMSTANCES OF THE ACCIDENT
IN WHICH EACH CASUALTY WAS INJURED.
