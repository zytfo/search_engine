ONTRIBUTION TO A SECTION DEVOTED TO THE IAML ANNUAL CONFERENCE, 1-7 JULY 79,
SALZBURG. SUMMARISES THE 2 COUNCIL SESSIONS, WITH DETAILS OF THE GENERAL
SECRETARY'S REPORT, IAML AND IFLA, AND VARIOUS IAML ACTIVITIES. A LIST OF
PARTICIPANTS IN THE SESSIONS IS GIVEN.
