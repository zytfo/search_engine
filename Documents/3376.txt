COMMON SUBDIVISIONS IN DEWEY DECIMAL CLASSIFICATION. CRITICAL COMMENTS OF
EDITION 19.
     
PRESENTS AND CRITICALLY EXAMINES THE MAIN CHANGES AND ADDITIONS WHICH HAVE
BEEN INCORPORATED IN THE 7 AUXILIARY TABLES OF EDITION 19 OF DDC. DISCUSSES
THE INTRODUCTION AND USE OF THE TABLE OF PRECEDENCE. SUGGESTS THAT THERE
SHOULD BE MORE INTENSIVE STUDY OF EDITION 19 TO IMPROVE THE SERVICE.
