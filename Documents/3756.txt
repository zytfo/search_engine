REGIONAL CO-ORDINATION IN SOCIAL SCIENCE DOCUMENTATION' THE VIENNA CENTRE.
     
CREATED IN 1963, THE EUROPEAN CO-ORDINATION CENTRE FOR RESEARCH AND
DOCUMENTATION IN SOCIAL SCIENCES (KNOWN AS THE VIENNA CENTRE) IS AN ATTEMPT TO
BROADEN SOCIAL SCIENCE PERSPECTIVES AND CONTRIBUTE TO THE FORMULATION OF
PROBLEMS, METHODS, AND SOLUTIONS ON A SUPRANATIONAL BASIS. ITS ORIGINAL BROAD
REMIT (STILL ADHERED TO) WAS TO 'STIMULATE, MAINLY IN EUROPE, INTERNATIONAL
COMPARATIVE RESEARCHERS IN SOCIAL SCIENCES'. OUTLINES THE CENTRE'S PAST
ACHIEVEMENTS AND DESCRIBES THE CHALLENGING NATURE OF ITS WORK.
