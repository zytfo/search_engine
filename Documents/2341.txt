THE FRENCH MINISTRY OF UNIVERSITIES GUIDELINES FOR LOANS BETWEEN UNIVERSITY
LIBRARIES. MAY 1981.
     
THE NEW OFFICIAL GUIDELINES ARE PRESENTED, COVERING ALL PROCEDURES INVOLVED IN
LOAN OPERATIONS, WITH SEPARATE INSTRUCTIONS FOR TELEX. THE FUNDAMENTAL AIM IS
TO SIMPLIFY PROCEDURES, IMPROVE SERVICES, AND RATIONALISE DOCUMENT DELIVERY
AND RETURN. SAMPLE LOAN REQUEST FORMS ARE APPENDED.
