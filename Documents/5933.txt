WHICH DATABASE? AN EVALUATIVE GUIDE TO ONLINE BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATABASES IN
BUSINESS AND THE SOCIAL SCIENCES.

A GUIDE TO THE RANGE AVAILABLE AND THE FACILITIES AND USES OFFERED, FOR
INFORMATION PROFESSIONALS AND LIBRARIANS WHO USE THE SERVICES LESS THAN
CONSTANTLY AND WISH TO INCREASE THEIR FREQUENCY OR IMPROVE THEIR RESULTS.

UPDATE SERVICE IS AVAILABLE ON SUBSCRIPTION FROM THE AUTHOR AT PRESTON
POLYTECHNIC LIBRARY AND LEARNING RESOURCES CENTRE.
