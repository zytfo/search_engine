GUIDELINE FOR INSPECTION AND QUALITY CONTROL FOR ALPHANUMERIC COMPUTER-OUTPUT
MICROFORMS. CATEGORY' HARDWARE. SUBCATEGORY' MEDIA.
     
THE STANDARD DESCRIBES OPERATIONAL AND QUALITY-CONTROL GUIDELINES FOR
ALPHANUMERIC COMPUTER-OUTPUT MICROFILM (COM) RECORDERS AND MICROFORMS. IT IS
LIMITED TO IMAGES OF LINE PRINTER EQUIVALENT OUTPUT ONLY, SUCH AS THOSE USED
FOR BUSINESS AND GOVERNMENT RECORDS.
