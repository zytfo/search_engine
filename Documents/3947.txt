THE ROLE OF AN INTERMEDIARY IN A UNIVERSITY DEPARTMENT.
     
AT THE FRANKFURT CHEMISTRY DEPARTMENT A RESEARCH PROJECT IS UNDER WAY
CONCERNED WITH TESTING AND EVALUATING INFORMATION SYSTEMS AND SERVICES AS WELL
AS USER EDUCATION. THE MAIN ACTIVITIES ARE' 1) DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT OF A
REFERRAL SYSTEM, WHICH WILL SERVE AS A BASIS FOR THE DECISION ON THE USE OF
DOCUMENT DELIVERY OR INFORMATION SERVICES FOR PARTICULAR NEEDS. 2)
ESTABLISHMENT OF AN ON-LINE SEARCH SERVICE IN THE CHEMISTRY DEPARTMENT. 3)
USER EDUCATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF USER AIDS FOR STUDENTS, MAINLY IN THE
CHEMISTRY DEPARTMENT.
