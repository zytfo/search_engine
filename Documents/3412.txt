VIDEOTEX CHALLENGE?.
     
AN OVERVIEW OF THE STATUS OF UK VIDEOTEX AND TELETEXT IS PRESENTED. THE
EXPERIENCE WHICH HAS BEEN GAINED THROUGH PRESTEL, CEEFAX AND ORACLE IS
DISCUSSED. AREAS WHERE DISTINCT TRENDS ARE SEEN ARE KINDS OF MATERIAL MOST
READILY ACCEPTABLE, GROWTH RATES, AND TYPES OF USER. THE DIFFERENT POTENTIALS
OF INTERACTIVE AND NON-INTERACTIVE SYSTEMS ARE DISCUSSED. THE POTENTIAL THREAT
TO NEWSPAPERS IN UK AND USA IS CONSIDERED.
