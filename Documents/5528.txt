DEVELOPMENT OF LIBRARY EDUCATION IN JORDAN.

(MA THESIS). THE LIBRARY EDUCATION MOVEMENT IN JORDAN WAS STUDIED AND
EVALUATED. EXAMINED THE FACTORS AFFECTING THE DEVELOPMENT OF LIBRARY EDUCATION
IN JORDAN, WHAT TYPES AND SIZE OF PERSONNEL NEEDED BY THE LIBRARY AND
INFORMATION SYSTEM AND HOW BEST THE EXISTING LIBRARY EDUCATION SYSTEM CAN
RESPOND TO EXPRESSED NEEDS.
