THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION/INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION SCIENTISTS SPECIAL LIBRARIES
AND INFORMATION UNITS STAFFING SURVEY 1980.
     
THE INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION SCIENTISTS (IIS) AND THE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION (LA)
CONDUCTED A JOINT STAFFING AND SALARIES SURVEY IN 1980. THIS REPLACED THE IIS
ANNUAL REMUNERATION SURVEY AND WAS PART OF THE LA'S PROGRAMME OF SUCH STUDIES.
117 QUESTIONNAIRES WERE RETURNED, EACH REPRESENTING A SPECIAL LIBRARY OR
INFORMATION UNIT IN THE UK. THE RESULTS OF THE SURVEY ARE REPORTED BRIEFLY.
