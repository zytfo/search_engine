RESOURCE IDENTIFICATION WORKSHOPS AND FORUM FOR RESOURCE SHARING, 1979. A
REPORT TO THE STATE COUNCIL OF HIGHER EDUCATION IN VIRGINIA.
     
DESCRIBES A SERIES OF WORKSHOPS AND A FORUM HELD TO IDENTIFY AND ASSESS
EXISTING COLLECTIONS IN NORTHERN VIRGINIA INSTITUTIONS (LIBRARIES,
UNIVERSITIES, AGENCIES, ETC.) IN THE FOLLOWING SUBJECT AREAS; NON-PRINT
MATERIALS, CHILDREN'S LITERATURE, CONSUMER EDUCATION, HEALTH SCIENCES, LIBRARY
AND INFORMATION SCIENCE, LOCAL HISTORY AND GENEALOGY, MAPS, ORAL HISTORY,
RELIGION, AND SERVICES TO THE HANDICAPPED. REPORT NOT AVAILABLE FROM NTIS.
