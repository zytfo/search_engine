STUDY TOUR OF HELSINKI, 6-12 DECEMBER 80.
     
WITHIN THE FRAMEWORK OF THE FINNISH-HUNGARIAN HEALTH AGREEMENT, THE DIRECTOR
OF THE MEDICAL INFORMATION CENTRE, HUNGARY, HAD THE OPPORTUNITY TO STUDY THE
WORK OF THE CENTRAL MEDICAL LIBRARY IN HELSINKI. DESCRIBES THE STAFFING,
ORGANISATION, AND SERVICES OFFERED BY THE LIBRARY.
