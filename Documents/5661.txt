NATIONAL SERVICE TO PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THE FIELD OF AUDIOVISUAL MEDIA. THE
SITUATION IN THE NETHERLANDS.

DISCUSSES THE DEVELOPMENTS IN THE NETHERLANDS WHICH HAVE LEAD TO THE INCLUSION
OF AUDIO-VISUAL MEDIA IN THE PUBLIC LIBRARIES AND THE CENTRAL SERVICES TO
PUBLIC LIBRARIES IN THIS FIELD. MENTIONS PROBLEMS REGARDING THE INCLUSION OF
AUDIO-VISUAL MATERIALS IN PUBLIC LIBRARIES, THE EFFECTS OF THE 1975 PUBLIC
LIBRARIES ACT WHICH MADE PROVISION FOR AUDIO-VISUAL MATERIALS, THE WORK OF THE
NEDERLANDS BIBLIOTHEEK EN LEKTUUR CENTRUM (DUTCH LIBRARY AND LITERATURE
CENTRE) AND PRESENT PRODUCTION ACTIVITIES OF THE AUDIO-VISUAL MEDIA SERVICE.
SINCE 1981 THE NBLC HAS PROVIDED A CENTRALISED SUPPLY OF AUDIO-VISUAL
MATERIALS TO PUBLIC LIBRARIES. THIS SERVICE HAS ENABLED LIBRARIES TO IMPROVE
THEIR AUDIO-VISUAL STOCK.
