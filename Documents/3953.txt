THE USE OF BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATABASES IN DEFENCE/MILITARY STUDIES.
     
DEFENCE STUDIES IS A DISCIPLINE WITH A WIDE SPREAD OF BIBLIOGRAPHIC SOURCES
AND NO EXISTING SPECIALISED ON-LINE DATA BASE. DESCRIBES EXPERIMENTS TO ASSESS
THE PERFORMANCE OF 17 BIBLIOGRAPHIC DATA BASES AT RETRIEVING NON-CLASSIFIED
DEFENCE LITERATURE. THE NEED FOR A NEW DEFENCE DATA BASE IS DISCUSSED.
