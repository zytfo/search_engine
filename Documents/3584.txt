A DELPHI STUDY OF PUBLIC LIBRARY GOALS, INNOVATIONS, AND PERFORMANCE
MEASUREMENTS.
     
UTILISING THE DELPHI TECHNIQUE, THIS STUDY IDENTIFIED AND RANKED PUBLIC
LIBRARY GOALS AND PERFORMANCE MEASUREMENTS, AS WELL AS VIABLE LIBRARY
INNOVATIONS LIKELY BY THE YEAR 2000. THE INDEPENDENTLY ELICITED STATEMENTS
FROM 2 SETS OF EXPERTS, PUBLIC LIBRARY DIRECTORS AND DIRECTORS OF
ALA-ACCREDITED GRADUATE LIBRARY SCHOOL PROGRAMMES WERE ANALYSED SEPARATELY AND
THEN COMPARED FOR SIMILARITIES AND DIFFERENCES.
