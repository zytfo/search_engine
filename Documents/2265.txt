GENEALOGY AND MAPS' SOME REFERENCE RESOURCES.
     
THE RECENT US INTEREST IN GENEALOGY HAS IMPLICATIONS FOR MAP LIBRARIANS WHO
ARE REQUIRED TO HELP TRACE THE AVERAGE FAMILY'S MOBILITY. DISCUSSES PUBLISHED
RESOURCES COMMONLY AVAILABLE TO MAP LIBRARIES WHICH WILL AID LIBRARIANS IN THE
SEARCHES. SECTIONS COVER LOCATION OF PLACE NAMES, FINDING A TOWNSHIP OR MINOR
CIVIL DIVISION, COUNTY/TOWNSHIP BOUNDARY CHANGES, URBAN PROBLEMS, AND LAND
OWNERSHIP.
