YOUTH, UNEMPLOYMENT, AND WORK WITH YOUNG ADULTS.
     
IN THE LAST 25 YEARS ONE OF THE MOST CRITICAL AREAS AFFECTING YOUTH, BUT ONE
OF THE LEAST DISCUSSED IN THE LIBRARY PROFESSION, IS HIGH UNEMPLOYMENT.
EXPLORES THIS TOPIC, WITH A VIEW TO DEVELOPING AN APPROACH THAT WILL SERVE AS
A MODEL FOR AN EXTENDED STUDY IN THIS AND OTHER AREAS SERIOUSLY AFFECTING THE
LIVES OF YOUTH. DISCUSSES THE DIMENSIONS OF THE US UNEMPLOYMENT PROBLEM, THE
EVOLUTION OF GOVERNMENT PROGRAMMES DEVELOPED TO DEAL WITH IT, AND A DEFINITION
OF THE ROLE OF LIBRARIES IN RELATION TO IT.
