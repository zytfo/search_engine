THE PUBLIC RECORD OFFICE AND ITS MEANS OF REFERENCE.
     
SUMMARY OF A TALK GIVEN TO THE SOCIETY OF INDEXERS, 12 FEB 80. OUTLINES THE
HISTORY OF THE PUBLIC RECORD OFFICE (PRO) WHICH HOLDS THE UK NATIONAL
ARCHIVES. GIVES SOME BRIEF INFORMATION ON THE INDEXES USED BY THE PRO, AND
DESCRIBES THE STAGES INVOLVED IN SEARCHING ITS RECORDS.
