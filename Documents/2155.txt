CANOEING, KAYAKING AND RAFTING PERIODICALS FOR LIBRARIES.
     
LISTS 24 PERIODICALS COVERING RECREATIONAL CANOEING, KAYAKING, AND RAFTING IN
THE USA AND CANADA AND OF AN OVERALL QUALITY SUITABLE FOR LIBRARY PURCHASE. 5
ARE DEVOTED TO THESE SPORTS; THE OTHER 19 FEATURE REGULAR OR OCCASIONAL
ARTICLES ON THE SUBJECT. ENTRIES CONTAIN TITLE, PLACE, PUBLISHER, BEGINNING
DATE, FREQUENCY, AND (EXCEPT FOR THE MOST WELL-KNOWN TITLES) DESCRIPTIVE
ANNOTATIONS.
