DEVELOPMENT OF AN INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR URBAN PLANNING AND HUMAN ENVIRONMENT
IN SFRY.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE EUROPEAN SEMINAR ON INFORMATION SYSTEMS, MADRID, JUNE
1979. THE YUGOSLAV PROJECT ON AN INFORMATION SYSTEM FOR URBAN PLANNING IS
DESCRIBED. THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE SYSTEM, ITS CHARACTERISTICS, ORGANISATIONAL
STRUCTURE, RELATIONSHIP WITH OTHER SYSTEMS, SOURCES AND USERS OF INFORMATION
AS WELL AS TASKS INVOLVED IN ITS REALISATION ARE ANALYSED.
