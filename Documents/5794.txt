BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL' A SURVEY OF THE LITERATURE.

A LIST OF REFERENCES COVERING BRAZILIAN AND FOREIGN LITERATURE ON
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL PUBLISHED BETWEEN 1970-80. PRELIMINARY DETAILS ARE GIVEN
OF SOURCES USED FOR COLLECTING MATERIAL AND THE SYSTEM OF ARRANGEMENT. THE
REFERENCES ARE DIVIDED INTO 12 SECTIONS' BIBLIOGRAPHIC CONTROL; INFORMATION
SYSTEMS; INTERNATIONAL CATALOGUING CODES; LEGAL DEPOSIT; NATIONAL
BIBLIOGRAPHIES; INTERNATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHIES; COOPERATIVE CATALOGUING; UNION
CATALOGUES; CATALOGUING IN SOURCE; SIMPLIFIED CATALOGUING; AUTOMATED
CATALOGUING; AND PERIODICALS.
