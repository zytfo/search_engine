INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION CENTRE ON THE SOURCES OF BALKAN HISTORY (CIBAL)' AN
EXAMPLE OF INTERNATIONAL CO-OPERATION.
     
THE INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION CENTRE ON THE SOURCES OF BALKAN HISTORY (CIBAL)
IS AN INTERNATIONAL NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANISATION FOUNDED IN 1976 AND HAVING A
MEMBERSHIP OF 20 COUNTRIES (INCLUDING THE UK, USA, AND USSR) AND 3
INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATIONS. OUTLINES THE HISTORICAL BACKGROUND, CURRENT
RESEARCH, AND PUBLICATION ACTIVITIES.
