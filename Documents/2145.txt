INFORMATION FLOW DYNAMICS ANALYSIS AS A METHOD OF STUDYING DEVELOPMENT OF
ASTRONOMY AND ITS SECTIONS.
     
INVESTIGATES THE CHARACTER OF CHANGE IN DOCUMENTARY INFORMATION FLOWS IN
ASTRONOMY DURING THE PERIOD 1899-1975, USING DATA OBTAINED BY ANALYSING
VARIOUS ISSUES OF THE WORLD'S LEADING ABSTRACTING JOURNALS COVERING ASTRONOMY.
ANALYSES THE DYNAMICS OF PUBLICATION GROWTH IN THE MAIN SECTIONS AND
SUBSECTIONS OF THE SOVIET ABSTRACTING JOURNAL 2ASTRONOMIYA1 AND IDENTIFIES
SPECIFIC CHARACTERISTICS IN THEIR DEVELOPMENT.
