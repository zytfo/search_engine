THE SLIPPERY MARKET FOR VIDEODISCS.
     
THE DEVELOPMENT COSTS, SALES, PRICES AND PRINCIPLE OF OPERATION OF THE RCA
SELECTAVISION VIDEODISC PLAYER ARE COMPARED WITH THE PIONEER AND MAGNAVOX
MACHINES. COMPETITION FROM VIDEO-CASSETTE RECORDERS IS DISCUSSED AND THE
TYPICAL KIND OF CUSTOMER FOR EACH IS CONSIDERED. THE DEVELOPMENT OF A RENTAL
MARKET FOR CASSETTES AND DISCS ARE DESCRIBED. JAPANESE DEVELOPMENT OF DISC
PLAYERS AND CASSETTE SYSTEMS IS CONSIDERED AND THEIR VIDEO COLOUR CAMERAS ARE
DISCUSSED. WHETHER RCA WILL RECOVER ITS DEVELOPMENT COSTS FOR THE
VIDEODISC-PLAYER IS ALSO EXAMINED.
