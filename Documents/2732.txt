RECENT TRENDS IN WEST GERMAN UNIVERSITY LIBRARY BUILDING PLANNING.
     
REVIEWS WEST GERMAN UNIVERSITY LIBRARY BUILDINGS AND DESCRIBES 5 BUILDINGS
COMPLETED SINCE 1973 AT' RUHR UNIVERSITY OF BOCHUM; DORTMUND UNIVERSITY;
BIELFELD UNIVERSITY; WESTPHALIAN WILHELMS UNIVERSITY OF MUNSTER; AND BREMEN
UNIVERSITY. SOME COMPARISONS AND CONTRASTS ARE MADE BETWEEN THESE BUILDINGS
AND OTHERS IN WEST GERMANY BUILT OR RESTORED BETWEEN 1945 AND 1973.
