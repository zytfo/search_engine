DEFINITION OF THE NATIONAL DOCUMENTARY HERITAGE.
     
AFTER PROVIDING A GENERAL DEFINITION OF NATIONAL HERITAGE AND NATIONAL
DOCUMENTARY HERITAGE, INTRODUCES THE DIFFERENT CATEGORIES OF DOCUMENTS WHICH
MAKE UP THE LATTER' WRITTEN DOCUMENTS, BOTH PRINT AND MANUSCRIPT, ICONOGRAPHIC
DOCUMENTS, SOUND, VISUAL AND MACHINE READABLE MATERIAL. IN CONCLUSION, A
DEFINITION-SYNTHESIS OF NATIONAL DOCUMENTARY HERITAGE, IS PROPOSED.
