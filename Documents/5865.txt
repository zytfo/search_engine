WEEDING: THE PROFESSIONAL PRACTICE OF GETTING RID OF DEAD WOOD IN THE LIBRARY
COLLECTION.

ATTEMPTS TO GIVE THE LIBRARY PROFESSIONALS' DEFINITION OF 'WEEDING', DISCUSSES
REASONS FOR AND AGAINST WEEDING OF LIBRARY COLLECTIONS, AND SUGGESTS POSSIBLE
SOLUTIONS TO ARGUMENTS AGAINST WEEDING, AND WHAT CAN BE DONE WITH WEEDED STOCK.
