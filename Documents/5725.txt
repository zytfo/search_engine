THE REPORT OF THE LIBRARIANS' UNION 1979-80.

INCLUDES THE ACCOUNTS OF THE LIBRARIANS' UNION FOR 1979, THE EXECUTIVE
COMMITTEE'S BUDGET PROPOSALS FOR 1981, REPORT OF THE LIBRARIANS' UNION'S
ACTION FUND FOR 1979-80, REPORT OF THE LIBRARIANS' UNION FOR 1979-80, REPORT
OF THE UNION'S GROUP FOR STATE EMPLOYED LIBRARIANS 1979-80, REPORT OF THE
UNION'S GROUP FOR PRIVATELY EMPLOYED LIBRARIANS 1979-80, AND REPORTS OF THE
FOLLOWING SPECIAL GROUPS' THE GROUP FOR BRANCHES AND MOBILE LIBRARIES, THE
GROUP FOR CHILDREN'S AND YOUNG PEOPLE'S LIBRARIANS, THE GROUP FOR OUTREACH
LIBRARY SERVICES, AND THE GROUP 'LIBRARY AND EDUCATION'.
