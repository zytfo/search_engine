THE COSTA RICAN SCHOOL LIBRARY SYSTEM.

EXPLAINS THE ORIGIN AND STRUCTURE OF THE SYSTEM OF SCHOOL LIBRARIES AND
RESOURCE CENTRES, INITIATED IN 1975 BY THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC EDUCATION IN
COSTA RICA. THE OPERATION OF THE REGIONAL SUBSYSTEMS IS DESCRIBED, SHOWING HOW
THE DEPARTMENTS OF ASSESSMENT AND SUPERVISION ORGANISE TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE
FOR EACH INDIVIDUAL COMPONENT INSTITUTION. THE CENTRALISED NATIONWIDE
CATALOGUING PROGRAMME IS OUTLINED, AND FINALLY THE POLICIES OF THE
INSTRUCTIONAL MATERIALS CENTRE, OPERATING AT REGIONAL LEVEL, ARE DELINEATED.
