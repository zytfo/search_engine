A DISCOURSE ON ISSUES' AN EXPLORATORY STUDY OF THE IMPLICATIONS OF INFORMATION
TECHNOLOGY FOR U.K. LIBRARY AND INFORMATION WORK MANPOWER PLANNING.
     
PRESENTS A PILOT STUDY OF A PROPOSED PROJECT TO FORECAST AND TO ASSESS THE
POTENTIAL DIRECT AND INDIRECT IMPACTS OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ON U.K.
LIBRARY/INFORMATION STAFF OVER THE NEXT 20 YEARS. DESCRIBES THE CHOICE OF
TECHNOLOGICAL FORECASTING AND ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGIES TO BE USED IN A
SUBSEQUENT DETAILED INVESTIGATION.
