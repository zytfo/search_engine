GENERAL AND SPECIAL BIBLIOGRAPHY.
     
BIBLIOGRAPHIC CATALOGUES COLLECT, DESCRIBE AND ARRANGE A COMPLEXITY OF DATA
FOR COMMUNICATION TO SCHOLARS BY PRECISE CRITERIA, THUS CONSTITUTING THE
THEORY AND METHODOLOGY OF BIBLIOGRAPHY AS A DISCIPLINE. AUTOMATION HAS BROUGHT
MORE COMPLEX PROBLEMS TO GENERAL NATIONAL BIBLIOGRAPHIES IN ITALY, CONSEQUENT
UPON INSUFFICIENT CENTRAL CONTROL AND DIFFICULTIES IN COLLECTING AND
DISSEMINATING INFORMATION. GENERAL AND SPECIAL BIBLIOGRAPHIES ARE
COMPLEMENTARY TO THE HISTORY OF BOOKS AND LIBRARIES AND LIBRARY ECONOMY.
