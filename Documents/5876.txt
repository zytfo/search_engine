THE AUTOMATED CIRCULATION SYSTEM MARKETPLACE' ACTIVE AND HEATING UP.

SURVEYS THE MARKET FOR COMPUTERISED CIRCULATION SYSTEMS OVER THE PERIOD
1977-81. A FINAL TOTAL OF 11 SYSTEMS ARE COVERED AND INFORMATION IS GIVEN FOR
THE NUMBER OF CUSTOMERS INSTALLING EACH SYSTEM, THE RANGE OF SIZES OF
INSTALLED SYSTEMS AND THE STATUS OF FUNCTIONS OF TURNKEY CIRCULATION VENDORS
AS OF 1 OCT, 81.
