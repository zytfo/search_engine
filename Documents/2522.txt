LIFE AND WORK OF PROFESSOR ARNTZ. AN FID TRIBUTE.
     
PAPER BASED ON AN ADDRESS DELIVERED AT THE FID CONGRESS BANQUET, HELD IN
COPENHAGEN 21 AUG 80. HIGHLIGHTS THE CONTRIBUTIONS AND ACHIEVEMENTS OF
PROFESSOR H. ARNTZ IN THE FIELD OF DOCUMENTATION AND INFORMATION SCIENCE AND
TRACES HIS EARLY CAREER AND INTEREST IN MICROFILMS AND REPROGRAPHY. REVIEWS
THE ACTIVITIES OF FID WHEN PROFESSOR ARNTZ WAS PRESIDENT AND PAYS TRIBUTE TO
HIS WORK AND ACHIEVEMENTS WITHIN THE FEDERATION.
