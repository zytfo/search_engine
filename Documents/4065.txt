LIBRARY EDUCATION STATISTICAL REPORT 1980.
     
THIS REPORT, THE 1ST IN A SERIES OF ANNUAL STATISTICAL SURVEYS, IS DESIGNED TO
PRESENT STATISTICAL INFORMATION ABOUT LIBRARY EDUCATION IN THE USA AND CANADA.
THE REPORT IS DIVIDED INTO 5 SECTIONS' FACULTY, BY RUSSELL E. BIDLACK;
STUDENTS, BY CAROL L. LEARMONT; INCOME AND EXPENDITURES, BY JAMES KRIKELAS;
CURRICULUM, BY ELSPETH POPE; CONTINUING EDUCATION, BY TIMOTHY W. SINEATH.
