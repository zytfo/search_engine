UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES IN MALAYSIA' A DESCRIPTIVE OUTLOOK.
     
(MLS DISSERTATION.) OUTLINES THE MALAYSIAN EDUCATIONAL ENVIRONMENT AND THE
GENERAL ROLE OF THE LIBRARY IN THE MALAYSIAN UNIVERSITIES. DESCRIBES THE
GENERAL BACKGROUND, COLLECTION, STAFFING, ACCOMMODATION, SERVICES AND
FACILITIES, AND TECHNICAL SERVICES OF THE LIBRARIES OF THE UNIVERSITIES OF
AGRICULTURE, TECHNOLOGY, AND SCIENCE, NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF MALAYSIA, AND
UNIVERSITY MALAYA. DESCRIBES THEIR COOPERATIVE ACTIVITIES. THEY ARE MUCH
BETTER DEVELOPED THAN OTHER MALAYSIAN LIBRARIES, ARE CONTROLLED BY
PROFESSIONAL LIBRARIANS, ARE BUILDING THEIR COLLECTIONS VERY QUICKLY, AND HAVE
DEVELOPED RAPIDLY IN THE AREAS OF SERVICES AND FACILITIES. COMPUTERISED
SYSTEMS ARE BEING INTRODUCED.
