HOW AN ELECTRONIC NEWSPAPER PROJECT WAS LAUNCHED IN WORCESTER, MASS.

THE LAUNCH BY THE WORCESTER TELEGRAM AND EVENING GAZETTE OF AN ELECTRONIC
PUBLISHING PROJECT IS DISCUSSED. THE DECISION TO LEASE A CABLE TV CHANNEL, THE
CHOICE OF EQUIPMENT AND ITS CAPABILITIES, THE PURCHASE OF A VIDEO DATA SYSTEMS
MICROSYSTEM I AND A JERROLD-COMMANDER MODULE ARE ALL DESCRIBED. THE NEWSWATCH
25 NEWS SERVICE, ITS CONTENT, THE PREPARATION OF NEWS STORIES, MUSIC TAPES AND
INFORMATION ARE EXPLAINED AND THE MUTUAL SUPPORT SYSTEM BETWEEN NEWSPAPER AND
CABLE NEWS DEPARTMENT IS DESCRIBED. CLASSIFIED ADVERTISING, A LIVE SHOW
COVERING LOCAL ELECTIONS AND FUTURE PLANS FOR NEWSWATCH 25 ARE ALSO DISCUSSED.
