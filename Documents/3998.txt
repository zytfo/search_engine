A BIBLIOGRAPHY OF LITTLE MAGAZINES IN THE ARTS IN THE U.S.A.
     
A BIBLIOGRAPHY OF US PERIPHERAL LITERARY PUBLICATIONS WITH A REPRESENTATIVE
SMATTERING OF ART WORK, ARTISTS' PERIODICALS, INSTITUTIONALLY-SPONSORED
MAGAZINES, INDEPENDENTLY PUBLISHED ALTERNATIVE ART PUBLICATIONS, PUBLICATIONS
WITH LOCAL OR REGIONAL SIGNIFICANCE, OTHER-FORMAT MATERIALS, SERIALS CONCERNED
WITH SPECIFIC ASPECTS OF THE CONTEMPORARY ART SCENE, AND CRITICAL AND
HISTORICAL PERIODICALS. THE PUBLICATIONS ARE LISTED ALPHABETICALLY BY TITLE
(WITH DESCRIPTIONS); DETAILS OF DEFUNCT PERIODICALS ARE GIVEN; AND A
GEOGRAPHIC LISTING AND SUBJECT INDEX ARE INCLUDED.
