THE CONTRIBUTION OF INFORMATION PROVISION TO NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT IN
SCIENTIFIC, INDUSTRIAL AND ECONOMIC SPHERES.
     
PAPER PRESENTED AT THE 1980 CONFERENCE OF THE SOUTH AFRICAN INSTITUTE FOR
LIBRARIANSHIP AND INFORMATION SCIENCE, TO WHICH THIS ISSUE IS DEVOTED. REVIEWS
THE EXTENT OF INFORMATION PROVISION IN SOUTH AFRICA'S SCIENTIFIC, INDUSTRIAL,
AND ECONOMIC SPHERES; THE SPECIFIC AREAS REQUESTING INFORMATION; THE
IMPORTANCE OF INFORMATION AS A NATIONAL RESOURCE; AND THE COMPONENTS OF A
NATIONAL INFORMATION POLICY.
