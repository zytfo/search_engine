REPORT' TRAINING SEMINAR ON BOOK DESIGN AND ILLUSTRATION 1980.
     
THE SEMINAR, JOINTLY ORGANISED BY THE COUNCIL AND THE ASSOCIATION OF
PUBLISHERS AND BOOKSELLERS OF THE FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF GERMANY, WAS HELD FROM
29 AUG TO 2 SEPT 80. PRESENTS PAPERS AND ADDRESSES FROM THE SEMINAR, ARRANGED
TO PROMOTE THE IDEA OF PACKAGING BOOKS ATTRACTIVELY IN SINGAPORE, WHERE THE
READING HABIT IS NOT FULLY DEVELOPED.
