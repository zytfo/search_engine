MICROFORMS AND ART LIBRARIES.
     
FROM THE DUAL REFERENCE POINTS OF 2MICROFORM REVIEW'S1 SUMMER 79 VISUAL ARTS
ISSUE AND THE ART LIBRARIES SOCIETY OF NORTH AMERICA 9TH ANNUAL CONFERENCE IN
FEB 81, EXAMINES THE CURRENT STATE OF ACCEPTANCE AND USE OF MICROFORMATTED
VISUAL MATERIALS FOR SERIOUS RESEARCH. PERSISTENT PROBLEMS PERTAINING TO
RESOLUTION, COLOUR FIDELITY AND PERMANENCE, AND LARGE AUDIENCE IMAGE DISPLAY
ARE DISCUSSED AGAINST SOME NEW TRENDS IN PUBLICATION AND DISTRIBUTION WHICH
CAN VASTLY INCREASE THE HOLDINGS AND RESEARCH CAPACITIES OF INDIVIDUAL
INSTITUTIONS.
