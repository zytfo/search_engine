THE INTERNATIONAL STANDARD SERIAL NUMBER (ISSN) AND ITS USE BY THE UNITED
STATES POSTAL SERVICE.
     
DESCRIBES THE RATIONALE AND PROCEDURES FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE MANDATORY
PRINTING OF THE INTERNATIONAL STANDARD SERIAL NUMBER (ISSN) ON SERIALS MAILED
AT SPECIAL RATES IN THE USA. THE EFFORTS OF THE US LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
NATIONAL SERIALS DATA PROGRAM (NSDP) TO ASSIGN AND HAVE THE ISSN PRINTED ON
EACH SERIAL PUBLICATION HAVE BEEN SIGNIFICANTLY REINFORCED THROUGH THIS
REQUIRED USE. CITES BIBLIOGRAPHIC, COMMERCIAL AND MANAGERIAL BENEFITS FOR THE
INFORMATION COMMUNITY.
