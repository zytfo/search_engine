ON-LINE DATA-BASE SERVICES MARKETS (PROSPECTUS ONLY).
     
THE STRUCTURE OF THE ON-LINE DATA BASE SERVICES INDUSTRY IS REVIEWED AND
FUTURE TRENDS ARE CONSIDERED. THE KINDS OF DATA BASES CURRENTLY AVAILABLE,
PRICE TRENDS AND SERVICE OFFERINGS ARE EXAMINED. THE ROLES OF AND RELATIONSHIP
BETWEEN PRODUCERS, DISTRIBUTORS AND END-USERS ARE EVALUATED. THE INTRODUCTION
OF NEW ELECTRONIC SYSTEMS IS CONSIDERED IN RELATION TO THEIR IMPACT ON ON-LINE
DATA BASE SERVICES. 10 YEAR MARKET FORECASTS ARE GIVEN.
