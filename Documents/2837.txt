DEATH AND DYING' A GUIDE TO BIBLIOGRAPHICAL SOURCES.
     
RECENTLY, A SPATE OF LITERATURE ON DEATH AND DYING HAS RAISED PHILOSOPHICAL,
SOCIAL, MORAL, ETHICAL, AND LEGAL QUESTIONS. PRESENTS AN ANNOTATED
BIBLIOGRAPHY DETAILING SOURCES THROUGH WHICH THIS NEW LITERATURE MAY BE
APPROACHED, SORTED OUT, AND, TO SOME DEGREE, EVALUATED.
