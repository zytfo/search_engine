THE FUTURE OF PRESTEL' THROUGH THE GATEWAY.
     
THE FUTURE OF THE PUBLIC PRESTEL SERVICE IN THE UK IS DISCUSED. THE
CHARACTERISTICS OF PRESTEL ARE DESCRIBED AND ITS PRESENT POSITION IN RELATION
TO BUSINESS AND DOMESTIC USE IS EXAMINED. THE STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES OF THE
PRESTEL SERVICE ARE REVIEWED AND THE MAIN FACTORS INFLUENCING PRESTEL'S FUTURE
SUCCESS IN THE UK ARE HIGHLIGHTED. THE NEW MARKETING STRATEGY FOR PRESTEL
INCLUDING THE PROVISION OF THIRD PARTY DATA BASE ACCESS (GATEWAY) IS DISCUSSED
AND ITS EFFECT ON PRESTEL'S FUTURE SUCCESS IS ASSESSED.
