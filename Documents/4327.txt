COMPUTERISED CIRCULATION.
     
REPORT OF A 1980 STUDY VISIT TO 11 AMERICAN UNIVERSITY LIBRARIES ANALYSING THE
PROBLEMS INVOLVED IN INSTALLING AN AUTOMATED LOAN SYSTEM. THE DISCUSSION
COVERS THE PREPARATION AND INPUT OF USER DATA; METHODS OF CONVERTING
CATALOGUES; AND PROCEDURES FOR ACCESSING FILES AND RECORDING TRANSACTIONS.
CONSIDERS THE ADVANTAGES OF AUTOMATION, AND THE ADDITIONAL APPLICATIONS OF
SUCH SYSTEMS IN LIBRARY MANAGEMENT OPERATIONS.
