THE RETRIEVAL OF INFORMATION IN THE HUMANITIES AND THE SOCIAL SCIENCES'
PROBLEMS AS AIDS TO LEARNING.
     
DESIGNED TO ASSIST STUDENTS IN LEARNING TO RETRIEVE INFORMATION IN THE
HUMANITIES/SOCIAL SCIENCES AND TO AID LIBRARY SCIENCE TEACHERS IN DEVISING
REFERENCE QUESTIONS FOR THEIR STUDENTS. THE BOOK CONSISTS OF OVER 4000
QUESTIONS ARRANGED IN 13 BROAD SUBJECT AREAS. AN ANSWER KEY TO ALL THE
QUESTIONS IS PUBLISHED SEPARATELY.
