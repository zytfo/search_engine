COMMONWEALTH OFFICIAL PUBLICATIONS AND THE RAZOR GANG' SOME THOUGHTS.
     
REVIEWS BRIEFLY THE CUTS IN AUSTRALIAN FEDERAL EXPENDITURE RECENTLY
RECOMMENDED BY THE REVIEW OF COMMONWEALTH FUNCTIONS AS THEY AFFECT THE
PUBLISHING ACTIVITIES OF THE FEDERAL EXECUTIVE. DISCUSSES THE IMPLICATIONS OF
THE RECOMMENDED MEASURES WHICH INCLUDE THE LEASING OF THE AGPS BOOKSHOPS TO
THE PRIVATE SECTOR, AND RESTRICTING THE MINISTERIAL DOCUMENT SERVICE, AND
MENTIONS SEVERAL PUBLICATIONS WHICH ARE TO CEASE. URGES LIBRARIANS TO
CAREFULLY MONITOR THE EFFECTS OF THESE ECONOMIES IN PUBLISHING, AND TO MAKE
KNOWN TO OFFICIAL CHANNELS THEIR CONCERN ABOUT THE CREATION OF ADEQUATE
PROCESSES OF CONSULTATION ON OFFICIAL PUBLISHING.
