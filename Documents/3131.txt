POSTCARDS ARE POPULAR, BUT NOT IN YOUR LIBRARY.
     
PRESENTS THE RESULTS OF A SURVEY OF US AND CANADIAN PUBLIC, ACADEMIC, SPECIAL
AND ART MUSEUM LIBRARIES REGARDING COLLECTIONS OF POSTCARDS-HOW THEY WERE
ORGANISED, HOW UPDATED AND WHO, IF ANYONE, USED THEM. MAKES PARTICULAR
REFERENCE TO ART REPRODUCTIONS, MAIL ART AND ARTIST'S POSTCARDS. A LIST OF
LIBRARIES RESPONDING TO THE SURVEY IS APPENDED.
