NEWS IN REVIEW, 1981.
     
A SURVEY OF US LIBRARIANSHIP DURING 1981. AREAS COVERED INCLUDE' PRESIDENTIAL
LIBRARIES; PRICES OF MATERIALS; STANDARDS; AUTOMATION; ELECTRONIC MAIL;
INTERLOANS; FUNDING; RESEARCH LIBRARIES; PUBLIC LIBRARIES; FEES AND FINES;
OUTREACH; LIBRARY OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE; PUBLIC RELATIONS; NETWORKS AND
COOPERATIVES; PERSONNEL; WOMEN IN LIBRARIES; PRESERVATION OF MATERIALS AND
LIBRARIANS IN THE NEWS.
