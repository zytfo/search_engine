THE VISUALLY IMPAIRED LIBRARY USER.
     
ABOUT 0.2% OF THE AUSTRALIAN POPULATION IS ESTIMATED TO BE 'LEGALLY BLIND' AND
A FURTHER 1% TO SUFFER IMPAIRMENT SEVERE ENOUGH TO DISABLE THEM IN DAILY
LIVING BUT NOT TO THE EXTENT OF 'LEGAL BLINDNESS'. REVIEWS THE TYPES OF
IMPAIRMENT AND THEIR EFFECTS AND THE AIDS USED TO HELP THE VISUALLY
HANDICAPPED TO OPERATE IN A WORLD DESIGNED FOR NORMAL VISION.
