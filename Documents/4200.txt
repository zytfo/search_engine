LIBRARY INFORMATION HANDBOOK' HANDBOOK OF STANDARD TERMINOLOGY FOR REPORTING
AND RECORDING INFORMATION ABOUT LIBRARIES. 1979 PRELIMINARY DRAFT.
     
COVERS THE BASIC MANAGEMENT INFORMATION NEEDS IN ACADEMIC, PUBLIC, SCHOOL AND
SPECIAL LIBRARIES AND OUTLINES THE TYPES OF INFORMATION USEFUL FOR
COMMUNICATING DATA ABOUT LIBRARY RESOURCES AND LIBRARY PROGRAMMES. A GLOSSARY
OF TERMS IS PROVIDED AS A GUIDE TO DATA ELEMENTS WHICH ARE USED FOR RECORDING,
REPORTING AND EXCHANGING DATA ABOUT LIBRARY RESOURCES AND LIBRARY PROGRAMMES.
