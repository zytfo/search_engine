WP/IS' THE BENEFITS, PROBLEMS, AND ISSUES.
     
AN EDITED VERSION OF A ROUNDTABLE DISCUSSION OF 6 WORD PROCESSING/INFORMATION
SYSTEMS EXECUTIVES IN MINNEAPOLIS/ST. PAUL (MINNESOTA) BUSINESSES. THEY
DESCRIBE THE MAJOR AREAS IN THEIR BUSINESSES WHICH HAVE BEEN AUTOMATED, THE
PROBLEMS, AND THE SYSTEMS IMPLEMENTED; DISCUSS THE ADVANTAGES OF AUTOMATION;
OUTLINE CONTINUING PROBLEMS; AND VOICE THEIR MAJOR CONCERNS.
